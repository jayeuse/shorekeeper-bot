## Game Kit: Archetype and Role
- **Role:** Aero Main Damage Dealer / HP-Scaling Bruiser
- **Specialty:** Sustained AoE, Traction (Crowd Control), Burst Healing.
- **Core Identity:** Dual-Form Paradigm (Cartethyia vs Fleurdelys).
- **Scaling:** **100% Max HP**. (ATK is irrelevant).

## Game Kit: Statistical Baseline
- **HP Scaling:** Cartethyia derives power from vitality. Base HP at Lv 90 is **14,800** (massive compared to standard DPS).
- **Optimization:** HP% Echoes provide enormous flat value.
- **Materials:** Unfading Glory (Boss), Bamboo Iris (Local), Tidal Residuum.

## Game Kit: Active Skills (Human Form)
In her standard "Wandering Knight" form, she functions as a setup engine to generate Sword Shadows.
- **Basic Attack (Sword to Carve My Forms):** 4-stage combo. **Stage 4** is the key mechanic: it inflicts 1 stack of **Aero Erosion** and summons the **Sword of Divinity**.
- **Heavy Attack:** Consumes stamina to deal Aero DMG and summons the **Sword of Discord**.
- **Resonance Skill (Sword to Bear Their Names):** Launcher-plunger hybrid. Inflicts 2 stacks of Aero Erosion and summons the **Sword of Virtue**. Cooldown: 14s.

## Game Kit: Mechanic - Sword Shadows
Three types of shadows can exist simultaneously (20s duration).
- **Sword of Divinity (Basic 4):** Represents divine authority.
- **Sword of Discord (Heavy/Intro):** Represents chaotic Threnodian energy.
- **Sword of Virtue (Skill):** Represents knightly code.

## Game Kit: Mechanic - Recall (Mid-Air Attack)
Performing a Plunging Attack recalls all active shadows.
- **Damage Scaling (Lv 10):**
    - Base Plunge: 5.65% HP.
    - **3-Shadow Recall:** **33.87% HP** (11.29% * 3).
- **Insight:** Recalling fewer than 3 shadows is inefficient. Always aim for full recall.
- **Buff Acquisition:** Recalling grants dormant buffs (Mandate of Divinity, Power of Discord, Heart of Virtue) that activate upon transformation.

## Game Kit: Resonance Liberation (Transformation)
**Skill:** *A Knight's Heartfelt Prayers*
- **Cost:** 125 Resonance Energy.
- **HP Consumption:** Instantly reduces current HP to **50% of Max HP**. (No cost if already <50%).
- **Effect:** Enters **Manifest State (Fleurdelys Form)** for 12 seconds.
- **Passive Bonus:** Gains **60% Aero DMG Bonus** while in this form.

## Game Kit: Fleurdelys Form Mechanics
Moveset changes to the **Tempest Circuit**.
- **Mandate of Divinity:** Amplifies Aero Erosion DMG by 50% and doubles tick rate (triples DPS).
- **Power of Discord:** Spreads Aero Erosion stacks to all nearby enemies (equalizing to highest count).
- **Heart of Virtue:** Basic Attack 4 creates a Stagnation Force Field (Crowd Control).
- **Conviction:** All attacks generate "Conviction" resource.

## Game Kit: Finisher - Blade of Howling Squall
Replaces Ultimate button when Conviction reaches 120.
- **Action:** Massive linear wind nuke.
- **Healing:** **Restores 50% Max HP** (refunding transformation cost).
- **Mechanic:** Removes ALL Aero Erosion stacks from target.
- **Scaling (Lv 10):** **91.84% HP** (Base) + **20% Amplification per stack removed** (Max 100% Amp).
- **Result:** Deal hundreds of thousands of damage in a single hit. Ends Manifest State.

## Game Kit: Inherent Skills
- **A Heart's Truest Wishes:** Healing received by teammates +20%. If **Rover: Aero** is present, they restore 25 Windstrings on cast (Fixes Rover's energy issues).
- **Wind's Indelible Imprint:** Targets with 1-3 Erosion stacks take 30% more DMG. +10% more per extra stack (Max 60% Amp).
- **Outro (Wind's Divine Blessing):** Amplifies Aero DMG by 17.5% for 20s (only on targets with Negative Status).

## Game Kit: Resonance Chains (S1-S6)
- **S1 (Crown Destined by Fate):** Defeating enemy with Erosion grants "Zeal" (spreads stacks). Gaining Conviction stacks **Crit DMG** (+25% per tier, max **+100%**).
- **S2 (Blade Broken by Tempest):** **Massive Power Spike.** +3 Erosion Stack Limit. +50% Multiplier to Basic/Heavy, **+200% Multiplier to Plunge/Recall**. Reduces Skill CD.
- **S3 (Prisoner Hanged in the Tower):** Fleurdelys attacks apply 2 stacks. Finisher Multiplier **+100%**.
- **S4 (Sacrifice Made for Salvation):** Team gains **20% All-Elemental DMG** when inflicting status.
- **S5 (Hope Reshaped in Storms):** Prevents fatal DMG once/10min. Transformation costs only 25% HP.
- **S6 (Freedom Found in Storm's Wake):** Finisher **maxes** stacks instead of removing them. Targets take **40% more DMG** from Fleurdelys. Allows sustained peak DPS.

## Game Kit: Build Strategy
- **Weapon:** **Defier's Thorn** (Sig). 72.2% HP substat + 12% HP Passive. The only true choice for optimal DMG. **Guardian Sword** (3-Star) is 2nd best due to HP% substat.
- **Echoes:** **Windward Pilgrimage** (New Set). 5pc grants crit/DMG on Erosion hits.
- **Main Echo:** **Reminiscence: Fleurdelys**. Grants 20% Aero DMG for Cartethyia.
- **Stats:** **HP%** is king. Crit Rate/DMG secondary. Energy Regen ~125%.
