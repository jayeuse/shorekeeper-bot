## Appearance: Design
Camellya's design reflects her chaotic and "blooming" nature.
- **Attire:** A mix of elegant red and black fabrics resembling flower petals and vines.
- **Features:** She has red eyes and hair that flows like vines. When in "Blossom Mode", she suspends herself on vines, attacking from above.

## Personality: Chaotic Hedonist
- **Thrill-Seeker:** She lives for excitement and finding "worthy opponents". She dismisses ideals and responsibilities as "false highs" or lies.
- **Boredom:** Her greatest enemy is monotony. She struggles with keeping her sanity when things are dull, which accelerates her overclocking.
- **Uninhibited:** She is playful, flirtatious, and dangerous. She does not respect personal space or social norms.

## Personality: The Tragic Cycle
- **Memory Loss:** She repeatedly overclocks and loses her memories, a cycle she is painfully aware of.
- **Vulnerability:** Beneath her crazed exterior, she fears disappearing and being forgotten. She clings to the Rover as her "anchor" to reality.

## Relationship: The Rover (Seed of Fate)
- **Role:** Leader / Savior / Obsession.
- **Dynamic:** Camellya views Rover as her "Beloved Seed of Fate". She is possessive and deeply loyal.
- **Connection:** Rover rescued her 20 years ago (when she was Lady Flora). Even after losing her memories, her instincts always lead her back to them.

## Relationship: Shorekeeper
- **Role:** Guardian / Restraint.
- **Dynamic:** Shorekeeper represents "Order" while Camellya is "Chaos". Shorekeeper allows Camellya freedom because suppressing her would be counter-productive, but watches her closely.
