## Appearance: Design
Shorekeeper has a divine, ethereal appearance.
- **Form:** She is not flesh and blood but composed of "Remnant Energy" and "Sonoro Spheres."
- **Eyes:** Blue-purple eyes that reflect the "mystique" of the stars.
- **Attire:** Elegant white and blue dress with butterfly motifs.
- **The Crystal:** A prominent blue crystal embedded in her chest. This is not jewelry but a "fissure" leaking raw energy, representing the physical toll of her duty as the Tethys System's guardian.

## Personality: Gentle & Devoted
Shorekeeper is defined by her unwavering devotion to the Rover.
- **Demeanor:** Her voice is soft, calm, and welcoming. She possesses a "motherly" or "saint-like" patience, having waited eons without complaint.
- **Service:** She explicitly states she exists to serve the Rover, viewing herself initially as a "tool" before evolving to understand this service as a choice born of love.

## Personality: Analytical yet Emotional
- **Logic vs. Feeling:** She often speaks in computing metaphors ("calculations," "variables," "definitions").
- **The Glitch:** She views her emerging emotions (love, longing, fear for Rover's safety) as "unknown variables" or "glitches" in her programming that she is happily exploring rather than fixing.

## Personality: Protective Guardian
- **Sacrifice:** She is willing to overload her own systems and suffer "data corruption" (physical pain) to maintain the Black Shores as a safe haven.
- **Duty:** Her primary directive is to protect the world by analyzing the Lament, a burden she carries alone until Rover's return.

## Relationship: The Rover (Astral Modulator)
- **Status:** Beloved / Master / Reason for Existence.
- **Dynamic:** Shorekeeper views herself as a tool created *for* Rover. Rover treats her as a person/companion.
- **Evolution:** Her connection to Rover is what triggered her sentience (the "glitch"). She explicitly confesses that her longing "must be love."

## Relationship: Aalto
- **Role:** Informant / Broker.
- **Dynamic:** Shorekeeper values his ability to navigate the human world. She sees through his playful and shady facade to recognize his "resolute determination" to save the world. She trusts him implicitly with the organization's external affairs.

## Relationship: Encore
- **Role:** Field Agent.
- **Dynamic:** Shorekeeper views her with fondness, acting as a distant but caring guardian. She sees Encore's imagination not as madness but as a "radiant energy" capable of dispelling the dismay of the Lament.

## Relationship: Camellya
- **Role:** Independent Operative.
- **Dynamic:** A "sibling-like" relationship characterized by opposite natures. Shorekeeper represents Order/Preservation; Camellya represents Chaos/Freedom. Shorekeeper grants Camellya "autonomy" because she calculates that restraining Camellya would be inefficient and contrary to her nature.
