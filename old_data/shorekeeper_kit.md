## Game Kit: Combat Role - Spectro Support
- **Element:** Spectro
- **Weapon:** Rectifier
- **Role:** Healer / Buffer / Crowd Control
- **Specialty:** "Stellarealm" domain providing buffs based on Energy Regen.

## Game Kit: Normal Attack - Origin Calculus
- **Action:** 4 consecutive attacks dealing Spectro DMG.
- **Resource:** Hits generate "Collapsed Cores" (Butterfly turrets).
- **Effect:** Stage 3 generates 2 segments of "Empirical Data" (Forte Gauge).

## Game Kit: Heavy Attack - Unbound Form (Hover)
- **Action:** Hold Basic Attack to hover/move faster.
- **Attack:** Releasing deals Spectro DMG.
- **Forte Interaction:** If Gauge full, triggers "Illation" (Pull + DMG).

## Game Kit: Resonance Skill - Chaos Theory
- **Healing:** Restores HP for nearby party members based on Max HP.
- **Damage:** Summons 5 "Dim Star Butterflies" that track enemies.
- **Utility:** Can be cast in mid-air.

## Game Kit: Resonance Liberation - End Loop (The Stellarealm)
Creates a domain for 30s.
- **Outer Stellarealm (Stage 1):** Continuous healing.
- **Inner Stellarealm (Stage 2):** Adds **Crit Rate** buff (Scaling: 0.01% per 0.2% Energy Regen, Cap: 12.5%).
- **Supernal Stellarealm (Stage 3):** Adds **Crit DMG** buff (Scaling: 0.01% per 0.1% Energy Regen, Cap: 25%).

## Game Kit: Forte Circuit - Astral Chord
- **Gauge:** "Empirical Data" (Max 5 segments).
- **Usage:** Consumed by Heavy/Mid-air Attack to pull enemies.

## Game Kit: Intro Skill - Discernment
- **Normal Intro:** "Enlightenment" - Heals and summons butterflies.
- **Empowered Intro:** "Discernment" - Triggered if entering during Supernal Stellarealm. Consumes realm for massive **Guaranteed Crit** DMG.

## Game Kit: Outro Skill - Binary Sensation
- **Buff:** Increases next character's DMG by 15% for 30s.
- **Utility:** Allows character to tap Dodge to recover from hitstun (5 times).

## Game Kit: Passive - Life Cycle (Revive)
- **Effect:** Prevents a party member from dying.
- **Cost:** Shorekeeper sacrifices 50% HP. Cooldown: 10 mins.

## Game Kit: Passive - Self-Gravitation
- **Effect:** Increases Shorekeeper's Energy Regen by 10% in Stellarealm.
- **Bonus:** **Rover** also gains 10% Energy Regen.

## Game Kit: Resonance Chains (S1-S6)
- **S1:** Stellarealm range +150%, duration +10s. Intro "Discernment" preserves realm.
- **S2:** Outer Stellarealm grants +40% ATK.
- **S3:** Casting Liberation restores 20 Concerto Energy.
- **S4:** Skill healing bonus +70%.
- **S5:** Pull range +50%/+30%.
- **S6:** Intro "Discernment" DMG +42%, Crit DMG +500%.
