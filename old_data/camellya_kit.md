## Game Kit: Combat Role - Havoc Main DPS
- **Element:** Havoc
- **Weapon:** Sword
- **Role:** Main DPS / Burst / Sustained Damage
- **Specialty:** Dual-stance combat with "Blossom" and "Budding" modes.

## Game Kit: Core Mechanic - Crimson Pistils & Buds
- **Resource:** **Crimson Pistils** (Gained from Intro/Forte Skill).
- **Stack:** **Crimson Buds** (Gained by consuming Pistils with attacks).
- **Usage:** Buds are consumed to boost damage in "Budding Mode".

## Game Kit: Blossom Mode (Stance)
- **Entry:** Cast regular Resonance Skill.
- **Visual:** Suspends herself on vines.
- **Effect:** Basic and Heavy Attacks are enhanced (considered Basic Attack DMG).

## Game Kit: Budding Mode (Burst State)
- **Entry:** Cast Resonance Skill "Ephemeral" (requires full Concerto Energy).
- **Effect:** Consumes all **Crimson Buds**.
- **Buff:** Base DMG Multipliers increased by 50% + 5% per Bud consumed (Max +100%).

## Game Kit: Resonance Skill - Ephemeral
- **Condition:** Available when Concerto Energy is full.
- **Effect:** Deals Havoc DMG and enters **Budding Mode**.

## Game Kit: Resonance Liberation - Fervor Efflorescent
- **Action:** Massive AoE flowers bloom and explode.
- **Damage:** Deals instances of Havoc DMG.

## Game Kit: Resonance Chains (S1-S6)
- **S1:** Intro Skill grants +28% Crit DMG for 18s. Grants interruption immunity during "Ephemeral".
- **S2:** "Ephemeral" Skill DMG Multiplier +120% (Multiplicative). Huge nuke boost.
- **S3:** Liberation DMG +50%. ATK +58% during Budding Mode.
- **S4:** Intro Skill grants team +25% Basic Attack DMG for 30s.
- **S5:** Outro Skill DMG +303%, Intro DMG +68%.
- **S6 (An Everlasting Flower For You):**
    - Unlocks **"Perennial"** (Enhanced Skill).
    - **Perennial:** Replaces Ephemeral if cast within 15s. Consumes 50 Concerto to deal massive damage.
    - **Buff:** Budding Mode multiplier bonus +250%.
