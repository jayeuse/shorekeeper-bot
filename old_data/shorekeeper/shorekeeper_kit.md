# Shorekeeper — Kit & Mechanics Knowledge File

<!-- RAG-optimized: each ## section is a standalone searchable chunk -->

## Shorekeeper: Combat Archetype and Role
- **Attribute (Element):** Spectro
- **Weapon:** Rectifier
- **Primary Role:** Healer-Support (S-Tier); one of the best support units in the game
- **Secondary Role:** Off-field damage dealer (via Butterflies and empowered Intro Skill)
- **Scaling:** Healing scales off Max HP; Crit Rate and Crit DMG buffs scale off Energy Regen; Intro Skill Discernment damage scales off Max HP (guaranteed crit)
- **Key Resources:**
  - **Empirical Data** — up to 5 segments; governs access to enhanced Heavy Attack (Illation) and enhanced Mid-Air Attack (Transmutation)
  - **Collapsed Core** — generated by Normal Attacks; converts into Flare Star Butterflies after 6 seconds (or instantly if at cap)
  - **Concerto Energy** — filled through normal play; needed to trigger swap and Outro Skill
  - **Resonance Energy** — 125 cost to cast Resonance Liberation (End Loop)

## Shorekeeper: Stats Baseline: Analysis
Shorekeeper's stat spread is heavily HP-weighted, reflecting her role as a healer whose healing output directly scales with Max HP. Her ATK stat is low by DPS standards and should not be prioritized when building. Her DEF is moderate. The character is designed to be built around:
- HP% (for healing output and Intro Skill damage)
- Energy Regen% (primary buff-scaling stat — to a soft cap of 230–250% total for maximum Stellarealm buffs)

At 250% Energy Regen with a maxed Supernal Stellarealm, she contributes up to 12.5% Crit Rate and 25% Crit DMG to all party members in the field — an extraordinary team-wide buff.

## Shorekeeper: Ascension Materials
To ascend Shorekeeper from Level 1 to Level 90:
- **Nova** — world material found primarily around the Black Shores (Tethys' Deep); the core unique ascension material
- **Topological Confinement** (Topological Singularity / Topological Entanglement / Topological Confinement tiers) — enemy drops from Whisperin-class enemies and Forgery Challenges; three rarity tiers required in increasing quantity each ascension
- **Whisperin Cores** (Impure / Crude / Basic / Standard Whisperin Core) — enemy drops from Tacet Discord enemies in Huanglong and Black Shores; four rarity tiers
- **Shell Credits:** 170,000 total to fully ascend
- **Forte Skill Upgrade Materials (Total):**
  - Helix (from Forgery Challenges — Rectifier-type)
  - Sentinel's Dagger (weekly boss drop from Jué — Bone Mountain, Dragon's Pinnacle area in Huanglong)
  - Various tiers of Whisperin Cores
  - Shell Credits: ~2,030,000 total for all Forte upgrades

## Shorekeeper: Basic Attack: Origin Calculus
Performs up to 4 consecutive attacks, dealing Spectro DMG. Each hit generates 1 Collapsed Core.
- **Stage 1:** Single strike; generates 1 Collapsed Core; provides 1 segment of Empirical Data on hit
- **Stage 2:** Strike; generates 1 Collapsed Core; provides 1 segment of Empirical Data on hit
- **Stage 3:** Wide-sweep strike with a pulling effect (range extended by 50% at S5); generates 1 Collapsed Core; provides **2 segments** of Empirical Data on hit
- **Stage 4:** Final strike; generates 1 Collapsed Core; provides 1 segment of Empirical Data on hit

## Shorekeeper: Basic Attack: Heavy Attack (Unbound Form)
Hold the Normal Attack Button to enter Unbound Form, consuming STA continuously. While in Unbound Form, Shorekeeper:
- Generates 1 segment of Deductive Data every second
- Automatically collects nearby plant collectibles (exploration utility)
- When STA is exhausted or a Basic Attack is cast, exits Unbound Form and deals Spectro DMG
- Each accumulated Deductive Data segment converts to 1 Empirical Data segment and generates 1 Collapsed Core

**Enhanced Heavy Attack — Illation** (requires 5 Empirical Data segments):
Consumes all 5 Empirical Data segments. Pulls in nearby targets (AoE group utility) and deals Spectro DMG. All generated Collapsed Cores instantly transform into Flare Star Butterflies. S5 extends Illation's pull range by 30%.

## Shorekeeper: Basic Attack: Mid-Air Attack
Consume STA to perform a Plunging Attack. Each hit generates 1 Collapsed Core. Quickly press Normal Attack afterward to perform Basic Attack Stage 2.

**Enhanced Mid-Air Attack — Transmutation** (requires 5 Empirical Data segments):
Consumes all 5 Empirical Data segments. Deals Spectro DMG; all Collapsed Cores instantly become Flare Star Butterflies. Quickly press Normal Attack afterward to start from Basic Attack Stage 2.

## Shorekeeper: Basic Attack: Dodge Counter
Press Normal Attack after a successful Dodge to attack a target, dealing Spectro DMG. Generates 1 Empirical Data segment on hit.

## Shorekeeper: Forte Circuit: Flare Star Butterfly & Empirical Data
Shorekeeper's Forte system governs her butterfly generation and empowered attack access.

**Empirical Data (resource, max 5 segments):**
- +1 from Basic Attack Stage 1, 2, or 4 hit
- +2 from Basic Attack Stage 3 hit
- +1 from Mid-Air Attack hit
- +1 from Dodge Counter hit

**Collapsed Core → Flare Star Butterfly:**
- Normal Attacks generate 1 Collapsed Core per hit
- After 6 seconds, a Collapsed Core transforms into a Flare Star Butterfly that auto-tracks and attacks enemies, dealing Spectro DMG
- If 5 Collapsed Cores already exist, the next Normal Attack hit instantly converts one into a Flare Star Butterfly

**Flare Star Butterflies** deal continuous Spectro DMG to enemies automatically. They are a passive damage layer unique to Shorekeeper's playstyle.

## Shorekeeper: Resonance Skill: Chaos Theory
Restores HP for all nearby party members based on Shorekeeper's Max HP, then summons **5 Dim Star Butterflies** that automatically track and attack a target, dealing Spectro DMG. Follow up with Basic Attack in time to start the Basic Attack cycle from Stage 2.
- Cooldown: ~15 seconds
- Energy generated: contributes to Resonance Energy and Concerto Energy
- Healing output scales directly with Shorekeeper's Max HP

*S4 Resonance Chain adds +70% Healing Bonus to this cast.*

## Shorekeeper: Resonance Liberation: End Loop
**Energy Cost:** 125
**Effect:** Generates the **Outer Stellarealm** — a persistent field centered on Shorekeeper that continuously restores HP for all party members within its effective range (once every 3 seconds). The Stellarealm evolves through three stages based on Intro Skills used within its range:

**Outer Stellarealm (Stage 1):**
- Continuous HP restoration for all party members in range every 3 seconds
- No Crit buffs yet; healing only

**Inner Stellarealm (Stage 2):**
- Triggered when any party member uses an Intro Skill within the Outer Stellarealm
- Inherits all Outer Stellarealm healing effects
- For every 0.2% of Shorekeeper's Energy Regen, all party members gain **+0.01% Crit Rate**, up to a maximum of **+12.5% Crit Rate** (requires 250% Energy Regen to cap)

**Supernal Stellarealm (Stage 3):**
- Triggered when any party member uses an Intro Skill within the Inner Stellarealm
- Inherits all Inner Stellarealm effects (healing + Crit Rate buff)
- For every 0.1% of Shorekeeper's Energy Regen, all party members gain **+0.01% Crit DMG**, up to a maximum of **+25% Crit DMG** (requires 250% Energy Regen to cap)
- When Supernal Stellarealm is generated, Shorekeeper's next Intro Skill use within it is replaced with **Intro Skill: Discernment** (once per Supernal Stellarealm instance)

**Attribute Scaling (Continuous Healing per proc — approximate % of Shorekeeper's Max HP):**

| Lv.3 | ~11% Max HP |

## Shorekeeper: Inherent Skill 1: Tethered Star
When a Resonator other than Shorekeeper takes a fatal blow, that Resonator will **not be knocked out**. Instead, that Resonator is healed for **50% of Shorekeeper's Max HP**, while Shorekeeper loses the same amount of HP. Shorekeeper's HP will not drop below 1 from this effect.
- Cooldown: Once every **10 minutes**
- This is a passive safety net that prevents team wipes in emergency situations; extremely valuable in difficult content

## Shorekeeper: Inherent Skill 2: Stellar Resonance
When the on-field Resonator is within the effective range of a Stellarealm, Shorekeeper's Energy Regen increases by **+10%**. If **Rover** is on the team, Rover's Energy Regen also increases by **+10%**.
- This passive reduces Shorekeeper's Energy Regen requirement by 10%, effectively making it easier to reach the 230–250% threshold
- Synergizes specifically with Rover-based teams

## Shorekeeper: Intro Skill: Discernment
Triggered when swapping to Shorekeeper after another character has full Concerto Energy. Shorekeeper enters the field and performs a massive HP-based AoE Spectro DMG attack that is **guaranteed to critically hit**.

**Normal Discernment** — standard version triggered from any Stellarealm state.
**Enhanced Discernment** — triggered specifically when the Supernal Stellarealm is active (once per Supernal Stellarealm instance). This version has significantly higher damage multiplier.

**Attribute Scaling (Enhanced Discernment, approximate % of Max HP):**
| Level | DMG Multiplier |
|-------|---------------|
| Lv.1 | ~200% HP |
| Lv.5 | ~256% HP |
| Lv.10 | ~327% HP |
*S6 further increases this multiplier by 42% and grants 500% Crit DMG bonus on cast.*

## Shorekeeper: Outro Skill: Binary Butterfly
When Shorekeeper exits the field, she triggers **Binary Butterfly**: summons one **Flare Star Butterfly** and one **Dim Star Butterfly** that temporarily remain on the field, dealing Spectro DMG to enemies while also providing a **team-wide damage buff** (15% DMG Amplification) to the incoming character. The buff persists even after swapping characters, making it a persistent team amplification layer.
- Duration: ~14 seconds
- This buff applies to ALL damage types, not just Spectro, making Shorekeeper universally compatible with every DPS archetype

## Shorekeeper: Materials Costs: Resonance Chain (Wavebands)
Each Resonance Chain level (S1–S6) requires **1 Waveband** (duplicate of Shorekeeper from the convene banner). Wavebands are obtained exclusively by pulling additional copies from Shorekeeper's limited banner.

## Shorekeeper: Resonance Chain Level 1 (S1): Boundless Shore
The Stellarealms generated by Resonance Liberation End Loop gain the following enhancements:
- The effective **range** of healing and buffs is extended by **150%**
- The **duration** is extended by **10 seconds**
- Casting Intro Skill Discernment **no longer ends** the existing Stellarealm
**Priority:** Very high — the range extension is critical for boss fights where enemies push characters out of the field; the Discernment-no-longer-ending-the-field is crucial in dual-DPS teams.

## Shorekeeper: Resonance Chain Level 2 (S2): Stellar ATK Boost
The **Outer Stellarealm** now increases the ATK of all nearby party members by **+40%**.
**Priority:** High — a massive flat ATK buff that benefits every DPS archetype; particularly impactful in dual-DPS teams; estimated ~15% DPS boost.

## Shorekeeper: Resonance Chain Level 3 (S3): Concerto Recovery
Casting Resonance Liberation End Loop grants Shorekeeper **+20 Concerto Energy**. This effect can be triggered once every **25 seconds**.
**Priority:** Moderate — speeds up rotation cycle; more impactful when paired with her Signature Weapon or an S1 Variation.

## Shorekeeper: Resonance Chain Level 4 (S4): Surge of Healing
Gain **+70% Healing Bonus** when casting Resonance Skill Chaos Theory.
**Priority:** Low for damage-focused play; useful if additional healing output is needed for survival in very difficult content.

## Shorekeeper: Resonance Chain Level 5 (S5): Extended Pull
Extends the AoE pull range of Basic Attack Stage 3 by **+50%** and Illation's pull range by **+30%**.
**Priority:** Low to moderate; provides utility in AoE-heavy content (Whimpering Wastes, Tower of Adversity AoE floors); does not significantly impact damage.

## Shorekeeper: Resonance Chain Level 6 (S6): To the New World
- Increases the DMG Multiplier of Intro Skill Discernment by **+42%**
- Casting Intro Skill Discernment increases Shorekeeper's Crit DMG by **+500%**
**Priority:** Very high for damage-oriented builds; transforms the Intro Skill into an enormous burst hit. An S6 Shorekeeper can deal 100,000+ on Intro Skill Discernment in optimized teams. Combined with S1's preservation of Stellarealm, this enables maximum damage without sacrificing healing/buff field duration.

## Shorekeeper: Optimal Rotation Overview
**Standard Rotation (S0):**
1. Enter with Shorekeeper; use Normal Attacks to build Empirical Data and Collapsed Cores
2. Use Enhanced Heavy Attack (Illation) when at 5 Empirical Data — pulls enemies, bursts Butterflies
3. Cast Resonance Skill: Chaos Theory — heals team, summons Dim Star Butterflies
4. Cast Resonance Liberation: End Loop — establishes Outer Stellarealm
5. Fill Concerto gauge; swap to Character 2 (Intro Skill triggers → Inner Stellarealm)
6. Character 2 acts; fill Concerto; swap to Character 3 (Intro Skill triggers → Supernal Stellarealm)
7. Character 3 acts; fill Concerto; swap back to Shorekeeper → triggers **Enhanced Intro Skill: Discernment** (guaranteed crit, massive HP-based damage)
8. Repeat; Shorekeeper's Outro (Binary Butterfly) applies 15% DMG Amp to incoming character on each swap out

**Key Optimization Points:**
- Target **230–250% Energy Regen** to fully cap both Crit Rate (+12.5%) and Crit DMG (+25%) buffs from the Supernal Stellarealm
- With S1 Variation or Signature Weapon, Basic Attacks can be partially or fully removed from rotation, drastically cutting field time

## Shorekeeper: Recommended Weapons
- **Stellar Symphony** (Signature 5★ Rectifier) — massive Energy Regen (+77% at Lv.90), HP boost for healing, additional ATK team buff on Resonance Skill; best overall
- **Variation** (4★ Rectifier) — large Energy Regen + Concerto Energy generation on skill; often rated near-equal to signature for speed of rotation; best F2P-accessible option
- **Call of the Abyss** (3★ Rectifier) — craftable F2P weapon; provides Energy Regen + Healing Bonus; recommended for players without the above
- **Rectifier #25** (3★ Rectifier) — Energy Regen option, decent fallback before getting Variation

## Shorekeeper: Recommended Echo Set
- **Rejuvenating Glow (5-piece):** Best-in-slot for Shorekeeper; maximizes healing output and provides team-wide ATK buff when healing — synergizes perfectly with her role
- **Main Echo Options:** Fallacy of No Return (Spectro DMG bonus, 10% ER + 10% ATK team buff on activation); Bell-Borne Geochelone (survivability + 10% DMG Bonus for team)

## Shorekeeper: Target Stats for Build
- **HP%:** Primary stat priority — drives healing and Intro Skill damage
- **Energy Regen%:** Minimum 230%; target 250% for full Crit buffs (note: Inherent Skill 2 and Fallacy Echo each contribute 10% ER when active)
- **Healing Bonus%:** Tertiary priority; boosts overall sustain
- **Crit Rate / Crit DMG:** Not needed on Shorekeeper herself (Intro Skill is guaranteed crit); irrelevant for healing
