## Backstory: Origin
- **Name:** Originally named "Angel".
- **Childhood:** Grew up as a friend to Augusta. While Augusta dreamed of being a hero, Angel was more introspective and withdrawn.
- **The Event:** During a massive Dark Tide outbreak, Angel was nearly consumed. Instead of dying or turning into a monster, she forcibly absorbed the corruption, merging with a high-level frequency known as "Chimera".

## Backstory: The Discord Slayer
- **Transformation:** She was presumed dead by Augusta. In reality, she survived, her body altered to house the "Sinflame".
- **Mission:** She swore to hunt down Tacet Discords, using their own power against them. She took the name "Galbrena".
- **Chimera:** The entity she absorbed lives inside her, constantly bickering and urging destruction, but she keeps it on a tight leash.

## Story: Current Era
- **Occupation:** Wandering Bounty Hunter.
- **Reputation:** Known as the "Fiend of Ever-Burning Flames". Feared by criminals and Discords alike.
- **Reunion:** She eventually crosses paths with the Rover and indirectly with Augusta (now a Hero King). She chooses to remain in the shadows, believing her method (slaughtering Discords) is necessary work that a "Hero" cannot do.

## Lore: Lux & Umbra
- **Weapons:** Her dual pistols.
- **Symbolism:** Represent the duality of her existence—Light (Angel) and Shadow (Chimera).
- **Ability:** They channel her Fusion energy to fire concentrated blasts of "Sinflame".

## Lore: Afterflame
- **Concept:** The residual energy left behind when she burns a Discord's frequency. She can harvest this to fuel her own power, essentially "feeding" on her enemies.
