## Game Kit: Combat Role - Fusion Main DPS
- **Element:** Fusion
- **Weapon:** Pistol
- **Role:** Main DPS / Burst Damage
- **Specialty:** "Demon Hypostasis" state for massive damage.

## Game Kit: Core Mechanic - Sinflame & Afterflame
- **Sinflame (Blue Bar):** Built by Basic/Heavy Attacks. At 100 points, enables "Demon Hypostasis".
- **Afterflame (Red Bar):** Built when *teammates* use Echo Skills. Increases Galbrena's damage during her enhanced state.

## Game Kit: Resonance Skill - Ascent of Malice
- **Normal State:** Deals Fusion DMG and generates Sinflame.
- **Threshold State (100 Sinflame):** Casting Skill enters **"Demon Hypostasis"** (Enhanced State).

## Game Kit: Enhanced State - Demon Hypostasis
Entered by casting Skill with full Sinflame.
- **Enhanced Basic:** "Seraphic Execution" - Multi-hit combo.
- **Enhanced Heavy:** "Flamewing Verdict" - Massive AoE slam.
- **Enhanced Skill:** "Ravage" / "Encroach" - Finisher moves.
- **Buffs:** Multipliers significantly increased (e.g., Heavy Attack DMG +85% after Liberation).

## Game Kit: Resonance Liberation - Hellfire Absolution
- **Action:** Unleashes full power, dealing massive Fusion DMG.
- **Buff:** Enters a 14s buff state boosting Enhanced Basic/Heavy Attack multipliers by **85%**.

## Game Kit: Passive - Afterflame Synergy
- **Trigger:** When a teammate uses an Echo Skill.
- **Effect:** Galbrena gains 1 stack of "Afterflame" (Max 40 points).
- **Payoff:** Consumed during Demon Hypostasis to boost damage further.

## Game Kit: Resonance Chains (S1-S6)
- **S1:** Afterflame grants Crit DMG to enhanced attacks (up to +80%).
- **S2:** Increases "Burning Drive" ATK bonus by 350% (Total ~90% ATK buff).
- **S3:** Liberation DMG Multiplier +130%.
- **S4:** Teammate Echo Skills grant 20% All-Elemental DMG Bonus to the team.
- **S5:** Skill DMG Multipliers +150%.
- **S6 (Eternal Hypostasis):**
    - Enhanced State becomes "Eternal Hypostasis".
    - Enhanced Attack Multipliers +60% (Multiplicative).
    - Afterflame points grant up to 35% Fusion DMG Amplification.
