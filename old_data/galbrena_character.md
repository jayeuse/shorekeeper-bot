## Profile: Basic Information
- **Name:** Galbrena (formerly Angel)
- **Title:** The Discord Slayer / Fiend of Ever-Burning Flames
- **Attribute:** Fusion
- **Weapon:** Pistol (Signature: Lux & Umbra)
- **Rarity:** 5-Star
- **Release Version:** 2.7
- **Voice Actor (EN):** Devora Wilde
- **Companion:** Chimera (Voiced by Toby Williams)

## Appearance: Design
Galbrena has a rugged, battle-hardened appearance suiting a bounty hunter.
- **Attire:** Practical combat gear mixed with demonic motifs reflecting her bond with Chimera.
- **Features:** She bears marks of corruption from the Dark Tide, which she has suppressed and controlled.

## Personality: The Discord Slayer
- **Ruthless:** She shows no mercy to Tacet Discords, viewing them as monsters to be exterminated.
- **Disciplined:** Despite harboring a "demon" (Chimera) inside her, she maintains absolute control over her sanity and powers.
- **Independent:** She prefers working alone or with Chimera, rejecting offers from mercenary groups like Kakaro's Ghost Hounds, though she remains on amicable terms.

## Personality: Hidden Warmth
- **Human Bonds:** Despite her harsh exterior, she deeply values humanity and connections. She fights to protect people from the horrors she endured.
- **Nostalgia:** She retains memories of her childhood friend Augusta (now a Hero King), though their paths have diverged.

## Relationship: Chimera
- **Role:** Symbiotic Partner / Inner Demon.
- **Origin:** A powerful Tacet Discord frequency Galbrena absorbed.
- **Dynamic:** Chimera constantly taunts her and suggests giving in to chaos, but Galbrena treats him like a noisy pet or a tool. They bicker constantly but fight in perfect sync.

## Relationship: The Rover
- **Role:** Ally / Guide.
- **Dynamic:** Galbrena sees Rover as a capable warrior. She reveals her true name ("Angel") to them, a sign of deep trust.
- **Quote:** "By my trigger... enter hell's gates!"

## Relationship: Augusta
- **Role:** Childhood Friend.
- **Dynamic:** Augusta represents the "Light" (Hero King) while Galbrena operates in the "Dark" (Discord Slayer). They are two sides of the same coin, fighting for the same goal through different methods.
