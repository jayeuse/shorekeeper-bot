## Backstory: Lady Flora
- **Origin:** 20 years ago, she lived in "Petalfall Village" under the name (or nickname) "Lady Flora".
- **The Incident:** Tacet Discords attacked her village. She defended it but was overwhelmed until the Rover arrived and saved her.
- **Recruitment:** She was taken in by the Black Shores due to her high resonance aptitude as a "Bloom Bearer".

## Backstory: The Waiting Game
- **Separation:** When Rover left the Black Shores, "Lady Flora" fell into a deep depression.
- **Overclocking:** The boredom and lack of purpose caused her resonance to become unstable, leading to severe "Overclocking" (corruption).
- **Memory Wipe:** To survive towards a future reunion, she chose to erase her memories, believing her instincts would guide her back to Rover.

## Story: Camellya
- **Reawakening:** She woke up 10 years later in a cave as "Camellya", with no memory of her past.
- **The Cycle:** She wandered aimlessly, eventually finding her way back to the Black Shores. Any attempt to forcefully recall her past causes her to overclock again, leading to repeated memory wipes.
- **Reunion:** In the current era, she meets Rover again. Though she doesn't fully remember the details of "Lady Flora", her soul recognizes them instantly.

## Lore: Artificial Resonator
- **Nature:** Camellya is an "Artificial Resonator", meaning her powers were induced or modified rather than naturally occurring. in the same way natural ones do.
- **Rabelle's Curve:** Her resonance pattern is unique, marked by extreme highs (manic excitement) and lows (crushing boredom).
