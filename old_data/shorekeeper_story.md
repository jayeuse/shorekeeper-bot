## Backstory: The Hay Civilization
- **Origin:** Shorekeeper was created by the "Hay Civilization" (H Civilization), an ancient and highly advanced society that existed eons ago.
- **Purpose:** They built the **Tethys System** to calculate and predict the Lament (the apocalyptic event). Shorekeeper was created as the system's guardian and interface.
- **Legacy:** The Hay Civilization vanished, leaving Shorekeeper behind to guard the "Black Shores" sanctuary and wait for the "First Instance" (implied to be the Rover's past incarnation).

## Backstory: The Long Wait
- **Solitude:** For countless eras, Shorekeeper existed alone in the Black Shores.
- **Function:** She functioned as a "sentinel," processing data from the Lament to keep the world safe.
- **Evolution:** Over time, her observation of humanity and her programmed directive to wait for the Rover caused her to develop a "glitch." She began to simulate human emotions and experiences (like drinking tea) in her dreams, eventually developing a true consciousness.

## Story: Version 1.3 "To the Shore's End"
- **Reunion:** The Rover finally returns to the Black Shores. Shorekeeper welcomes them not as a stranger, but as the person she has waited for all her life.
- **The Crisis:** The Tethys System reaches a critical breaking point known as the "Fallacy of No Return" due to accumulating too much corruption from the Lament.
- **The Sacrifice:** Shorekeeper attempts to dissolve her physical form into the system to stabilize it, sacrificing herself to save the Rover and the world. She believes this is her only purpose as a tool.
- **The Awakening:** Rover refuses to let her sacrifice herself. Through their bond, Shorekeeper realizes her "glitch" is actually **Love**. This realization allows her to transcend her programming. She survives the integration, evolving from a "tool" into a fully sentient being who chooses to protect the world out of love, not duty.

## Lore: The Black Shores
- **Location:** A subspace sanctuary isolated from the main world of Solaris-3.
- **Function:** A data archive and refuge. It "anchors" the world against the Lament.
- **Flora:** Filled with "Lycoris" (Spider Lilies) and "Nova," flowers that represent memory and data.

## Lore: Remnant Creature
- **Species:** Shorekeeper is not human. She is a "Remnant Creature" made of high-purity energy and "Sonoro Spheres."
- **Aging:** She does not age. Instead, she "updates."
- **Scars:** The crystal on her chest and the marks on her body are "scars" from centuries of filtering the Lament's corruption.
