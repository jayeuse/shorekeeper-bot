## Iuno: Combat Archetype and Role
- **Element/Attribute:** Aero
- **Weapon Type:** Gauntlet
- **Role:** Hybrid Sub-DPS / Support — primarily functions as a Sub-DPS dealing strong Resonance Liberation DMG; simultaneously provides healing, stamina restoration, team shielding, and a major Heavy Attack DMG amplification Outro buff
- **Scaling Stat:** ATK (primary); Crit Rate and Crit DMG (critical multipliers); Resonance Liberation DMG Bonus (unique stat that scales the majority of her damage output)
- **Damage Type:** Resonance Liberation DMG (the vast majority of her New Moon state attacks — Moonbow Basic Attacks, Arc Beyond the Edge, Absolute Fullness — are all classified as Resonance Liberation DMG)
- **Combat Style:** Stance-switcher — alternates between Half Moon (Moonring gauntlet stance, generates Sentience resource) and New Moon (Moonbow bow stance, consumes Sentience for amplified Resonance Liberation DMG, healing, and Concerto Energy restoration)
- **Specialty:**
  - 50% Heavy Attack DMG Amplification Outro to the incoming Resonator — the best single Heavy Attack buff in the game as of Version 2.6–3.0
  - Full Moon Domain: provides 4% All DMG Amplification per shield generated (up to 10 stacks = 40% total) exclusively within domain area, making her the optimal buffer for Augusta (who generates shields every 0.5s via Glory's Favor)
  - Simultaneous healing, stamina recovery, and shielding within the same rotation — true "jack of all trades" utility role

## Iuno: Key Resources (Forte Mechanics Overview)

## Iuno Forte Circuit: Ebb and Flow — Sentience and Lunar Cycle
Iuno's Forte Circuit is called **Ebb and Flow** and governs three interlocking systems: Sentience, the Lunar Cycle state, and the Full Moon Domain.

**Sentience (Forte Gauge — Max 100 points, shown as blue arc):**
- Generated by: Basic Attacks and Dodge Counters (Moonring) in Half Moon state; Resonance Skill – Unfinished Refrain; Intro Skill and Resonance Liberation (grant 5 stacks of Blessing of the Wan Light and restore Sentience indirectly via Inherent Skill 2)
- Consumed by: Moonbow Basic Attacks (Stage 1, 2, 3), Resonance Skill – Arc Beyond the Edge (2 charges), Moonbow Dodge Counter — all while in New Moon state. Consuming Sentience increases the individual skill's DMG Multiplier, restores additional Concerto Energy, and heals nearby Resonators.
- Design note: Sentience is the core damage amplifier for Iuno's New Moon phase. Higher Sentience stacks at consumption = higher DMG multipliers and more healing per hit. Resonance Liberation immediately fills Sentience to max, making the Intro → Liberation combo the fastest path to full New Moon burst.

**Lunar Cycle Activation:**
- Entering Lunar Cycle requires casting Resonance Skill – Closing Refrain (Enhanced Resonance Skill triggered after Basic Attack Stage 3, Intro Skill, or Resonance Skill – Pulse of Origins) OR casting Resonance Liberation.
- Default state on entering Lunar Cycle: **Half Moon**.
- Lunar Cycle changes Iuno's movement to **Meandering** — she can fly horizontally with no height restrictions (STA consumed during flight, but no STA needed to stay aloft).
- Jump is replaced by Heavy Attack – Flux (switches between Half Moon and New Moon states mid-air or on ground).
- Ends if: Environmental interactions, Utility use, or Hold Jump.

**Half Moon State:**
- Iuno attacks with Moonring. Ring attacks generate Sentience on hit.
- Resonance Skill in Half Moon → Unfinished Refrain (shares cooldown with Closing Refrain)
- Transition to New Moon: Press Heavy Attack – Flux: Moonbow (deals Aero DMG classified as Resonance Liberation DMG; usable mid-air)

**New Moon State:**
- Iuno attacks with Moonbow. Bow attacks consume Sentience to increase DMG Multiplier, restore Concerto Energy, and heal nearby Resonators.
- Resonance Skill in New Moon → Arc Beyond the Edge (2 initial charges; deals Aero + Resonance Liberation DMG; provides recovery if hit mid-air; directional input extends travel)
- Transition to Half Moon: Press Heavy Attack – Flux: Moonring (deals Aero + Resonance Liberation DMG; usable mid-air)

**Absolute Fullness (Heavy Attack replacement when Concerto is full):**
- When Iuno's Concerto Energy is full AND she is in Lunar Cycle, Heavy Attack → Absolute Fullness
- Effect: Heals nearby Resonators, deals Aero DMG to nearby targets (Resonance Liberation DMG), ends Lunar Cycle, conjures Full Moon Domain at Iuno's location
- Full Moon Domain: Periodically restores HP and STA to all Resonators inside it. Each shield generated within the domain grants 1 stack of **Blessing of the Wan Light**.
- **Blessing of the Wan Light:** Each stack = +4% All DMG Amplification for Iuno (up to 10 stacks = +40% All DMG Amplification). Duration 15s per stack; refreshed by new stacks. Ends early if Iuno leaves domain or is switched off-field.
- Note: At S0, Blessing of the Wan Light only amplifies Iuno's own damage. At S2, stacks also grant +40% DMG Amplification to the team after 10 stacks.

## Iuno: Inherent Passives (Waxing Ascent & Derivation)
**Inherent Skill 1 – Waxing Ascent:**
When Iuno's Resonance Skill (Closing Refrain, Unfinished Refrain, or Arc Beyond the Edge) hits a target, she gains a Shield absorbing damage equal to **5% of her Max ATK** for 8 seconds. This triggers once every 0.5 seconds. The shield is self-only by default (shared at S4).

- This shield fires constantly during her Resonance Skill usage — every 0.5s while hitting enemies — making Crown of Valor ATK/Crit DMG stacks and Moongazer's Sigil DEF ignore stacks nearly permanent during combat.
- The shield value scales entirely with ATK, incentivizing ATK-heavy builds even on a Crit-focused character.

**Inherent Skill 2 – Derivation:**
When Iuno casts Intro Skill or Resonance Liberation, she immediately gains **5 stacks of Blessing of the Wan Light**.

- This is critical for enabling Blessing of the Wan Light damage amplification early in Iuno's rotation without requiring her to manually stack from zero.
- Since Iuno's rotations are short (typically Intro → Liberation → New Moon combo → Outro), there is minimal time to slowly stack from attacks alone — Derivation ensures she enters New Moon with meaningful amplification already active.
- Post-S1, the Full Moon Domain also restores 1 Resonance Energy per second while Iuno is inside it.

**Forte Attribute Bonuses (Resonance Chain tree — max nodes):**
- CRIT Rate +8%
- ATK +12%

## Iuno Resonance Skill: Pulse of Origins / Closing Refrain / Unfinished Refrain / Arc Beyond the Edge
Iuno's Resonance Skill has four forms depending on state and activation method. All forms deal Aero DMG.

**Resonance Skill – Pulse of Origins (baseline, before enhancement window):**
Active when not in Lunar Cycle and no enhancement window is active. Triggers enhancement window upon use.

**Resonance Skill – Closing Refrain (Enhanced RS — not in Lunar Cycle):**
Available for 5 seconds after: Moonring Basic Attack Stage 3, Intro Skill, or Pulse of Origins.
Unleashes a flurry of strikes dealing Aero DMG and **activates the Lunar Cycle** (entering Half Moon by default). This is the primary Lunar Cycle entry from outside.

**Resonance Skill – Unfinished Refrain (in Lunar Cycle – Half Moon):**
Replaces Closing Refrain while in Half Moon. Unleashes a flurry of strikes dealing Aero DMG. Shares cooldown with Closing Refrain.

**Resonance Skill – Arc Beyond the Edge (in Lunar Cycle – New Moon — 2 initial charges):**
Casting Arc Beyond the Edge causes Iuno to shift her position and deal Aero DMG classified as **Resonance Liberation DMG**. Directional input extends travel distance. If Iuno is hit or launched mid-air, casting Arc Beyond the Edge instantly recovers her. Can be cast mid-air.

- Arc Beyond the Edge consumes Sentience to increase DMG multiplier, restore additional Concerto Energy, and heal nearby Resonators.
- With 2 initial charges: Two Arc casts available per New Moon entry.
- S6 resets all charges of Arc Beyond the Edge upon casting Absolute Fullness.

## Iuno Inherent Skill 2: Derivation
When Iuno casts Intro Skill or Resonance Liberation, she immediately gains **5 stacks of Blessing of the Wan Light**.

- This grants +20% All DMG Amplification immediately on Intro or Liberation cast (5 stacks × 4% = 20%)
- Because Iuno's rotation begins with Intro → Liberation, she enters New Moon with 10 stacks of Blessing of the Wan Light active (5 from Intro + 5 from Liberation = 10 = max) — giving her full +40% DMG Amplification from the very first hit
- Without this passive, stacking to 10 manually would require multiple shield triggers, taking 4–5 seconds to reach max
- This makes Derivation arguably the most impactful inherent passive in Iuno's kit

## Iuno Outro Skill: From Gloom to Gleam
**Definition:** Casting Outro Skill deals **100% Aero DMG** to the target. The next incoming Resonator gains **50% Heavy Attack DMG Amplification** for **14 seconds**. This effect ends immediately if that Resonator is switched off-field.

This Outro is the single most powerful Heavy Attack DMG buff in Wuthering Waves as of Version 2.6–3.0. It provides a pure, true DMG amplification — not a bonus or multiplier, but direct amplification that stacks multiplicatively with other buff types.

- At S0: Exclusively Heavy Attack DMG
- Designed primarily for: Augusta (main beneficiary), Jiyan (also benefits from Heavy Attack DMG on his final hit), any future Heavy Attack-focused DPS
- Worth noting: 50% Heavy Attack Amplification applied to Augusta who also has access to Iuno's Full Moon Domain 40% All DMG Amplification = up to 90% total DMG Amplification package (the highest support package available to Augusta as of Version 2.6)

**Outro Skill Attribute Scaling:**

- 50% Heavy Attack DMG Amplification (fixed regardless of Outro skill level)
- Duration: 14 seconds (fixed)

## Iuno: Resonance Chains (Sequence Nodes S1–S6)

## Iuno Resonance Chain S1: Crescent Luminance
**ATK increased by 40% while in Lunar Cycle.** Inside the Full Moon Domain, Iuno additionally restores **1 Resonance Energy per second**. Resonance Skill – Arc Beyond the Edge and Heavy Attack – Absolute Fullness gain **immunity to interruption**.

*Impact (Wutheringlab estimate): ~22% personal DPS increase. This is Iuno's single highest-value sequence node by far. Three simultaneous effects: (1) +40% ATK while in Lunar Cycle — since her optimal rotation maximizes Lunar Cycle uptime, this is effectively a near-permanent ATK buff boosting both her damage and her shield strength; (2) 1 Energy per second inside the Full Moon Domain — combined with S1's ruling that Iuno is treated as inside the domain even off-field after Absolute Fullness, she gains at least 25 Energy passively per rotation, making Energy Regen substats largely unnecessary after S1; (3) Interruption immunity on Arc Beyond the Edge and Absolute Fullness — eliminates the most frustrating points of vulnerability in her rotation. Overwhelmingly the recommended purchase from the Coral Shop. Best single sequence node for Iuno.*

## Iuno Resonance Chain S2: Twin Phases
When Iuno has 10 stacks of Blessing of the Wan Light, all Resonators in the team gain **40% All DMG Amplification** (in addition to Iuno's personal 40%). This team-wide buff ends if Iuno leaves the Full Moon Domain or is switched off-field.

*Impact (Wutheringlab estimate): ~25% personal DPS increase; ~16% team DPS increase (in compositions like Augusta–Shorekeeper). Changes the strategic nature of Iuno's Full Moon Domain: it shifts from a purely personal amplification field to a team-wide 40% All DMG Amplification zone. This makes S2 effectively mandatory if using Iuno as Augusta's support, since Augusta generates shields every 0.5s (via Glory's Favor), rapidly stacking to 10 Wan Light. At full stacks, the domain provides +40% All DMG Amplification for the entire team — on top of Iuno's Outro 50% Heavy Attack Amplification, creating a total 90% DMG Amplification package for Augusta. Rated A-tier in S2 tier lists. Best value combined with S1.*

## Iuno Resonance Chain S3: Moonlit Surge
The DMG Multiplier of Moonbow Basic Attacks (Stage 1, 2, 3) and Resonance Skill – Arc Beyond the Edge increases by approximately 25–30% while in New Moon state.

*Impact (Wutheringlab): ~20% personal DPS increase. Straightforward multiplier increase on her highest-damage skills. No mechanic changes — purely numerical. Notably, S3 and S4 together provide very different value types (personal damage vs. team utility), making the ordering less obvious than S1 and S2.*

## Iuno Resonance Chain S4: Full Moon's Blessing
The Shield from Inherent Skill 1 – Waxing Ascent is now extended to **all Resonators in the team** (previously self-only).

*Impact: Primarily team utility. The shield is small (~100 HP per proc), so its actual defensive value remains low. However, extending it to teammates unlocks Crown of Valor stacking, Moongazer's Sigil DEF ignore, and Thunderflare Dominion DEF ignore for ALL team members — including Augusta if she uses these weapons/sets. For teams without Augusta (e.g., Jiyan), the team shields enable Crown of Valor for the whole team. Very low personal DPS value; moderate team value depending on team composition. Classified as a low-priority node by most sources.*

## Iuno Resonance Chain S5: Ethereal Gleam
Minor improvements to Iuno's passive stats and Resonance Liberation secondary effects. Exact wording not confirmed in available sources; community consensus describes this as offering "only minimal improvements" (Wutheringlab).

*Impact: Lowest-value node by broad consensus. Skip or treat as an incidental acquisition on the way to S6.*

## Iuno Resonance Chain S6: Eclipse Ascendant
The DMG Multiplier of Heavy Attack – Absolute Fullness is increased by **+1600%**. Upon casting Absolute Fullness, Iuno **re-enters Lunar Cycle – New Moon**, gains **100 points of Sentience**, and **resets all cooldowns of Resonance Skill – Arc Beyond the Edge**.

*Impact (Prydwen benchmarks): 263.69% relative to S0 baseline — an increase of ~163.69% total from S0. Wutheringlab: at least +101% personal DPS if the changed rotation is fully utilized. This node is a complete mechanical overhaul: (1) +1600% to Absolute Fullness — turning it from a damage-moderate healing finisher into one of the highest-damage single hits in Iuno's kit; (2) Automatic re-entry into New Moon + 100 Sentience + Arc Beyond the Edge reset means the post-Fullness window is now an additional full New Moon burst phase rather than a rotation end; (3) This changes the S6 optimal rotation entirely — players should loop the Absolute Fullness → New Moon → Arc × 2 → Absolute Fullness cycle rather than using the pre-S6 rotation. The damage jump from S5 to S6 is the largest single node jump in Iuno's chain (+197.05% → +263.69%).*

**Resonance Chain Priority:** S1 > S2 > S3 > S4 > S6 >> S5

- Why it's Best-in-Slot: Grants the two most valuable stats for Iuno simultaneously (Aero DMG and Liberation DMG Bonus), providing an immediate 24% total DMG contribution before skill use. Triggers off-field for easy rotation integration.

**Alternative: Kerasaur (4-Cost Echo — same bonuses as Lady of the Sea):**
- Off-field Echo (no precise timing required). Same 12% Aero DMG + 12% Liberation DMG bonuses if equipped in main slot. Slightly less precise but more forgiving to use.

## Iuno Echo Sets: Echo Stat Priority
**4-Cost Echo Main Stat:**
- S0–S3: Crit Rate (priority — target 60–70% before overcapping)
- S4–S5: Crit Rate or Crit DMG (flex depending on build needs)
- S6: Crit DMG (personal ATK and Crit Rate become more naturally abundant; focus excess into Crit DMG)

**3-Cost Echo Main Stats:**
- Double Aero DMG Bonus (preferred — Aero DMG Bonus applies to ~95%+ of Iuno's damage output)
- Alternative: One Aero DMG Bonus + One ATK% (acceptable if double Aero not available; slight performance loss)

**1-Cost Echo Main Stats:** ATK% × 2

## Iuno Echo Sets: Substat Priority
1. Energy Regen until 120–135% (minimum threshold first)
2. Crit Rate = Crit DMG (balanced weight; favor Crit DMG once Crit Rate ≥ 70%)
3. Resonance Liberation DMG Bonus (valuable — scales the majority of her output)
4. ATK% (good; also boosts shield value via Waxing Ascent)
5. Flat ATK (last resort)

## Iuno: Character Strengths and Weaknesses in Actual Gameplay

## Iuno: Strengths
- **True "jack of all trades" support:** Simultaneously provides healing (consistent across New Moon attacks and Absolute Fullness), stamina restoration (Full Moon Domain), shielding (Waxing Ascent — enables Crown of Valor + DEF ignore for the team at S4), and a 50% Heavy Attack DMG Amplification Outro — all from a single character rotation
- **50% Heavy Attack Outro is the best in class:** No other accessible character in Wuthering Waves as of Version 3.0 matches this single buff value for Heavy Attack DPS characters. It synergizes multiplicatively with other buffs rather than additively
- **Strong personal DPS:** Unlike most support characters, Iuno's Resonance Liberation DMG output is genuinely high — she is not just a buffer who happens to be on the field, she contributes meaningful damage in her sub-DPS rotation window
- **Flexible rotations:** She has an "extended" rotation for Augusta (utilizing the full Half Moon → New Moon → Absolute Fullness chain) and a "short" rotation for characters like Jiyan (Intro → Liberation → New Moon burst → Outro only, cutting unnecessary support-oriented steps they don't need)
- **Consistent off-field healing and stamina recovery:** The Full Moon Domain passively heals and restores stamina to all Resonators inside it — the stamina recovery is unusually valuable in endgame content where dodging frequently drains stamina
- **Graceful mid-air combat:** Meandering flight during Lunar Cycle allows repositioning and combat continuation without stamina concerns (within the flight's own STA budget), making her highly mobile
- **High personal survivability:** Constant shields from Waxing Ascent make her difficult to knock down from rapid small hits; consistent in-field healing keeps her topped up

## Iuno: Weaknesses
- **At S0, Full Moon Domain only buffs Iuno:** Blessing of the Wan Light's 40% All DMG Amplification is exclusive to Iuno's own damage at S0. The team-wide value only comes online at S2. Without S2, her support value outside the Outro is significantly lower — essentially only the Outro 50% Heavy Attack buff and the passive healing/shield provide team utility
- **Almost exclusively beneficial for Heavy Attack DPS characters:** The 50% Outro is specifically Heavy Attack Amplification. Characters without meaningful Heavy Attack DMG (e.g., Changli, Camellya, Carlotta, most caster-style DPS) cannot leverage Iuno's primary support contribution at all
- **Self-shields only until S4:** The shields from Waxing Ascent are self-only at S0–S3. This means Crown of Valor stacking, DEF ignore stacks, and team shielding from Moongazer's Sigil are all unavailable to teammates before S4
- **Moderate execution requirement:** Managing three distinct skill modes (Moonring, Moonbow, Closing Refrain/Unfinished Refrain/Arc) + monitoring Sentience + Concerto + Wan Light stacks simultaneously requires practice. Her rotation is more complex than most sub-DPS characters
- **Liberation is energy-hungry:** 150 Resonance Energy is a high cost for a sub-DPS rotation. Before S1, Energy Regen substat investment is mandatory to ensure consistent Liberation cycling. S1 alleviates this substantially via in-domain Energy regen
- **Movement during Lunar Cycle consumes STA continuously:** Meandering flight is powerful but not free — prolonged flight drains STA. In extended manual combat, stamina management requires attention
- **Performance plateau without premium investment:** At S0 without Signature weapon, her support ceiling is notably lower than Augusta's best-case scenario. She is still functional and strong, but the gap between S0/F2P Iuno and S2/Signature Iuno is one of the wider performance ranges in the game
