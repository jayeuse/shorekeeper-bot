## Brant: Combat Archetype and Role

- **Element/Attribute:** Fusion
- **Weapon Type:** Sword (switches between sword, whip, and pistol mid-combo)
- **Role:** Hybrid DPS/Sub-DPS with Support capabilities (healing, shielding, buffing)
- **Scaling Stat:** ATK (primary) + Energy Regeneration (unique secondary scaling)
- **Damage Type:** Primarily **Basic Attack DMG** (majority of damage output) + some Resonance Skill DMG; Enhanced Forte skill "Returned from Ashes" is considered **Basic Attack DMG**
- **Combat Style:** Mid-air combo specialist; aerial grapple-based attacks; builds Bravo gauge through mid-air sequences then unleashes Enhanced Resonance Skill for burst damage + team shield
- **Specialty:** Energy Regen → ATK conversion (gains +12 ATK per 1% ER above 150%, up to +1,560 ATK at 280% ER); team-wide healing at Bravo milestones (25/50/75/100); massive team shield from Forte Circuit; Outro buffs next character with +20% Fusion DMG and +25% Resonance Skill DMG Deepen

## Brant: Key Resources (Forte Mechanics Overview)

**Bravo (Primary Forte Gauge):**

- Max: 100 points
- Generation: Obtain Bravo when Normal Attacks, Intro Skill, or Resonance Skill hit targets
- **Mid-air Attacks** are the primary Bravo generation method (optimal playstyle)
- Consumption: When Bravo reaches 100, Resonance Skill "Anchors Aweigh!" is replaced with **"Returned from Ashes"** (Enhanced Resonance Skill); casting this consumes all Bravo
- **Passive Healing:** All nearby Resonators heal when Bravo reaches **25, 50, 75, and 100** points (healing scales with Energy Regen: 6.60 HP per 1% ER)

**Aflame State (Liberation Buff):**

- Triggered by: Casting Resonance Liberation
- Duration: 12 seconds
- Effects: Increases Bravo generation rate; enhances Basic Attack sequences; boosts overall combat performance
- Ends when: Duration expires OR Brant casts "Returned from Ashes" (Enhanced Resonance Skill ends Aflame state after the skill completes)

**Theatrical Moment (Energy Regen → ATK Conversion):**

- **Unique Scaling Mechanic:** For every **1% of Energy Regen over 150%**, Brant gains additional **+12 ATK**, up to **+1,560 ATK** bonus
- Breakpoint: 280% Energy Regen = maximum bonus (+130% × 12 = 1,560 ATK)
- Why Important: Makes Energy Regen a primary offensive stat for Brant, not just utility; allows him to scale damage through ER substats and Echo sets

**Returned from Ashes (Enhanced Forte Skill):**

- Replaces standard Resonance Skill when Bravo is full (100 points)
- Deals massive Fusion DMG (considered **Basic Attack DMG** type for buff purposes)
- Generates **9,000+ team-wide shield** lasting 30 seconds (individual shields per character; cannot be transferred when swapping)
- Generates **50 Concerto Energy** immediately (fills half Concerto gauge)
- If cast while in Aflame state, ends Aflame after skill completes
- **Core to Brant's gameplay:** This skill is primary damage source (~60% of total damage) and primary reason to build Brant (shield + healing + burst damage in one ability)

## Brant: Character Kit: Basic Attack — Echoes from Afar

**Ground Basic Attack (4-Stage Chain, Rarely Used):**
Perform up to 4 consecutive attacks using sword, whip, and pistol, dealing Fusion DMG.

- **Total Multiplier:** ~1,900% at max level (but rarely utilized in optimal rotations)

**Mid-air Attack (Primary Combat Mode, 4-Stage Grapple Chain):**
This is Brant's **core combat mechanic**. Press Normal Attack following Mid-air Attack Stage 1 or Stage 2 to **swing to the target with Grapple**. If Brant reaches target with swing, he attacks and can:

- Release to flip backward after attack hits
- Hold Normal Attack to attack target continuously then flip backward after finishing move
- Brant flips backward automatically after Mid-air Attack Stage 3
- Press Normal Attack after each flip to perform next stage
- **Flip resets Mid-air Dodge attempts** (mobility mechanic)

**Grapple Mechanics:**

- If Brant **fails to reach target** with Stage 1 Grapple, he performs slash forward instead, dealing Fusion DMG
- If Brant **reaches target but attack misses**, he drops after temporary mid-air suspension

**Heavy Attack: Rhapsodic Riff:**
Consume STA to perform boosted attack dealing Fusion DMG. Can be activated after:

- Basic Attack Stage 2 or Stage 4 (hold Basic Attack button)
- Mid-air Attack Stage 4 (press Basic Attack button)

**Dodge Counter:**
Press Basic Attack after successful Dodge to attack target, dealing Fusion DMG.

**Plunging Attack:**
When in mid-air without full Bravo, can plunge at target at cost of STA, dealing Fusion DMG (considered **Basic Attack DMG**).

## Brant: Character Kit: Forte Circuit — Theatrical Moment & Waves of Acclaims

**Theatrical Moment (Passive ER → ATK Conversion):**
Brant gains additional ATK based on his Energy Regen:

- For every **1% of Energy Regen over 150%**, Brant gains **+12 ATK**
- Maximum bonus: **+1,560 ATK** (achieved at 280% ER)
- Formula: (Energy Regen - 150) × 12 = Bonus ATK
- Examples:
  - 200% ER: (200 - 150) × 12 = +600 ATK
  - 250% ER: (250 - 150) × 12 = +1,200 ATK
  - 280% ER: (280 - 150) × 12 = +1,560 ATK (maximum)

**Waves of Acclaims (Passive Milestone Healing):**
Heal all nearby Resonators in team when Bravo reaches **25, 50, 75, and 100** points.

- Healing Amount: **6.60 HP per 1% Energy Regen**
- At 250% ER: 1,650 HP per milestone × 4 milestones = **6,600 total healing per Bravo cycle**
- At 280% ER: 1,848 HP per milestone × 4 milestones = **7,392 total healing per Bravo cycle**

**Returned from Ashes (Enhanced Resonance Skill, described above):**
When Bravo is full, replace Resonance Skill with this enhanced version dealing massive damage and generating team shield.

**Bravo Gauge Mechanics:**

- Max: 100 points
- Obtain Bravo when Normal Attacks hit target
- Obtain Bravo when Intro Skill hits target
- Obtain Bravo when Resonance Skill hits target
- **Mid-air attacks are primary generation method**

## Brant: Resonance Chains (Sequence Nodes S1 - S6)

**S1: The Flaming Fool**

- **Returned from Ashes** temporarily causes nearby targets to **stagnate** (freeze in place) while casting
- Effect ends if Brant swaps out
- **Priority:** Moderate — CC utility is helpful but not gamechanging for DPS

**S2: The Joyful Fool**

- Using **Intro Skill "Applaud for Me!"** or each **flip after Mid-air Attack** boosts Brant's DMG by **+20%** for 5 seconds
- Stacks up to **3 times** (maximum +60% DMG bonus)
- **Priority:** High — consistent damage boost throughout rotation; easily maintained

**S3: The Faithful Fool**

- Casting **Mid-air Attack** or **Returned from Ashes** increases Brant's **CRIT Rate by +30%**
- **Priority:** Very High — massive CRIT Rate boost dramatically improves consistency; frees up Echo substats for CRIT DMG stacking

**S4: The Clever Fool**

- Brant's **Outro Skill "The Course is Set!"** gains new enhancement:
  - When Resonance Skill cast by incoming Resonator (or nearby Resonators who activated Brant's Outro) hits target within 20 seconds, Brant blasts the target
  - Deals **Fusion DMG equal to 440% of Brant's ATK** (considered **Basic Attack DMG**)
  - Triggers **1 per second**, up to **2 explosions total**
  - Remains active even when Brant is off-field
- **Priority:** Very High — adds significant damage to already-strong Outro buff; benefits team without requiring Brant on-field

**S5: The Unflickering Fool**

- **DMG Multiplier of Returned from Ashes** increased by **+42%**
- **Priority:** Highest single value node — Returned from Ashes is 60% of Brant's damage; +42% multiplier translates to ~25% total damage increase

**S6: The Immortal Fool**

- **Shield obtained from Returned from Ashes** increased by **+20%** (stacks with Inherent Skill 1 for total +40% shield boost)
- **Mid-air Attack DMG Multiplier** increased by **+30%**
- Casting **Returned from Ashes** causes **secondary blast** dealing Fusion DMG equal to **30% of Returned from Ashes damage** (considered **Basic Attack DMG**)
- **Priority:** Very High — triple benefit (stronger shield, stronger mid-air attacks, bonus explosion damage); secondary blast adds ~18% more damage on top of already-massive Returned from Ashes

## Brant: Character's Strengths and Weaknesses in Actual Gameplay

**Strengths:**

1. **Triple Threat: DPS + Healing + Shielding:** Brant is one of the most versatile characters in Wuthering Waves — he can act as main DPS while simultaneously providing 9,000+ team-wide shield and 6,600+ healing per rotation cycle
2. **Unique Energy Regen Scaling:** Converting ER → ATK makes him scale with a stat that also improves his utility (healing) and enables Echo set bonuses (Tidebreaking Courage 5pc); players don't have to choose between damage and sustain
3. **Powerful Outro Buffs:** +20% Fusion DMG + +25% Resonance Skill DMG Deepen makes him excellent support for Changli, Encore, Lupa, and Calcharo
4. **Mid-Air Combat Mastery:** Grapple-based aerial combos provide excellent dodge utility while dealing damage; flip mechanic resets mid-air dodge attempts allowing extended airborne time
5. **Low Gear Dependency for Utility:** Even with budget builds, Brant's shield and healing scale with ER making him useful support without heavy investment
6. **F2P Friendly:** Overture (craftable 4★) provides 51.8% ER enabling Tidebreaking Courage 5pc bonus; teams work with Verina (free selector) instead of premium supports
7. **Aesthetic Appeal:** Flashy weapon-switching combat (sword → whip → pistol) with theatrical animations makes him fun to play regardless of meta performance
8. **Team Flexibility:** Can slot into Mono Fusion teams, Hypercarry teams, or traditional DPS/Sub-DPS/Healer compositions

**Weaknesses:**

1. **Energy Regen Stat Requirement:** Needs 250%+ ER to maximize damage potential (Tidebreaking Courage 5pc + Theatrical Moment max bonus); requires specific Echo substats and weapon choice limiting flexibility
2. **Signature Weapon Dependency:** Unflickering Valor provides massive Basic Attack DMG bonuses tailored to his kit; alternatives lose 8-25% performance making him expensive to fully optimize
3. **Rotation Complexity:** Must manage Bravo gauge timing, Aflame state duration, mid-air combo execution, and Concerto generation simultaneously; higher skill floor than simpler characters
4. **Ground Combo Rarely Used:** Despite having 4-stage ground combo with ~1,900% multiplier, optimal play never uses it; feels like wasted design space
5. **Returned from Ashes Long Animation:** Enhanced Resonance Skill has lengthy animation; most vulnerable during initial spinning motion despite high interruption resistance during descent
6. **Liberation Relatively Weak:** Resonance Liberation damage is low (~850% at Lv.10) compared to other 5★ characters; primarily used for Aflame buff rather than damage
7. **Energy Generation Challenges in Multi-Wave:** Whimpering Wastes and similar content requires rebuilding Bravo from zero each wave; can feel sluggish without proper ER investment
8. **Team Slot Competition:** As hybrid DPS/support/healer, competes with pure DPS for damage ceiling, pure supports for buffing power, and pure healers for sustain; "jack of all trades, master of none" syndrome in some team comps
9. **Shield Cannot Transfer:** 9,000+ shield sounds massive but each character's shield is independent and **cannot be transferred when swapping**; if you swap before taking damage, shield value is wasted
10. **Standard Resonance Skill Rarely Used:** "Anchors Aweigh!" is mostly ignored in favor of building Bravo for Enhanced version; makes skill point investment feel inefficient
