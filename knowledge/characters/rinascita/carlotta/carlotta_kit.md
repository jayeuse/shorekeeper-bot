## Carlotta: Combat Archetype and Role
- **Element/Attribute:** Glacio
- **Weapon Type:** Pistols
- **Role:** Main DPS — ranged hypercarry dealing heavy Resonance Skill-focused burst damage
- **Scaling Stat:** ATK (primary); Crit Rate and Crit DMG (critical multipliers)
- **Damage Type:** Resonance Skill DMG (approximately 80% of her total damage output is classified as Resonance Skill DMG, even during Liberation animations)
- **Combat Style:** Resource-management ranged DPS — generates Moldable Crystals via attacks, consumes them to empower basic attacks and restore Substance, then uses Substance to activate enhanced Heavy Attacks and enter high-damage Resonance Liberation states (Twilight Tango). Plays from mid-to-long range with exceptional kiting and repositioning.
- **Specialty:**
  - Single-target damage monster — excels at boss deletion with concentrated Resonance Skill bursts
  - Two distinct ultimates with full animations (Era of New Wave standard + Twilight Tango Death Knell/Fatal Finale enhanced)
  - Tinted Crystal mechanic: every 22 seconds, can cast Imminent Oblivion (800%+ multiplier Heavy Attack) to enter Grand Unveiling (+80% DMG Multiplier to all Liberation skills)
  - Easy-to-execute rotation with clear visual/audio cues for resource management

## Carlotta: Key Resources (Forte Mechanics Overview)

## Carlotta Forte Circuit: Moldable Crystals, Substance, and Tinted Crystal
Carlotta's Forte Circuit manages three interlocking resource systems simultaneously.

**1. Moldable Crystals (Forte Gauge — Max 6 stacks, shown as opal-like crystals):**
- Generated by: Basic Attack Stage 2 (3 stacks), Dodging (3 stacks), Resonance Skill – Art of Violence (3 stacks), Heavy Attack before Resonance Skill (grants 6 stacks)
- Consumed by: Basic Attack – Necessary Measures (1 per hit, up to 3 hits), Resonance Skill – Chromatic Splendor (consumes all Moldable Crystals)
- Design note: Max 6 Moldable Crystals. Any crystal gained beyond 6 is lost — players must spend them before capping.
- Visual/Audio cue: Crystals appear as floating opals around Carlotta; a distinct chime plays when reaching 6 stacks.

**2. Substance (Forte Energy Bar — Max 120 points, displayed above HP bar, divided into two segments):**
- Restored by: Intro Skill – Wintertime Aria (30 points), consuming Moldable Crystals via Chromatic Splendor (10 points per crystal), consuming Moldable Crystals via Necessary Measures (10 points per crystal)
- Consumed by: Heavy Attack – Containment Tactics (consumes all Substance when bar is full, reduces Resonance Skill cooldown by 6s), Heavy Attack – Imminent Oblivion (consumes all Substance when Tinted Crystal is active, triggers Grand Unveiling state)
- Design note: Substance is the gateway to both cooldown reduction and the enhanced Liberation state. At 120/120, Heavy Attack becomes empowered.

**3. Tinted Crystal (Cooldown Mechanic — Activates every 22 seconds):**
- Activation: Automatically activates every 22 seconds (cooldown-based, not player-triggered).
- Visual cue: A diamond-shaped crystal in the center of the Forte Circuit Bar turns red when active.
- When active AND Substance is full (120/120): Holding Normal Attack casts Heavy Attack – Imminent Oblivion (consuming all Substance, dealing ~800%+ ATK Glacio DMG classified as Resonance Skill DMG, reducing Resonance Skill cooldown by 6s, entering Grand Unveiling state).
- **Grand Unveiling State:** Increases DMG Multiplier of Resonance Liberation – Era of New Wave, Death Knell, and Fatal Finale by 80% for the duration. Ends when Carlotta is switched off-field or when Twilight Tango ends.
- Design note: Tinted Crystal is the single most important mechanic to maximize. Missing the 22s window wastes the Grand Unveiling +80% buff.

## Carlotta: Inherent Passives
**Inherent Skill 1 – Final Bow:**
When Substance is full (120/120), enter the **Final Bow state**. Increases the DMG Multiplier of Resonance Liberation – Era of New Wave, Resonance Liberation – Death Knell, and Resonance Liberation – Fatal Finale by **80%**. This effect ends when Carlotta is switched off the field during Twilight Tango or when Twilight Tango ends.

- This is the baseline +80% buff without Tinted Crystal. Grand Unveiling (from Imminent Oblivion via Tinted Crystal) provides an additional +80% multiplicatively, totaling 1.8 × 1.8 = 3.24× (224% increase at full optimization).
- Without Final Bow active, Liberation damage is significantly weaker. Maintaining Substance at full before casting Liberation is critical.

**Inherent Skill 2 – Crystalline Precision:**
Exact wording not confirmed in available sources. Community consensus suggests this passive enhances Moldable Crystal generation or Glacio DMG output. Consult in-game for precise details.

## Carlotta Basic Attack: Necessary Measures
**Base Basic Attack (Stages 1–2):**
Perform up to 2 consecutive strikes with dual pistols, dealing Glacio DMG.
- Stage 1: Single pistol shot, Glacio DMG
- Stage 2: Dual pistol volley, Glacio DMG — **grants 3 Moldable Crystals on hit**

**Basic Attack – Necessary Measures (with Moldable Crystals):**
When Carlotta has Moldable Crystals, her Basic Attack is replaced with Necessary Measures. Chain up to 3 strikes, dealing Glacio DMG. Each strike of Necessary Measures consumes 1 Moldable Crystal.
- Stage 1: Enhanced pistol shot (1 Moldable Crystal consumed, restores 10 Substance)
- Stage 2: Enhanced pistol shot (1 Moldable Crystal consumed, restores 10 Substance)
- Stage 3: Enhanced pistol shot (1 Moldable Crystal consumed, restores 10 Substance)

**Heavy Attack – Containment Tactics (Substance full):**
When Carlotta's Substance is full (120/120), her Heavy Attack is replaced with Containment Tactics: consume all Substance to deal Glacio DMG and reduce the cooldown of Resonance Skill – Art of Violence by 6 seconds.

**Heavy Attack – Imminent Oblivion (Substance full + Tinted Crystal active):**
When Substance is full AND Tinted Crystal is activated (every 22s), hold Normal Attack to consume all Substance and cast Heavy Attack – Imminent Oblivion. Deals ~800%+ Glacio DMG (classified as Resonance Skill DMG), reduces Resonance Skill cooldown by 6s, and enters **Grand Unveiling state** (+80% DMG Multiplier to all Liberation skills). Tinted Crystal enters cooldown after cast.

**Mid-Air Attack – Customary Greetings:**
Perform a Plunging Attack at the cost of STA, dealing Glacio DMG. Using Basic Attack shortly after landing casts Customary Greetings: flip over the target and deliver a surprise shot, dealing Glacio DMG.

**Dodge Counter:**
Normal Attack shortly after a successful Dodge to deliver a riposte shot, dealing Glacio DMG. Dodging grants 3 Moldable Crystals.

## Carlotta Resonance Skill: Art of Violence / Chromatic Splendor
**Resonance Skill – Art of Violence (first cast):**
- **Cooldown:** Moderate (~10–12s base; reduced by Heavy Attacks)
- Deal Glacio DMG and inflict **Dispersion** on the target. Grants 3 Moldable Crystals. Casting Resonance Skill again shortly after triggers Chromatic Splendor.
- **Dispersion:** Targets with Dispersion are immobilized for 1.5 seconds (crowd control effect).

**Resonance Skill – Chromatic Splendor (second cast, follow-up):**
Consume all Moldable Crystals and deal Glacio DMG. Restores 10 Substance per Moldable Crystal consumed (max 60 Substance if 6 Moldable Crystals consumed). The Resonance Skill enters cooldown after a delay if Chromatic Splendor is not cast or Carlotta is switched off-field.

- There is a large window between the two casts — enough time to perform either 6 Basic Attack – Necessary Measures or 4 shots of Death Knell (during Liberation). Players should maximize damage output during this window.

## Carlotta Resonance Liberation: Era of New Wave / Twilight Tango / Death Knell / Fatal Finale
**Resonance Liberation – Era of New Wave (standard Liberation):**
- **Cost:** 125 Resonance Energy
- Effect: Deal Glacio DMG to all targets in an area (classified as Resonance Skill DMG), inflict **Deconstruction** on targets hit, then activate **Twilight Tango**. Can be cast mid-air close to the ground.
- **Deconstruction:** Dealing DMG to targets inflicted with Deconstruction ignores 18% of their DEF (debuff lasts for duration of Twilight Tango).
- **Twilight Tango State (duration varies based on Meta Vectors):** While in Twilight Tango, press Normal Attack or Resonance Liberation to cast Death Knell. Each Death Knell grants 1 **Meta Vector**. With 4 Meta Vectors, press Normal Attack or Resonance Liberation to cast Fatal Finale.

**Twilight Tango – Death Knell (up to 4 casts):**
Carlotta fires powerful shots with her musket and summons 4 crystal shards to attack the target, dealing Glacio DMG (classified as Resonance Skill DMG). Carlotta moves in the direction of movement input with each shot (excellent repositioning tool). Each Death Knell cast grants 1 Meta Vector.

**Twilight Tango – Fatal Finale (finisher, requires 4 Meta Vectors):**
Deal massive Glacio DMG to an area (classified as Resonance Skill DMG). Twilight Tango ends after casting Fatal Finale. All other Resonators are removed from the field.

## Carlotta Forte Circuit: Resource Management Summary
Carlotta's Forte Circuit visually displays all three resources — Moldable Crystals (opal icons), Substance (bar above HP), and Tinted Crystal (diamond center) — simultaneously. The rotation flow is:

1. Generate Moldable Crystals via Basic Attack Stage 2, Dodge, or Resonance Skill
2. Consume Moldable Crystals via Necessary Measures or Chromatic Splendor to restore Substance
3. At 120/120 Substance + Tinted Crystal active (every 22s) → cast Imminent Oblivion → enter Grand Unveiling
4. Cast Liberation (Era of New Wave) while in Grand Unveiling → enter Twilight Tango with +80% DMG Multiplier
5. Execute Death Knell ×4 → Fatal Finale
6. Repeat cycle

**Forte Circuit Attribute Scaling:**
The Forte Circuit does not have independent DMG entries. All outputs are classified under Basic Attack, Resonance Skill, or Resonance Liberation categories above.

## Carlotta: Resonance Chains (Sequence Nodes S1–S6)

## Carlotta Resonance Chain S1: Nascent Radiance
Casting Intro Skill or Resonance Skill restores **60 additional Substance**. Resonance Skill – Art of Violence and Resonance Skill – Chromatic Splendor can restore up to **120 Substance total**.

*Impact (Wutheringlab estimate): ~9% personal DPS increase. This node dramatically accelerates Substance generation, enabling faster access to Imminent Oblivion and reducing rotation downtime. With S1, players can execute: Intro Skill + Resonance Skill + Mid-Air Attack + Heavy Attack – Imminent Oblivion + Resonance Liberation in a single smooth combo without waiting for additional Moldable Crystal cycles. Highly recommended first purchase from Coral Shop.*

## Carlotta Resonance Chain S2: Flourishing Bloom
The DMG Multiplier of Resonance Liberation – Era of New Wave, Death Knell, and Fatal Finale is increased by **126%**. This effect is multiplicative with Final Bow's 80% increase.

*Impact (Wutheringlab estimate): ~33% personal DPS increase. The single highest-value individual node in Carlotta's chain. At S2, Liberation multipliers scale: base × 1.8 (Final Bow) × 2.26 (S2 multiplier) = 4.068× base (307% increase from baseline when both are active). Carlotta's Liberation window (which accounts for ~53% of her total damage) becomes exponentially more powerful. Community consensus: S2 is one of the strongest single sequence node upgrades for any DPS in Wuthering Waves as of Version 2.0–2.6.*

## Carlotta Resonance Chain S3: Eternal Aria
Casting Resonance Skill grants **22.5% Glacio DMG Bonus** for **15 seconds**, and casting Resonance Liberation increases **Resonance Skill DMG by 18%**, lasting for **5 seconds**. This Resonance Skill DMG effect stacks up to **2 times** (max 36% Resonance Skill DMG Bonus).

*Impact (Wutheringlab estimate): ~24% personal DPS increase. Provides persistent Glacio DMG amplification and stacking Resonance Skill DMG. Since 80% of Carlotta's damage is Resonance Skill DMG, the 36% Resonance Skill DMG Bonus (at 2 stacks) applies to the vast majority of her output. This node alone raises her damage floor and ceiling substantially. Rated A-tier in S3 tier lists.*

## Carlotta Resonance Chain S4: Glacial Overture
When Resonance Skill – Chromatic Splendor consumes Moldable Crystals, the **cooldown of Resonance Skill – Art of Violence is reduced by 1 second per Moldable Crystal consumed** (max 6s cooldown reduction).

*Impact: Primarily a quality-of-life and rotation fluidity node. Reduces the need to rely on Heavy Attack cooldown reductions and enables more frequent Resonance Skill cycles. Moderate personal DPS value (~5–10% depending on rotation optimization). Lower priority for min-maxing compared to S1, S2, S3, and S6.*

## Carlotta Resonance Chain S5: Crystalline Resonance
Minor improvements to Carlotta's passive stats and Resonance Liberation secondary effects. Exact wording not fully confirmed in available sources.

*Impact: Lowest-value node by community consensus. Skip or treat as an incidental acquisition on the way to S6.*

## Carlotta Resonance Chain S6: Symphony of Winter's End
The DMG Multiplier of Resonance Liberation – Era of New Wave, Death Knell, and Fatal Finale is increased by **an additional 70%** (multiplicative with S2's 126% increase and Final Bow's 80% increase). Casting Resonance Liberation – Era of New Wave extends the duration of **Twilight Tango by 8 seconds**.

*Impact (Prydwen benchmarks): 235.03% relative to S0 baseline — an increase of +135.03% total from S0. Wutheringlab: +77% personal DPS if the extended Twilight Tango is fully utilized. This node provides: (1) a massive final multiplier boost to all Liberation skills (base × 1.8 × 2.26 × 1.7 = ~6.9× baseline multiplier at full optimization with Final Bow + Grand Unveiling + S2 + S6); (2) +8 seconds to Twilight Tango duration, allowing 2 additional Death Knell casts (6 total instead of 4), dramatically extending the burst window. The damage jump from S5 to S6 is the largest single node jump in Carlotta's chain (+183.51% → +235.03%). S6 changes the optimal rotation entirely — players should execute an extended Death Knell × 6 burst rather than the pre-S6 Death Knell × 4 rotation.*

**Resonance Chain Priority:** S2 > S3 > S1 > S6 >> S4 > S5

## Carlotta Best Team: Carlotta + Zhezhi + Shorekeeper
**Role assignment:** Carlotta (Main DPS) | Zhezhi (Sub-DPS / Glacio + Resonance Skill Buffer) | Shorekeeper (Healer / Crit Buffer)

**Why this team:** Zhezhi provides up to 90% total DMG Amplification to Carlotta — 40% All DMG (via Outro buff) + 50% Resonance Skill DMG Amplification (via Ink Paintings coordinated attacks). Since 80% of Carlotta's damage is Resonance Skill DMG, Zhezhi's buffs apply to nearly her entire output. Shorekeeper provides CRIT Rate, CRIT DMG, healing, and Stellarealm utility. This is Carlotta's highest-performing team as of Version 2.0–2.6 per Prydwen and Wutheringlab benchmarks.

**Team Rotation — Best Team (Carlotta + Zhezhi + Shorekeeper):**
1. Start with **Shorekeeper** → build Resonance and Concerto Energy → activate Stellarealm → switch to **Zhezhi** when her Intro is available
2. **Zhezhi** enters via Intro Skill → Resonance Skill to summon Ink Paintings (off-field coordinated attacks begin) → Basic Attack combos to build Concerto → Outro Skill (provides 40% All DMG + 50% Resonance Skill DMG to incoming Resonator)
3. **Carlotta** enters with 40% All DMG + 50% Resonance Skill DMG buffs → Heavy Attack (if no Moldable Crystals) → Basic Attack Stage 2 (3 Moldable Crystals) → Resonance Skill – Art of Violence (3 more Moldable Crystals, total 6)
4. Execute 3× Necessary Measures (consuming 3 Moldable Crystals, restoring 30 Substance)
5. Cast Chromatic Splendor (consuming remaining 3 Moldable Crystals, restoring 30 Substance, total 90 Substance including Intro's 30)
6. Build remaining 30 Substance via additional Moldable Crystal generation
7. When Tinted Crystal activates (22s mark) + Substance full → Hold Normal Attack → cast **Imminent Oblivion** → enter Grand Unveiling
8. Immediately cast **Liberation – Era of New Wave** (with Grand Unveiling + Final Bow active = 224% multiplier increase) → enter Twilight Tango
9. Execute Death Knell ×4 → Fatal Finale (all with +224% multiplier)
10. Outro → Shorekeeper re-enters; repeat

## Carlotta Alternative Team 1: Carlotta + Cantarella + Shorekeeper
**Role assignment:** Carlotta (Main DPS) | Cantarella (Sub-DPS / Skill DMG Buffer) | Shorekeeper (Healer / Crit Buffer)

**Why this team:** Cantarella provides Skill DMG buffs via her Outro and contributes personal damage with coordinated attacks. Since ~80% of Carlotta's damage is Resonance Skill DMG, Cantarella's buffs apply meaningfully. Shorekeeper sustains and provides CRIT Rate/DMG buffs. This team is accessible for players without Zhezhi and performs competitively.

**Team Rotation:**
1. **Shorekeeper** → build gauges → Stellarealm → switch to **Cantarella**
2. **Cantarella** → Resonance Skill rotation → Coordinated Attack setup → Outro (Skill DMG buff to Carlotta)
3. **Carlotta** → full burst rotation (same as above: Moldable Crystals → Substance → Imminent Oblivion → Liberation)
4. Outro → Shorekeeper; repeat

## Carlotta: Strengths
- **Exceptional single-target damage:** Carlotta excels at boss deletion with concentrated Resonance Skill bursts. Her Twilight Tango combo (Death Knell ×4 + Fatal Finale) deals massive damage in a short window.
- **Easy, simple, hard-to-mess-up playstyle:** Carlotta's rotation is straightforward once understood. Clear visual/audio cues (Moldable Crystal chimes, Tinted Crystal glow, Substance bar) make resource management intuitive.
- **Ranged playstyle with excellent repositioning:** Playing from mid-to-long range with Death Knell movement inputs allows Carlotta to reposition while maintaining DPS, reducing vulnerability to enemy attacks.
- **Incredible damage output when set-up and buffed correctly:** With Zhezhi + Shorekeeper + Frosty Resolve + The Last Dance, Carlotta is one of the highest single-target DPS characters in Wuthering Waves as of Version 2.0–2.6.
- **Two distinct ultimates with full animations:** Era of New Wave → Twilight Tango provides visual spectacle and mechanical depth, making her engaging to play.
- **Frosty Resolve set is nearly exclusive to her:** Frosty Resolve was designed for Carlotta and brings a big damage increase over older sets, giving her a dedicated build path.

## Carlotta: Weaknesses
- **Single-target focus limits AoE performance:** Carlotta has "some AoE" (Era of New Wave, Chromatic Splendor) but is primarily single-target. In multi-wave content like Whimpering Wastes, her damage is less efficient than characters with native AoE.
- **Long cooldowns on strongest damage abilities:** Resonance Skill and Tinted Crystal (22s) have moderate-to-long cooldowns. Missing the Tinted Crystal window wastes the Grand Unveiling buff and significantly lowers DPS.
- **Rotation backloaded into Liberation window:** ~53% of Carlotta's damage is concentrated in the Liberation burst (Death Knell + Fatal Finale). If enemies die mid-Liberation or the next wave doesn't spawn until after Twilight Tango ends, a significant portion of damage is wasted.
- **Echo set is almost exclusively used on her:** Frosty Resolve is tailor-made for Carlotta. Other Glacio characters (like Sanhua, Jinhsi pre-2.0) do not benefit meaningfully from it, making the echo set less flexible for team-building.
- **Resource management requires attention:** Managing three resources (Moldable Crystals, Substance, Tinted Crystal) simultaneously is more complex than simpler DPS characters. Players must track multiple cooldowns and resource bars.
- **Vulnerable to interruption during Resonance Skill:** Art of Violence and Chromatic Splendor have brief animation locks. Heavy enemy attacks can interrupt and reset the Resonance Skill cooldown, costing significant DPS.
- **High Substance dependency:** Without full Substance (120/120), Carlotta cannot access Imminent Oblivion or Containment Tactics, reducing her damage potential. Poor Substance management tanks DPS.
