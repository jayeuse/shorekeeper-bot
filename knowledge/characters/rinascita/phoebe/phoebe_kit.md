## Phoebe: Combat Archetype and Role

- **Element/Attribute:** Spectro
- **Weapon Type:** Rectifier (staff-based combat)
- **Role:** Main Damage Dealer (Absolution Mode, T1 tier) OR Hybrid Support/Sub-DPS (Confession Mode, T1.5 tier)
- **Scaling Stat:** ATK (all damage scales off Attack stat)
- **Damage Type:** Primarily **Basic Attack DMG** (majority of rotation) + **Heavy Attack DMG** (Starflash finishers) + **Spectro Frazzle DMG** (applied in Confession mode)
- **Combat Style:** Dual-mode stance-switching character; players choose between Absolution (selfish DPS) or Confession (team buffer/enabler) at start of each rotation; medium field-time with flexible rotation lengths
- **Specialty:** Spectro Frazzle specialist — either exploits it (Absolution) or applies/amplifies it (Confession); Ring of Mirrors zone control with enemy grouping; exceptional F2P-friendliness with viable 4★ weapons and team options

## Phoebe: Key Resources (Forte Mechanics Overview)

**Prayer (Primary Stance Resource):**

- Max: 120
- Regeneration: **5 per second** (passive, automatic) = 24 seconds to full
- Purpose: When full, consumed to enter either Absolution or Confession mode
- **Cannot manually stop or control** — always regenerates passively

**Divine Voice (Burst Attack Resource):**

- Max: 60
- Generated by: Casting Absolution Litany OR Utter Confession (restores full 60 immediately)
- Consumed by: Heavy Attack Starflash (15 in Absolution, 30 in Confession)
- Purpose: Enables special Heavy Attack finishers after Basic Attack Stage 3 or Dodge Counter
- **Cannot generate Divine Voice outside of entering Absolution/Confession modes**

**Absolution Mode (Selfish DPS Stance):**

- Entered by: Hold Basic Attack with 120 Prayer → cast **Absolution Litany** (consumes all Prayer)
- Duration: Permanent until Confession is cast OR until Prayer refills and stance is manually changed
- Bonuses:
  - +12% Spectro DMG Bonus (Inherent Skill 2)
  - Heavy Attack Starflash costs only 15 Divine Voice (can cast 4 per rotation)
  - Heavy Attack Starflash gains **+256% DMG Amplification** when hitting targets with Spectro Frazzle
  - Resonance Liberation gains +255% DMG Multiplier boost
  - Outro Skill gains +255% DMG Multiplier boost
- Playstyle: Main DPS who exploits Spectro Frazzle applied by teammates (Spectro Rover, Yuanwu, etc.)

**Confession Mode (Team Buffer/Enabler Stance):**

- Entered by: Hold Resonance Skill with 120 Prayer → cast **Utter Confession** (consumes all Prayer)
- Duration: Permanent until Absolution is cast OR until Prayer refills and stance is manually changed
- Bonuses:
  - +12% Spectro DMG Bonus (Inherent Skill 2)
  - Heavy Attack Starflash applies **5 stacks of Spectro Frazzle** to targets hit
  - Resonance Liberation applies **8 stacks of Spectro Frazzle** to targets hit
  - Outro Skill grants **Silent Prayer** buff to next character:
    - Reduces Spectro RES of nearby targets by 10%
    - Grants **100% Spectro Frazzle DMG Amplification**
    - Extends Spectro Frazzle damage interval by 50% (stacks last longer)
    - Lasts 30 seconds or until Phoebe switches to Absolution
- Playstyle: Hybrid sub-DPS who applies massive Spectro Frazzle and buffs Main DPS (currently only Zani benefits fully)

**Ring of Mirrors (Zone Control Mechanic):**

- Summoned by: Resonance Skill at target location
- Duration: 30 seconds (new Ring replaces existing one)
- Effects:
  - Stagnates targets hit for 2 seconds (freezes up to 12 enemies)
  - Can teleport Phoebe to Ring center by recasting Resonance Skill shortly after summoning
  - **Outside Ring:** Basic Attacks hitting the Ring refract Holy Light, dealing Spectro DMG to targets inside and **pulling them to center** (triggers once per 0.5s)
  - **Inside Ring:** Basic Attack becomes **Chamuel's Star** (3-hit enhanced chain dealing ~double damage)

## Phoebe: Resonance Chains (Sequence Nodes S1 - S6)

**S1: Mode-Specific Liberation Boosts**

- **When in Absolution:** Resonance Liberation DMG Multiplier increase changes from +255% to **+480%** (total ~860% at Lv.10; approximately 35% total damage increase for Absolution builds)
- **When in Confession:** Resonance Liberation DMG Multiplier increases by **+90%** and applies Spectro Frazzle to targets with the **maximum stack the targets can receive** (no longer caps at 8; applies full 10 stacks if target can hold them)
- **Priority:** Very High for Absolution (massive Liberation damage boost); High for Confession (guarantees max Frazzle stacks)

**S2: Outro and Silent Prayer Amplification**

- **When in Absolution:** DMG dealt by Outro Skills to targets with Spectro Frazzle is **Amplified by +120%**
- **When in Confession:** Silent Prayer grants **+120% more DMG Amplification** for Spectro Frazzle (total **220% Spectro Frazzle DMG Amplification** instead of 100%)
- **Priority:** Highest single value node — S2 Confession Phoebe is mandatory for optimal Zani teams (+120% Frazzle Amp is gamechanging); S2 Absolution Outro becomes a true nuke

**S3: Starflash Multiplier Increases**

- **When in Absolution:** DMG Multiplier of Heavy Attack Starflash increased by **+91%**
- **When in Confession:** DMG Multiplier of Heavy Attack Starflash increased by **+249%**
- **Priority:** Very High — Starflash is the primary damage source in both modes; +249% in Confession transforms it from utility (Frazzle application) to genuine damage

**S4: Team-Wide Spectro RES Shred**

- When Basic Attack, Basic Attack Chamuel's Star, Dodge Counter, or Chamuel's Star: Dodge Counter hits, target's **Spectro RES is reduced by 10%** for **30 seconds**
- **Priority:** High — unconditional team-wide RES shred with permanent uptime; benefits all Spectro teammates

**S5: Intro Skill Spectro DMG Buff**

- Casting Intro Skill Golden Grace increases Phoebe's **Spectro DMG Bonus by +12%** for **15 seconds**
- **Priority:** Moderate — free +12% Spectro DMG at start of every rotation; stacks with Inherent Skill 2 for total +24% Spectro DMG

**S6: Ring of Mirrors Enhancements + Free Starflash**

- Targets entering Ring of Mirrors are stagnated for **additional 2 seconds** (total 4s stagnation)
- Stagnation now affects **all targets entering the Ring** (not just initial cast), up to 12 targets max; each target affected once
- **When in Absolution or Confession:** Summoning Ring with Resonance Skill increases Phoebe's **ATK by +10%** for 20s and triggers an **extra Heavy Attack Starflash** at Ring location (does not consume Divine Voice; not considered casting Heavy Attack for animation cancel purposes)
- **Priority:** Very High for speedclears (+10% ATK, free Starflash = extra damage without extending rotation); moderate for casual play (QoL improvements)

## Phoebe: Character's Strengths and Weaknesses in Actual Gameplay

**Strengths:**

1. **Dual-Mode Flexibility:** Phoebe can function as either a Main DPS (Absolution) or Hybrid Support (Confession) by simply choosing which Forte skill to cast; allows players to adapt to roster and content
2. **Exceptional F2P-Friendliness:** Absolution Phoebe teams work perfectly fine with Spectro Rover (free) + Verina (selector) + 4★ weapons like Ocean's Gift or Augment; no premium investment required beyond Phoebe herself
3. **Wide AoE and Enemy Grouping:** Ring of Mirrors pulls enemies to center; Chamuel's Star attacks have broad cleave; handles multi-target content excellently
4. **Straightforward Damage Output (Absolution):** Once Spectro Frazzle is applied, Phoebe's damage is consistent and easy to execute; no complex combos or animation cancels required
5. **Best-in-Class Spectro Frazzle Application (Confession):** Applies 16–18 stacks per rotation; no other character comes close; enables Zani's full potential
6. **High Mobility:** Mid-air Heavy Attack can be cast twice (Inherent Skill 1); staff-riding provides horizontal movement; useful for exploration and dodging
7. **Benefits Minimal from Outro Buffs in Absolution:** While seemingly a weakness, this means she's **not dependent on premium supports** — her Starflash +256% Amp is self-sufficient; can pair with any healer

**Weaknesses:**

1. **Absolution Mode Multiwave Problem:** When new enemies spawn in Whimpering Wastes or similar content, they have **no Spectro Frazzle**; Phoebe's damage drops dramatically until teammates reapply Frazzle; significantly hurts her Wastes performance
2. **Absolution Requires Spectro Frazzle Teammate:** Cannot self-apply sufficient Frazzle for her +256% Starflash Amp; locks her into Spectro Rover / Yuanwu / Ciaccona teams; restricts team-building
3. **Confession Mode Extremely Restrictive:** Currently **only Zani** benefits from Confession buffs; if you don't own Zani, Confession mode is nearly useless; future Spectro Frazzle DPS characters may expand this, but as of now it's a single-character synergy
4. **Confession Deals Subpar Personal Damage:** Phoebe in Confession mode is not a Main DPS; her damage is 50–60% of Absolution; entirely reliant on buffing Zani to carry teamwide DPS
5. **Ring of Mirrors Summon is Stationary:** Once placed, Ring cannot be moved for 30 seconds; if boss teleports away, Ring value drops; requires prediction of enemy positioning
6. **Divine Voice Cannot be Generated Mid-Rotation:** Must fully exhaust Divine Voice before casting Absolution Litany or Utter Confession again; cannot "top off" the resource; if rotation is interrupted, must complete it or wait for Prayer refill
7. **Prayer Regeneration is Fixed and Slow:** 24 seconds to refill 120 Prayer; cannot accelerate this; locks rotation cadence rigidly; no flexibility for faster cycling
