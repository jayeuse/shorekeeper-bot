## Zani: Combat Archetype and Role
- **Element/Attribute:** Spectro
- **Weapon Type:** Gauntlets (visually wields a giant flaming sword in combat animations)
- **Role:** Main Damage Dealer (S-Tier) — Spectro Frazzle specialist; premiere Spectro DPS alongside Phoebe
- **Scaling Stat:** ATK (all damage scales off Attack stat)
- **Damage Type:** Primarily **Heavy Attack DMG** + **Spectro Frazzle DMG** (simultaneously counted as both types during Inferno Mode)
- **Combat Style:** Stance-switching DPS; enters "Inferno Mode" via Resonance Liberation; parry-focused with counterattack mechanics
- **Specialty:** High-risk/high-reward burst windows; exceptional tankiness via damage reduction and parry mechanics; requires Spectro Frazzle teammates to maximize Blaze generation

## Zani: Key Resources (Forte Mechanics Overview)

**Redundant Energy (Pre-Inferno Mode Resource):**
- Max: 100
- Generated by: Normal Attacks hitting targets, Intro Skill, Standard Defense Protocol (Resonance Skill), Pinpoint Strike (parry follow-up)
- **Cannot be generated while in Inferno Mode**
- When full (100), Resonance Skill transforms into enhanced **Crisis Response Protocol** (Targeted Action / Forcible Riposte)

**Blaze (Primary Burst Resource):**
- Max: 100 (outside Inferno Mode); **150 (inside Inferno Mode)**
- Generated by:
  - Entering Inferno Mode (Resonance Liberation Rekindle): +50 Blaze
  - Casting Targeted Action or Forcible Riposte (Enhanced Skill): +10 Blaze
  - **Every stack of Heliacal Ember converted from Spectro Frazzle: +5 Blaze**
- Consumed by: Heavy Slash attacks during Inferno Mode (Daybreak 10, Dawning 20, Nightfall up to 40, Lightsmash 20)

**Heliacal Ember (Spectro Frazzle Conversion Mechanic):**
- Max: 60 stacks
- Duration: 6 seconds per stack
- **Passive Conversion:** When Zani is in the team and a nearby Resonator inflicts Spectro Frazzle on a target, Zani **immediately consumes all Spectro Frazzle stacks**, triggers the corresponding Spectro Frazzle DMG, and converts them into an equal number of Heliacal Embers
- **Each Heliacal Ember grants Zani +5 Blaze**
- Heliacal Ember stacks count toward Spectro Frazzle stacks for **Eternal Radiance Echo Set** activation purposes
- **Critical Note:** Heliacal Ember does NOT benefit from Spectro Frazzle-specific buffs (e.g., Phoebe's Confession mode Frazzle depletion extension); exception is that it triggers Eternal Radiance set bonuses

**Sunburst Mode (Combat Buff State):**
- Duration: 14 seconds
- Triggered by: Casting Targeted Action or Forcible Riposte
- Effect: **Spectro Frazzle DMG dealt by Zani is Amplified by +20%**

**Inferno Mode (Ultimate Stance):**
- Duration: 20 seconds (or until Blaze is depleted / The Last Stand is cast)
- Grants access to: Heavy Slash attack chain (Daybreak → Dawning → Nightfall), Enhanced parry skill (Scorching Light), Basic Attack DMG Multiplier +25%
- All attacks during Inferno Mode are considered **both Heavy Attack DMG and Spectro Frazzle DMG simultaneously**
- Ends when: The Last Stand (second Liberation) is cast OR 20 seconds elapse OR Zani swaps out

## Zani: Character Kit: Basic Attack — Routine Negotiation

**Basic Attack (4-Stage Chain):**
Perform up to 4 consecutive attacks dealing Spectro DMG. After Basic Attack Stage 3, press Normal Attack at the right time to perform **Basic Attack Breakthrough**, which can be followed by Stage 4 by pressing Normal Attack again. Stage 4 can also be triggered by dodging and pressing Normal Attack within a certain time after Stage 3.

**Heavy Attack:**
Consume STA to attack the target, dealing Spectro DMG. Press Normal Attack within a certain time to perform Basic Attack Stage 3.

**Mid-air Attack:**
Consume STA to perform a plunging attack, dealing Spectro DMG. Press Normal Attack within a certain time to perform Basic Attack Stage 3.

**Dodge Counter:**
Press Normal Attack after successful Dodge to attack the target, dealing Spectro DMG. Then press Normal Attack at the right time to cast Basic Attack Breakthrough. If Dodge Counter is not followed by Stage 4, press Normal Attack on the ground within a certain time to cast Stage 4.

## Zani: Character Kit: Resonance Skill — Restless Watch

Zani's Resonance Skill has **two forms** depending on her current Redundant Energy status:

**Standard Defense Protocol (Default, <100 Redundant Energy):**
- Attack the target to deal Spectro DMG and enter a **block stance** for up to 2 seconds
- This state ends early if Zani is switched off field
- Press Normal Attack within a certain time to perform Basic Attack Stage 3, recovering 10 points of Redundant Energy and Stagnating the target on hit
- **Parry Mechanic:** When attacked by an enemy in the block stance, **reduce damage by 100%** (perfect block) and Stagnate nearby targets, then cast **Resonance Skill Pinpoint Strike** dealing Spectro DMG and reducing enemy Vibration Strength by 5%
- **Damage Reduction After Parry:** DMG taken by Zani is reduced by 30% for the next 2 seconds

**Crisis Response Protocol (Enhanced, 100 Redundant Energy):**
- Hold Resonance Skill to enter **Ready Stance** (up to 2s duration; Zani is **immune to interruption** during this stance)
- This state ends early if Zani is switched off field
- **Release Method 1 (Manual):** After releasing Resonance Skill button or when stance duration ends, consume all Redundant Energy to cast **Targeted Action**, dealing Spectro DMG
- **Release Method 2 (Counterattack):** When attacked by an enemy in Ready Stance, **reduce damage by 100%** and consume all Redundant Energy to cast **Forcible Riposte**, dealing Spectro DMG, Stagnating the target, and reducing Vibration Strength by 5%; DMG taken reduced by 30% for next 2s
- **Both Targeted Action and Forcible Riposte:**
  - Inflict 1 stack of **Heliacal Ember** on the target on hit
  - Recover **10 Blaze**
  - Enter **Sunburst mode** (Spectro Frazzle DMG Amplified by +20% for 14s)

**Cooldown:** 5 seconds
**Concerto Regen:** 5 (Standard Protocol), 10 (Targeted Action / Forcible Riposte), 5 (Pinpoint Strike)

## Zani: Character Kit: Forte Circuit — There Will Be A Light

**Heliacal Ember (Core Mechanic):**
When Zani is in the team and a nearby Resonator inflicts Spectro Frazzle upon a target:
- Immediately **consume all Spectro Frazzle stacks**
- Trigger the corresponding Spectro Frazzle DMG
- Convert Spectro Frazzle into **equal number of Heliacal Embers**
- Every conversion grants Zani **Blaze based on stacks** (5 Blaze per Heliacal Ember)
- Max Heliacal Ember: **60 stacks**
- Duration: 6 seconds per stack
- Heliacal Ember stacks count toward Spectro Frazzle stacks for **Eternal Radiance Sonata Effect**

**Scorching Light (Inferno Mode Heavy Slash Chain):**
When Zani is in Inferno Mode, **Daybreak, Dawning, Nightfall,** and **Lightsmash** become available. All deal Spectro DMG considered **both Heavy Attack DMG and Spectro Frazzle DMG simultaneously**.

**Heavy Slash - Daybreak (1st Attack):**
- Replaces Basic Attack when Blaze > 30
- After entering Inferno Mode, casting Basic Attack immediately triggers Resonance Skill Scorching Light
- Hold Basic Attack to enter Ready Stance
- Consumes **10 Blaze**

**Heavy Slash - Dawning (2nd Attack):**
- After performing Daybreak, Basic Attack is replaced with Dawning when Zani has > 30 Blazes
- Consumes **20 Blaze**

**Heavy Slash - Nightfall (3rd Attack / Primary Damage Source):**
- After performing Dawning, Basic Attack is replaced with Nightfall
- Consumes **up to 40 Blaze** on hit
- **Each Blaze consumed increases Nightfall's DMG Multiplier by +5% per Blaze** (max +200% at 40 Blaze consumed)
- This is Zani's highest damage skill

**Heavy Slash - Lightsmash (Parry Counter Alternative):**
- When Blaze > 30, Resonance Skill Standard Defense Protocol is replaced with Scorching Light
- Hold Resonance Skill to enter Ready Stance (immune to interruption; ends early if switched out)
- Release button: consume Blaze and perform Daybreak
- **Parry Trigger:** When attacked during Ready Stance, reduce damage by 100%, Stagnate nearby targets, perform **Lightsmash** (consumes 20 Blaze), reduce Vibration Strength by 10%, reduce DMG taken by 30% for next 2s
- After Lightsmash, Basic Attack is replaced with **Nightfall** (consuming up to 40 Blazes)
- When Ready Stance ends, immediately perform Nightfall
- If Dodge Counter is successful and Basic Attack is not replaced with Nightfall, press Normal Attack within a certain time to perform Lightsmash at the cost of Blazes

## Zani: Character Kit: Outro Skill — Beacon For the Future

## Zani: Resonance Chains (Sequence Nodes S1 - S6)

**S1: Enhanced Combat Buffs**
- Casting Targeted Action or Forcible Riposte gives **+50% Spectro DMG Bonus** for 14 seconds
- **Immune to interruptions while casting Heavy Slash - Nightfall**
- **Priority:** Very High — the +50% Spectro DMG boost is enormous; interruption immunity on Nightfall removes her biggest vulnerability during burst windows

**S2: Parry Damage and CRIT Rate Boost**
- **CRIT Rate is increased by +20%**
- DMG Multiplier of Targeted Action and Forcible Riposte is increased by **+80%**
- **Priority:** Highest single value node — the flat +20% CRIT Rate is gamechanging for stat optimization; massively simplifies gearing by reducing CRIT Rate requirements

**S3: The Last Stand Scaling with Blaze Consumed**
- When in Inferno Mode, **every 1 Blaze consumed increases the DMG Multiplier of The Last Stand by +8%**, maxed at **+1200%**
- **Priority:** Very High — assuming 150 Blaze consumed per rotation, this adds +1200% DMG to The Last Stand (multiplicative with all other bonuses); transforms the finisher into a genuinely massive nuke

**S4: Team ATK Buff**
- When Intro Skill Immediate Execution is cast, **ATK of all Resonators in the team is increased by +20%** for 30 seconds
- **Priority:** High — 30-second duration covers full rotation for entire team; makes Zani a team-wide buffer in addition to her personal DPS role

**S5: Rekindle Damage Increase**
- The DMG Multiplier of Resonance Liberation Rekindle is increased by **+120%**
- **Priority:** Moderate — Rekindle is entry damage, not a primary damage source; +120% is substantial but The Last Stand damage (S3) is more impactful

**S6: Heavy Slash Multiplier Boost + Blaze Restoration + Fatal Blow Protection**
- The DMG Multipliers of Heavy Slash - Daybreak, Dawning, Nightfall, and Lightsmash are increased by **+40%**
- **Every Blaze consumed increases the DMG Multiplier of Heavy Slash - Nightfall by +40%** on hit (stacks with base +5% per Blaze = +45% total per Blaze consumed; max +1800% at 40 Blaze)
- Gain following effects when in Inferno Mode:
  - When Blaze is lower than 70, **restore 70 Blazes immediately** (triggered once per Inferno Mode)
  - Within 8s after entering Inferno Mode, Zani will **remain standing with at least 1 HP if hit by a fatal blow** (immortality buff)
- **Priority:** Theoretically very high (+60–80% theoretical DPS improvement) but rotation length extension reduces practical uptime of existing buffs, making real improvement closer to +50–55%; S6 Zani is exceptionally powerful for speedclears and whale showcases

## Zani: Character's Strengths and Weaknesses in Actual Gameplay

**Strengths:**
1. **Very High Baseline Cleave Damage:** Zani's Heavy Slash chain has exceptional AoE coverage; handles clumps of enemies extremely well
2. **Optional Swap Cancel Windows:** During Inferno Mode, all Heavy Slash animations can be swap-canceled for quickswap optimization (advanced players can significantly boost team DPS)
3. **Easy to Use and Tanky:** Built-in damage reduction (40% in Ready Stance, 30% for 2s after parry, 100% damage block on successful parry), multiple parry windows, high DEF stat; survives situations that would kill most DPS characters
4. **Dual Damage Type Synergy:** Inferno Mode attacks count as **both Spectro Frazzle and Heavy Attack DMG**; can benefit from buffs to either category; flexible team-building with Heavy Attack buffers (Mortefi, Phrolova, Iuno) or Spectro Frazzle buffers (Phoebe, Spectro Rover)
5. **Parry Mechanic Rewards Skill:** Successful parries grant 100% damage block, Stagnate enemies, deplete Vibration bar faster (5–10% Vibration Strength reduction), and enable counterattacks with no Blaze cost; skilled players can optimize rotations around parry timings
6. **Looks Exceptionally Cool:** Her Inferno Mode transformation, flaming sword animations, and ram-themed design are visually striking

**Weaknesses:**
1. **Expensive to Access Maximum Potential:** Zani **heavily prefers** Phoebe (S0 minimum) for consistent Spectro Frazzle application; without Phoebe, Zani loses ~30% team DPS and cannot execute full combos; additionally, both Zani's signature (Blazing Justice) and Phoebe's signature weapons contribute ~15–20% damage via Spectro Frazzle DMG Amplification — in short, optimal Zani requires 4★ pity (Zani), 5★ pity (Phoebe), and ideally 2× weapon banners (Blazing Justice + Phoebe weapon)
2. **Team Flexibility is Extremely Limited:** Zani is **locked into Spectro Frazzle teams** to function; currently only Phoebe, Spectro Rover, and Ciaccona can apply Spectro Frazzle, and only Phoebe does so adequately; this restriction severely limits team-building creativity and forces specific compositions
3. **Rotation is Lengthy:** Zani's Inferno Mode requires 20–25 seconds on-field to execute full combo; combined with Phoebe's 10–12s setup, total rotation cycles are 45–50 seconds — much longer than quickswap teams or characters with <15s field time
4. **Energy Regen Requirements:** Needs 115–120% Energy Regen to maintain Liberation uptime; requires 1–2 substat rolls on multiple Echo pieces, potentially sacrificing CRIT/ATK optimization
5. **Counter-Intuitive Parry Timing:** Zani's parry windows are generous (up to 2 seconds in Ready Stance), but some enemy attacks have delayed hit detection or multi-hit sequences that can bypass the block and interrupt her combos; requires fight-specific knowledge
6. **Heliacal Ember Doesn't Benefit from All Spectro Frazzle Buffs:** While Heliacal Ember counts for Echo set activation, it does NOT benefit from certain Spectro Frazzle-specific mechanics (e.g., Phoebe Confession mode's Frazzle depletion extension, Absolution Phoebe's Heavy Attack Starflash Amplification trigger) — this creates confusing interactions and limits certain synergies
