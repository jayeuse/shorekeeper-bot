## Ciaccona: Combat Archetype and Role

- **Element/Attribute:** Aero
- **Weapon Type:** Pistols
- **Role:** Sub-DPS / Support — provides Aero DMG amplification, applies Aero Erosion and Spectro Frazzle, deals consistent off-field damage via Ensemble Sylphs
- **Scaling Stat:** ATK (primary)
- **Damage Type:** Resonance Skill DMG (majority of her damage output comes from Ensemble Sylphs, which are classified as Resonance Skill DMG)
- **Combat Style:** Summoner/Buffer hybrid — deploys Ensemble Sylph clones that attack independently, applies Aero Erosion to amplify team damage, maintains buffs through quick rotations
- **Specialty:**
  - **Solo Concert:** 24% Aero DMG Bonus for all team members for 30 seconds (one of the highest Aero buffs in the game)
  - **Aero Erosion:** Increases Aero DMG taken by enemies by 8% per stack, max 3 stacks (24% total amplification)
  - **Spectro Frazzle:** Can apply Spectro Frazzle via green Tonics for mixed-element team support
  - **Ensemble Sylphs:** Summons up to 2 independent Aero clones that deal coordinated off-field damage

## Ciaccona: Forte Circuit Overview (Musical Essence, Ensemble Sylphs, Recital System)

Ciaccona's Forte Circuit manages three interconnected systems.

**1. Musical Essence (Resource Bar — Max 3 stacks):**

- Generated by: Basic Attack Stage 4 (1 stack), Resonance Skill – Ensemble Sylph: Serenade (1 stack), Intro Skill (1 stack)
- Consumed by: Heavy Attack – Quadruple Downbeat (requires 3 Musical Essence to cast)
- Visual cue: Displayed as musical note icons above Ciaccona's health bar
- Design note: Musical Essence gates access to Quadruple Downbeat, which is Ciaccona's highest single-hit damage ability and triggers her Recital system

**2. Ensemble Sylphs (Summon System — Max 2 active simultaneously):**

- Summoned by: Resonance Skill – Ensemble Sylph: Serenade (1 Sylph per cast)
- Duration: Each Sylph lasts 30 seconds
- Behavior: Sylphs attack independently at fixed intervals (~2 seconds between attacks). Each attack deals Aero DMG classified as Resonance Skill DMG. Sylphs coordinate with Ciaccona's attacks — when Ciaccona uses Basic Attack Stage 4 or Resonance Skill, all active Sylphs immediately execute an additional attack.
- Max cap: 2 Sylphs active simultaneously. Casting Resonance Skill when 2 Sylphs are already active replaces the oldest Sylph.
- Design note: Ensemble Sylphs are Ciaccona's primary damage source and provide consistent off-field pressure

**3. Recital System (Heavy Attack — Tonic Colors):**
When Ciaccona casts Heavy Attack – Quadruple Downbeat (consuming 3 Musical Essence), she enters **Recital** and fires a powerful shot. The shot's effect depends on input:

- **Yellow Tonic (no directional input):** Deals Aero DMG. Applies 1 stack of **Spectro Frazzle** to hit enemies. Commands all Ensemble Sylphs to execute an additional attack.
- **Green Tonic (with directional input):** Deals Aero DMG. Applies 1 stack of **Aero Erosion** to hit enemies (increases Aero DMG taken by 8%, stacks up to 3× = 24%). Commands all Ensemble Sylphs to execute an additional attack.
- **Aero Erosion:** The most important application for Aero-focused teams. Max 3 stacks = 24% increased Aero DMG taken by enemies. Duration: 15 seconds per stack.
- **Spectro Frazzle:** Useful for mixed-element teams, particularly those using Spectro DPS (like Jinhsi, Xiangli Yao). Duration: 15 seconds per stack.

## Ciaccona: Inherent Passives

**Inherent Skill 1 – Harmonic Resonance:**
When Ciaccona's Ensemble Sylphs attack, they restore **5 Resonance Energy** to Ciaccona. This effect can trigger once every 2 seconds.

- This passive ensures Ciaccona generates her own Resonance Energy consistently without needing Energy Regen substats, enabling frequent Liberation casts.
- With 2 active Sylphs attacking every 2 seconds, Ciaccona gains approximately 5 Energy per 2-second window = 2.5 Energy per second = 75 Energy per 30-second rotation (more than half her Liberation cost).

**Inherent Skill 2 – Melodic Amplification:**
Exact wording not fully confirmed in available sources. Community consensus suggests this passive enhances either Musical Essence generation, Sylph attack frequency, or Aero DMG output. Consult in-game for precise details.

## Ciaccona: Basic Attack (Windborne Verse)

Perform up to 4 consecutive attacks with dual pistols, dealing Aero DMG.

- **Stage 1:** Single pistol shot, Aero DMG
- **Stage 2:** Dual pistol volley, Aero DMG
- **Stage 3:** Spinning shot, Aero DMG
- **Stage 4:** Powerful finishing shot, Aero DMG — **grants 1 Musical Essence on hit**. Commands all active Ensemble Sylphs to immediately execute an additional attack.

**Heavy Attack – Quadruple Downbeat (requires 3 Musical Essence):**
When Ciaccona has 3 Musical Essence, her Heavy Attack is replaced with Quadruple Downbeat. Consume all 3 Musical Essence to fire a powerful charged shot dealing Aero DMG. Enter **Recital** — the shot's effect depends on input:

- **Yellow Tonic (no directional input):** Applies 1 stack of Spectro Frazzle to enemies hit
- **Green Tonic (with directional input):** Applies 1 stack of Aero Erosion to enemies hit
  Both versions command all Ensemble Sylphs to execute an additional attack.

**Mid-Air Attack:** Consume STA to perform a Plunging Attack, dealing Aero DMG.

**Dodge Counter:** Press Normal Attack after a successful Dodge to deliver a counter shot, dealing Aero DMG.

## Ciaccona: Resonance Liberation (Solo Concert)

- **Cost:** 125 Resonance Energy
- **Effect:** Ciaccona performs a powerful musical attack, dealing Aero DMG to all enemies in an area. Grants all team members (including Ciaccona) **24% Aero DMG Bonus** for **30 seconds**. Commands all active Ensemble Sylphs to immediately execute coordinated attacks.

This is Ciaccona's primary team support ability — the 24% Aero DMG Bonus applies to all team members and lasts 30 seconds, which typically covers an entire DPS rotation. It stacks additively with other Aero DMG sources (echoes, weapons, buffs).

## Ciaccona: Resonance Chains (Sequence Nodes S1–S6)

## Ciaccona: Resonance Chain S1 (Prelude to Freedom)

When Ciaccona casts Resonance Skill – Ensemble Sylph: Serenade, she gains **18% ATK** for **20 seconds**. This effect stacks up to **2 times** (max 36% ATK).

_Impact: Straightforward ATK buff that stacks quickly (Resonance Skill has ~8s cooldown, so maintaining 2 stacks is easy). Approximately +10–12% personal DPS increase. Recommended first purchase from Coral Shop._

## Ciaccona: Resonance Chain S2 (Harmonic Interlude)

When Ciaccona's Ensemble Sylphs attack, they deal **40% increased damage**. Additionally, the **Aero Erosion** effect applied by Ciaccona's Green Tonic increases to **12% per stack** (up from 8%), max 3 stacks = **36% total** (up from 24%).

_Impact: Massive buff to both personal DPS and team support. The +40% Sylph damage increases Ciaccona's own output substantially (Sylphs are her primary damage source). The Aero Erosion increase from 24% → 36% is a huge team-wide amplification buff. This is Ciaccona's highest-value sequence node. Approximately +25–30% personal DPS increase + significant team DPS increase._

## Ciaccona: Resonance Chain S3 (Crescendo of the Wind)

The DMG Multiplier of Resonance Liberation – Solo Concert is increased by **80%**. When Solo Concert is cast, restore **15 Musical Essence** to Ciaccona (capping at max 3).

_Impact: +80% Liberation multiplier is substantial, though Liberation is not Ciaccona's primary damage source. The 15 Musical Essence restoration (effectively instant access to Quadruple Downbeat post-Liberation) is the more valuable effect — accelerates rotation fluidity. Approximately +15–18% personal DPS increase._

## Ciaccona: Resonance Chain S4 (Serenade of Unity)

When Ciaccona applies Aero Erosion or Spectro Frazzle via Quadruple Downbeat, all team members gain **12% ATK** for **20 seconds**.

_Impact: Team-wide ATK buff that triggers frequently (every Quadruple Downbeat cast). Primarily team utility rather than personal DPS. Moderate value in coordinated teams. Approximately +5–8% team DPS increase._

## Ciaccona: Resonance Chain S5 (Adagio of the Wanderer)

Minor improvements to Ciaccona's passive stats and Resonance Liberation effects. Exact wording not fully confirmed in available sources.

_Impact: Lowest-value node by community consensus. Skip or treat as incidental acquisition on the way to S6._

## Ciaccona: Resonance Chain S6 (Finale of the Free Spirit)

The DMG Multiplier of Ensemble Sylphs is increased by **70%**. When Ciaccona casts Resonance Liberation – Solo Concert, summon **2 additional Ensemble Sylphs** (these do not count toward the 2-Sylph cap and last 15 seconds).

_Impact: +70% Sylph multiplier is enormous (Sylphs are Ciaccona's primary damage source). The 2 additional temporary Sylphs from Liberation create a burst window with up to 4 Sylphs active simultaneously (2 permanent + 2 temporary = quadruple coordinated attacks). This changes the optimal rotation substantially. Approximately +50–60% personal DPS increase from S5. S6 Ciaccona becomes a genuine Sub-DPS powerhouse rather than pure support._

**Resonance Chain Priority:** S2 > S3 > S1 > S6 >> S4 > S5

## Ciaccona: Team Composition Guide

## Ciaccona: Best Team (Ciaccona + Cartethyia + Rover)

**Role assignment:** Cartethyia (Main DPS) | Ciaccona (Sub-DPS / Aero Buffer) | Rover (Flex Support/Sub-DPS)

**Why this team:** Ciaccona provides Cartethyia with 24% Aero DMG Bonus (Solo Concert) + 24% Aero Erosion (S0; 36% at S2+) + consistent off-field damage via Ensemble Sylphs. Cartethyia is an Aero DPS who benefits maximally from Aero amplification. Rover provides additional damage and utility. This is Ciaccona's optimal team per Prydwen and community guides.

**Team Rotation — Best Team (Ciaccona + Cartethyia + Rover):**

1. Start with **Ciaccona** → Intro Skill (1 Musical Essence) → Resonance Skill twice (2 Ensemble Sylphs summoned, 3 total Musical Essence)
2. Cast Heavy Attack – Quadruple Downbeat with directional input (Green Tonic) → Apply 1 stack Aero Erosion
3. Basic Attack Stage 4 (grants Musical Essence) → Resonance Skill (refresh Sylph) → Quadruple Downbeat (2nd Aero Erosion stack)
4. Cast Resonance Liberation – Solo Concert (24% Aero DMG Bonus to team for 30s) → Quadruple Downbeat (3rd Aero Erosion stack = 24% total at S0)
5. **Outro** → **Cartethyia** enters with 24% Aero DMG Bonus + 24% Aero Erosion on enemies + Ciaccona's Sylphs dealing off-field damage
6. **Cartethyia** executes full DPS rotation (all attacks amplified by Ciaccona's buffs)
7. Switch to **Rover** for additional support/Sub-DPS contribution
8. Repeat cycle

## Ciaccona: Strengths and Weaknesses

## Ciaccona: Strengths

- **Best-in-class Aero support:** 24% Aero DMG Bonus (Solo Concert) + 24% Aero Erosion (S0; 36% at S2+) provides the highest total Aero amplification package in the game as of Version 2.6
- **Consistent off-field damage:** Ensemble Sylphs deal damage automatically for 30 seconds, providing persistent pressure without requiring Ciaccona to stay on-field
- **Flexible application:** Can apply either Aero Erosion (for Aero teams) or Spectro Frazzle (for Spectro teams) depending on Tonic choice
- **Self-sufficient Energy generation:** Harmonic Resonance passive generates ~75 Energy per 30s rotation without requiring Energy Regen substats
- **Easy, forgiving rotation:** Ciaccona's kit is straightforward — summon Sylphs, apply Aero Erosion, cast Liberation, swap out. No complex resource juggling or tight timing windows.
- **Long buff durations:** Solo Concert's 24% Aero DMG Bonus lasts 30 seconds, covering entire DPS rotations comfortably
- **Strong Signature weapon synergy:** Woodland Aria is tailor-made for her kit, providing massive ATK% + Aero DMG Bonus + Aero RES reduction all triggered naturally

## Ciaccona: Weaknesses

- **Primarily benefits Aero teams:** Her strongest buffs (Aero DMG Bonus, Aero Erosion) only apply to Aero damage. Non-Aero teams cannot leverage her full support potential (though Spectro Frazzle provides some flexibility).
- **Limited personal damage at S0:** Without constellations, Ciaccona's own damage contribution is modest. She is a buffer first, Sub-DPS second. S2 and S6 significantly improve her personal damage.
- **Sylphs have limited AI:** Ensemble Sylphs attack automatically but cannot be manually controlled. They may target enemies that are already dead or waste attacks on low-priority targets.
- **Buff snapshotting:** Solo Concert's 24% Aero DMG Bonus applies when cast but does not dynamically update if Ciaccona swaps out early. Timing matters.
- **Quadruple Downbeat requires precise input:** Green Tonic (Aero Erosion) requires directional input during Heavy Attack; Yellow Tonic (Spectro Frazzle) requires no input. Players must be intentional about which they're applying.
- **Lower personal survivability:** Ciaccona has no self-healing, shielding, or damage reduction abilities. She relies on team healers/shielders for sustain.
- **Rotation downtime if Sylphs expire:** If both Sylphs expire simultaneously and Resonance Skill is on cooldown, Ciaccona loses her primary damage source temporarily
