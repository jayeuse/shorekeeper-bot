# Augusta Kit Documentation
<!-- Sources: https://www.prydwen.gg/wuthering-waves/characters/augusta/, https://game8.co/games/Wuthering-Waves/archives/524890, https://wuthering.gg/characters/augusta, https://wutheringwaves.fandom.com/wiki/Augusta -->

## Augusta: Combat Archetype and Role
- **Element/Attribute:** Electro
- **Weapon Type:** Broadblade
- **Role:** Main DPS — aggressive Heavy Attack-focused hypercarry; activates a time-stopping second Liberation stance (Sworn Allegiance) that halts all timers and allows an uninterrupted 9-hit + finisher burst rotation; generates and distributes Shields to the full team through her Intro Skill and Ruler's Realm mechanic; provides a 15% DMG Amplification Outro
- **Scaling:** ATK (standard ATK-based damage; Heavy Attack DMG Bonus is the primary damage type multiplier)
- **Damage Profile:** Heavy Attack DMG constitutes the majority of her output; Basic Attack chains and Resonance Skill generate the resources needed to reach her Liberation finisher

## Augusta: Key Resources (Forte Mechanics Overview)

Augusta manages three overlapping resource systems simultaneously. **Prowess** (0–100) is a purple bar generated by almost every attack and fully restored by her Intro Skill; reaching full Prowess upgrades her Heavy Attack and Dodge Counter into their enhanced Thunderoar forms. **Ascendancy** (0–100) is a gold circle built through Normal Attacks, Intro Skill, Resonance Skill, and Resonance Liberation; a full Ascendancy bar upgrades her Resonance Skill from Warrior's Blade into the three-part Undying Sunlight: Strike → Leap → Plunge chain, with the Plunge being her primary Majesty stack generator. **Majesty** (0–2 stacks) is a gold bar charged through the Undying Sunlight: Plunge and through allies casting Outro Skills under Augusta's Outro effect; reaching 2 stacks enables her second, time-stopping Liberation.

A fourth buffer, **Crown of Wills**, is activated through the Outro Skill — discussed in the Outro section. Together these systems create a loop: Normal Attacks → build Prowess + Ascendancy → enhanced Heavy Attacks and Undying Sunlight Plunge → build Majesty → time-stop Liberation → Ruler's Realm → team Shields + Crown of Wills.

## Augusta Forte Circuit: Prowess / Ascendancy / Majesty

**Prowess (0–100):**
- Generated by: Basic Attack, Heavy Attack — Steelclash, Mid-air Attack, Mid-air Dodge Counter, Resonance Skill — Warrior's Blade, Dodge Counter, Dodge Counter — Heavy Attack: Steelclash
- Fully restored on: Intro Skill — Stride of Goldenflare cast
- At 100 Prowess:
  - Heavy Attack — Steelclash → replaced by **Heavy Attack — Thunderoar: Backstep** (27.00%; consuming all Prowess)
  - Thunderoar: Backstep can chain into **Heavy Attack — Thunderoar: Spinslash** (71.28%×3; 20 STA cost) — the primary sustained Heavy Attack damage dealer
  - Jump during Prowess full → **Heavy Attack — Thunderoar: Uppercut** (90.00%×2; 20 STA cost) — burst single-hit option
  - Dodge Counter → **Dodge Counter — Heavy Attack: Steelclash** (23.34%×3; Heavy Attack DMG tag; 20 STA)
  - When Ascendancy is also full, Dodge Counter → **Dodge Counter — Thunderoar: Backstep** (27.00%; Heavy Attack DMG), which chains to Spinslash

**Ascendancy (0–100):**
- Generated by: Normal Attacks (ongoing)
- Restored by: Intro Skill → +20%; Resonance Skill → +10%; Resonance Liberation — Sword of Eternal Oath → +40%
- At 100 Ascendancy, Resonance Skill — Warrior's Blade is replaced by the enhanced **Undying Sunlight** chain:
  - **Strike:** 70.00%×2 Electro DMG (10 STA); press again → Leap
  - **Leap:** 112.00% + 14.00%×2 Electro DMG (10 STA); press again → Plunge
  - **Plunge:** 43.55% + 391.95% Electro DMG (considered Heavy Attack DMG); grants **1 stack of Majesty**; Concerto Regen: 7

**Majesty (0–2 stacks):**
- 1 stack gained from: Resonance Skill — Undying Sunlight: Plunge
- 1 stack gained from: Other Resonators casting Outro Skill while under Augusta's Outro Skill effect (Battlesong of the Unyielding)
- At 2 stacks: Hold Resonance Liberation to cast **Resonance Liberation — Sublime is the Sun** (time-stop Liberation, no energy cost — costs 2 Majesty stacks instead)

## Augusta: Character Kit: Resonance Skill — Warrior's Blade / Undying Sunlight

**Resonance Skill — Warrior's Blade** (when Ascendancy < 100):
- Augusta leaps and slams down her Broadblade, dealing Electro DMG
- Multiplier: 110.00%×3
- Concerto Regen: 10; Cooldown: 15s
- Each cast restores +10% Ascendancy

**Resonance Skill — Undying Sunlight: Strike** (when Ascendancy = 100):
- Replaces Warrior's Blade; 70.00%×2 Electro DMG; 10 STA
- Leads into Leap → Plunge chain (can only activate chain while airborne after Leap)

**Resonance Skill — Undying Sunlight: Leap:**
- 112.00% + 14.00%×2 Electro DMG; 10 STA; mid-air only

**Resonance Skill — Undying Sunlight: Plunge:**
- 43.55% + 391.95% Electro DMG; **Heavy Attack DMG tag**; Concerto Regen: 7
- **Grants 1 stack of Majesty** — the primary means of building toward the time-stop Liberation

## Augusta: Character Kit: Resonance Liberation — Sunward Conquest

**Resonance Liberation — Sword of Eternal Oath** (press-and-release; costs 125 Resonance Energy):
- Augusta sweeps her Broadblade forward; DMG is Heavy Attack DMG
- Multiplier: 16.59%×2 + 66.36%×3 + 16.59%×2 + 287.56%
- Concerto Regen: 20; Cooldown: 25s
- Restores +40% Ascendancy on cast

**Resonance Liberation — Sublime is the Sun** (hold; requires 2 Majesty stacks; no Resonance Energy cost):
- Augusta generates the **Ruler's Realm** and enters **Sworn Allegiance** state for **7 seconds**
- Time is completely stopped during Sworn Allegiance — Resonator switching is disabled; only Sunborne, Everbright Protector, and Dodge are available
- **Ruler's Realm lasts 30s:** while active, Resonators who cast Intro Skill gain a Shield equal to **650 + 5% of Augusta's Max HP** for 10s (unstackable per character)
- In Sworn Allegiance: press/hold Normal Attack to cast **Sunborne** (60.00% Heavy Attack DMG) up to 9 times, then Normal Attack/Liberation to cast **Everbright Protector** to end the stance

**Sublime is the Sun — Everbright Protector** (finisher):
- DMG: 120.00% + 450.00% + 3.00%×10 (Heavy Attack DMG)
- Ends Sworn Allegiance; all Resonators exit the field; consumes all Crown of Wills stacks
- Can also be triggered early by holding Liberation during Sworn Allegiance

**Note:** Performing any environmental, gameplay, or other interaction ends Sworn Allegiance without triggering Everbright Protector — be careful during time-stop state.

## Augusta: Inherent Passives

**Glory's Favor**
- Whenever Augusta deals damage, she gains a Shield equal to **350 + 2.5% of her Max HP** for 5s
- Triggered at most once every 0.5s; unstackable; Shield does not transfer to incoming Resonators
- This perpetual micro-shielding is the kit engine for the Crown of Valor Echo set, her signature weapon passive, and S1 Crown of Wills generation — maintaining the shield is essential to her full damage output

**Blazing Valor**
- When Augusta has been out of combat for over 4s, once every 4s:
  - If Majesty < 1 stack, restore 1 stack of Majesty
  - Fully restore Crown of Wills
- This out-of-combat recovery ensures she can enter a new fight already partially resourced, improving rotation consistency across multi-wave content

## Augusta: Intro/Outro Skills

**Intro Skill — Stride of Goldenflare**
- DMG: 50.00%×2 Electro DMG; Concerto Regen: 10
- On cast: **fully restores Prowess to 100**
- On cast: restores **20% Ascendancy**
- If S1 is active: also grants 1 stack of Crown of Wills
- This makes Intro Skill the strongest single-button resource reset in Augusta's kit — entering via Intro is the ideal rotation opener

**Outro Skill — Battlesong of the Unyielding**
- The next Resonator switched onto the field gains for **14s** (ends on switch-out):
  - **15% DMG Amplification** for all Attributes
  - Casting *their* Outro Skill grants Augusta **1 stack of Majesty** and **1 stack of Crown of Wills**
- **Crown of Wills:** 1 stack (base) = 15% Electro DMG Bonus; cleared when Everbright Protector ends
- Augusta's Outro is a primary Majesty generation source in team rotations — pairing her with a fast Outro character accelerates her Liberation cycle significantly

## Augusta: Resonance Chains (Sequences)

**S1**
Each stack of Crown of Wills additionally increases Augusta's Crit. DMG by 15% (stacks with Crown of Wills max count increase to 2). Casting Intro Skill — Stride of Goldenflare now grants 1 stack of Crown of Wills. Resonance Skill — Undying Sunlight: Strike, Leap, and Plunge are now immune to interruption.
*Value: Strong QoL — interruption immunity on the Undying Sunlight chain is meaningful in endgame content, and the Crit. DMG per Crown of Wills stack compounds nicely with S2. Recommended stopping point for budget players.*

**S2**
Crown of Wills additionally provides: each stack increases Augusta's Crit. Rate by 20%. For every 1% of Crit. Rate over 100%, Augusta gains 2% Crit. DMG (cap: +100% Crit. DMG from this effect).
*Value: Game-changing — the strongest single Sequence Node in her chain. At 2 stacks of Crown of Wills (enabled by S1), this is +40% Crit. Rate, which then overflows into massive Crit. DMG via the overflow formula. Redefines her damage ceiling. S0→S2 is the highest-priority investment bracket.*

**S3**
The following skills' DMG Multipliers are increased by 25%: Heavy Attack — Thunderoar: Backstep, Thunderoar: Backstep (Dodge Counter), Thunderoar: Spinslash, Thunderoar: Uppercut; Resonance Skill — Undying Sunlight: Plunge; Resonance Liberation — Sublime is the Sun: Sunborne and Everbright Protector.
*Value: Substantial — 25% across her entire Heavy Attack toolkit and both Liberation phases. The marginal per-hit gain across 9 Sunborne hits + Everbright Protector makes this a meaningful upgrade in absolute DPS.*

**S4**
Casting Intro Skill — Stride of Goldenflare increases the ATK of all Resonators in the team by 20% for 30s.
*Value: Team-wide ATK buff — useful but not transformational. Less impactful than S2 and S3 in isolation; most valuable when the entire team scales ATK and the buff duration covers a full rotation.*

**S5**
The Shield provided by Inherent Skill — Glory's Favor is increased by 50%.
*Value: Moderate survivability improvement. Expands Crown of Valor and signature weapon interactions marginally. Not a priority for DPS players.*

**S6**
Augusta can now hold up to 4 stacks of Crown of Wills. For every 1% of Crit. Rate over 150%, Augusta gains 2% Crit. DMG (cap: +50% from this effect, stacking with S2's cap). When Augusta performs Thunderoar: Spinslash or Thunderoar: Uppercut, she gains 2 stacks of Crown of Wills (once per 1s via Engraved in Radiant Light). During these attacks, **Thunder Rage** triggers at the spot: 2 instances of Electro DMG, each equal to 100% of Augusta's ATK, considered Heavy Attack DMG.
*Value: Transformational for sustained combat — persistent 4-stack Crown of Wills maintenance without needing Outro cycles, continuous Thunder Rage procs during every Thunderoar rotation, and the extended overflow Crit. Rate cap. Full S6 Augusta is a top-tier sustained DPS.*

## Augusta: Recommended Echo Sets

**Best: Crown of Valor (3-pc) + Void Thunder (2-pc)**
Crown of Valor (Version 2.6 set): While a Shield is active, increases the equipped character's ATK by 24%. Casting Intro Skill or Resonance Skill increases Heavy Attack DMG by 40% for 15s. Gaining a Shield causes Heavy Attack DMG to ignore 12% of target's DEF for 7s (max 5 stacks, once per 0.5s). Also: each Shield gain increases ATK by 6% and Crit. DMG by 4% for 4s (max 5 stacks, once per 0.5s).

Since Augusta's Inherent Skill — Glory's Favor generates a Shield every 0.5s while dealing damage, she can stack Crown of Valor buffs nearly continuously. Combined with Void Thunder's 2-pc Electro DMG Bonus, this is her strongest set by a significant margin.

**Alternative: Full Void Thunder (5-pc)**
Weaker than the hybrid set but acceptable if Crown of Valor pieces have poor substats. The 5-pc bonus grants an additional Electro DMG Bonus, but the lack of ATK%, Heavy Attack DMG%, and DEF ignore from Crown of Valor represents a meaningful loss.

**Main Echo:** The False Sovereign (4-cost Electro)
The optimal main Echo for Augusta. Its passive provides both Electro DMG Bonus and Heavy Attack DMG Bonus by simply being equipped. Its Echo Skill deals Electro DMG in a forward dash with four hits (55.35%×4 at Lv. 25). Synergizes with the Ruler's Realm thematic identity.

**Alternative Main Echo:** Tempest Mephis (4-cost Electro)
Provides Electro DMG and Heavy Attack DMG after its claw attack connects. Requires time investment to activate buffs and the Echo Skill causes rotation disruption. Inferior to The False Sovereign but usable.

**Echo Main Stats:**
- 4-cost: CRIT DMG or CRIT Rate
- 3-cost #1: Electro DMG Bonus
- 3-cost #2: Electro DMG Bonus (or ATK% if unavailable)
- 1-cost ×2: ATK%

**Sub-stat Priority:** CRIT Rate > CRIT DMG > Heavy Attack DMG Bonus > ATK% > Energy Regen > Flat ATK
**Target Ratio:** ~75% CRIT Rate / 220–250% CRIT DMG (S0); higher Crit. Rate at S2 to maximize overflow formula

## Augusta: Best Weapon

**Signature — Thunderflare Dominion (5★ Broadblade)**
Augusta's purpose-built signature weapon. Provides 12.1% CRIT Rate as its main sub-stat. Passive: increases ATK by 24%; casting Intro Skill or Resonance Skill increases Heavy Attack DMG by 40% for 15s; gaining a Shield causes Heavy Attack DMG to ignore 12% of target's DEF for 7s (up to 5 stacks). The DEF-ignore stacking interacts directly with her perpetual micro-shielding from Glory's Favor — maintaining near-constant 5-stack DEF ignore in combat.

**Alternative — Verdant Summit (5★ Broadblade)**
Strong Resonance Skill and Resonance Liberation DMG focus. A competitive option when Thunderflare Dominion is unavailable; lacks the DEF ignore and Heavy Attack specific bonus but provides high base ATK and multiplicative buffs.

**Alternative — Ages of Harvest (5★ Broadblade)**
Charges up during combat for a large burst bonus. Works well in Augusta's Liberation-focused rotation but requires build-up time. Viable at R1 but below Verdant Summit in consistent output.

**Alternative — Aureate Zenith (5★ Broadblade)**
Specifically increases Heavy Attack DMG. Competitive with Verdant Summit depending on fight duration and rotation cadence. Recommended as an R5 option when farming 5-star alternatives.

**F2P Note:** Augusta's damage drops more meaningfully without a premium weapon than most characters. The DEF ignore from her signature's passive is difficult to replicate via Echo substats alone. Budget players should prioritize pulling Thunderflare Dominion alongside Augusta or plan for an extended grind on alternative options.

## Augusta: Best Teams

**S-Tier: Augusta + Iuno + Shorekeeper**
- **Augusta** (Main DPS): Leads the rotation; generates Shields, builds Majesty, executes time-stop Liberation
- **Iuno** (Sub-DPS/Healer/Buffer): Provides 50% Heavy Attack DMG bonus and up to 40% all-DMG amplification via her Full Moon Domain; her shield-generation synergy with Augusta's kit triggers Iuno's own buffs; heals and restores stamina
- **Shorekeeper** (Support): Universal top-tier support; DMG Amplification, CRIT Rate buff, and fast rotation speed covers Augusta's slight multi-wave weakness by enabling rapid Liberation cycling
*Best overall team — strong in both Tower of Adversity and Whimpering Wastes.*

**A-Tier: Augusta + Iuno + Verina**
- **Verina** (Support/Healer): Provides consistent healing and ATK% buff via Outro; less buff ceiling than Shorekeeper but excellent sustain and Concerto Regen support

**A-Tier: Augusta + Mortefi + Shorekeeper**
- **Mortefi** (Sub-DPS/Buffer): Provides heavy attack follow-up damage and Heavy Attack DMG amplification via his Resonance Liberation coordinated attack; strong synergy with Augusta's Heavy Attack-heavy rotation
*Best when Iuno is unavailable. Mortefi's follow-up attacks during Augusta's Sworn Allegiance are NOT active (time-stop freezes NPCs) — his value is in the pre-Liberation rotation.*

**A-Tier: Augusta + Iuno + Qiuyuan**
- **Qiuyuan** (Support/Sub-DPS): When his CRIT Rate reaches 65%, grants the entire team a CRIT DMG buff; Aero element also provides minor Aero-based amplification synergies. Strong option that pushes Augusta's Crit. DMG ceiling further.
*Noted by community as a top-tier variant for single-target content.*

**F2P-Friendly: Augusta + Sanhua + Baizhi**
- **Sanhua** (Sub-DPS): Fast Concerto generation and Basic Attack DMG buff via Outro; helps Augusta cycle Liberation more frequently
- **Baizhi** (Healer): Consistent healing; her Outro and coordinated healing support Augusta's survivability
*Viable for clearing high-difficulty content without premium supports; DPS gap vs. S-Tier is meaningful but manageable.*

## Augusta: DPS Benchmarks

Augusta is rated **SS-Tier** in both Tower of Adversity and Whimpering Wastes end-game modes (Game8, Patch 3.0). Prydwen rates her T0.5 in Tower of Adversity and T1 in Whimpering Wastes.

**Key performance notes:**
- Approximately half of her total rotation damage is concentrated in the Sworn Allegiance + Everbright Protector window. This backloading means mismanaging the time-stop activation (such as triggering it when the next enemy wave is ready to spawn) wastes significant damage in multi-wave content
- Sworn Allegiance prevents the next enemy wave from spawning — in Whimpering Wastes content specifically, timing the Liberation after the current wave dies (rather than before) is critical to avoiding dead time
- At S0 + Thunderflare Dominion + Iuno + Shorekeeper: competes with the highest single-target DPS benchmarks in the game as of Version 3.0

**Standard Rotation Outline:**
1. Pre-rotation: swap in Shorekeeper/Iuno for buffs and Outro → grants Augusta 1 Majesty via Outro interaction (if rotation includes this)
2. Augusta enters via Intro Skill → Prowess fully restored; 20% Ascendancy restored; Crown of Wills 1 stack
3. Basic Attack chain → building Ascendancy and Prowess rapidly
4. Resonance Skill — Undying Sunlight: Strike → Leap → Plunge (1 Majesty stack gained; 7 Concerto Regen)
5. Thunderoar: Spinslash or Backstep sequence (Heavy Attack burst damage)
6. Hold Resonance Liberation (2 Majesty) → Sworn Allegiance activates → 9× Sunborne → Everbright Protector finisher
7. Ruler's Realm active for 30s — all incoming Resonators who cast Intro Skill gain a Shield (and Crown of Valor buffs)
8. Outro Skill → next Resonator receives 15% DMG Amplification; casting their Outro grants Augusta another Majesty stack
