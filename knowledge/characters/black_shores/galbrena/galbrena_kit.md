## Galbrena: Combat Archetype and Role

- **Element:** Fusion
- **Weapon Type:** Pistols
- **Role:** Main DPS; Echo Skill DMG and Heavy Attack DMG primary damage sources
- **Archetype:** Dual-state resource manager; Sinflame accumulator (Threshold State) → Demon Hypostasis burst window; Afterflame pre-charge from team Echo Skills; Fated End combo-stack amplification
- **Introduced:** Version 2.7; banner "Fiend of Ever-burning Flame" (first half)
- **Design Inspiration:** Explicitly inspired by Devil May Cry (Dante/Vergil), including the Fated End enemy Combo counter (C → S rank, shown below enemy HP bars), acrobatic combat animations, dual-pistol fighting style, and wing-based melee attacks

Galbrena is a 5-star Fusion Pistol Main DPS whose combat design centers on a two-phase resource cycle: a preparation phase (Threshold State) and a high-damage burst phase (Demon Hypostasis). Her damage profile is split between **Echo Skill DMG** and **Heavy Attack DMG**, with both scaling paths active simultaneously during Demon Hypostasis — making her the primary beneficiary of Qiuyuan's unique Echo Skill DMG buffs. She is straightforward to execute once the resource cycle is understood, but resource management and precise rotation timing are required to extract her full damage ceiling. Multiple sources describe her as one of the strongest Fusion DPS options in Version 2.7 with appropriate teammates, and note that her main weakness is the small pool of weapons that optimize both her damage types simultaneously.

## Galbrena: Key Resources — Sinflame, Afterflame, Purging Flame

Galbrena's entire kit is organized around three interconnected resources, each serving a distinct role in the damage cycle.

**Sinflame (Purple Bar — 0 to 100 points)**
- The gate to Demon Hypostasis; generated in Threshold State only
- **Generation sources:** Basic Attack hits, Heavy Attack — Volley of Death hits, Mid-air Attack — Ashfall Barrage hits, Resonance Skill — Encroach hits, Intro Skill — Hellflare Overload hits
- When Sinflame reaches **100**: the Resonance Skill changes from Encroach to **Ascent of Malice** — the trigger for Demon Hypostasis entry
- Higher generation efficiency: Basic Attacks and Heavy Attacks are the fastest Sinflame generators; the standard Threshold fill sequence is Basic Attack chain × multiple hits until 100 is reached

**Afterflame (Red Bar — 0 to 40 points)**
- The damage amplifier for Demon Hypostasis; generated by team Echo Skill casts, not by Galbrena's own attacks
- **Generation source (only):** Any Resonator in the team (including Galbrena) casts an Echo Skill → **+8 Afterflame per cast**; same-named echoes only count once per Demon Hypostasis window; effect resets when Galbrena exits Demon Hypostasis
- **Maximum:** 40 points = 5 Echo Skill casts from differently-named echoes
- **Current team maximum:** In V2.7 optimized teams, approximately 24 Afterflame points are consistently achievable per window; the theoretical maximum of 40 requires specifically curated echo selection across the team
- **On Demon Hypostasis entry (Ascent of Malice cast):** All Afterflame is consumed; for every 1 point consumed, Galbrena's Demon Hypostasis attacks gain **+1% Fusion DMG Bonus** to Seraphic Execution, Flamewing Verdict, Hellsent Barrage, and Ravage; maximum +40% Fusion DMG Bonus at full 40 Afterflame
- All Afterflame is removed when Galbrena exits Demon Hypostasis; the window resets each cycle

**Purging Flame (Demon Hypostasis Gauge — converted from Sinflame)**
- Activated by the Ascent of Malice entry into Demon Hypostasis: all 100 points of Sinflame are converted at a 1:1 ratio into **100 Purging Flame**
- In Demon Hypostasis, every enhanced attack (Seraphic Execution, Flamewing Verdict, Hellsent Barrage, Purgatory Scourge, Ravage) consumes Purging Flame points per hit
- When Purging Flame reaches 0, Galbrena exits Demon Hypostasis and returns to Threshold State; Sinflame rebuilding begins again
- The goal of the Demon Hypostasis rotation is to spend Purging Flame as efficiently as possible through the enhanced attack chain, with Liberation cast before the gauge is depleted to maximize the 85% DMG multiplier buff duration

## Galbrena: Fated End — Inherent Passive DMG Amplification System

**Fated End** is Galbrena's innate combo-escalation mechanic, applied to enemies as a debuff rather than to Galbrena herself as a buff.

**How Fated End Works:**
- When Galbrena hits an enemy with any of the following: Intro Skill, Basic Attack, Basic Attack — Seraphic Execution, Dodge Counter, Heavy Attack — Volley of Death, Heavy Attack — Flamewing Verdict, Mid-air Attack, Hellstride (special Dodge), Resonance Skill — Encroach, Resonance Skill — Ascent of Malice, or Resonance Liberation → the enemy receives **+1 stack of Fated End**, up to **4 stacks maximum**
- **Each stack of Fated End** amplifies the DMG directly dealt by Galbrena's Normal Attack, Resonance Skill, Forte Circuit, Resonance Liberation, Intro Skill, and Outro Skill by **+5% for 5.5 seconds** — stackable to **+20% total DMG amplification** at 4 stacks
- The 5.5-second window per stack means that aggressive, varied skill use maintains near-permanent 4-stack Fated End during active combat
- The enemy Combo counter (C → S rank level shown beneath the target HP bar) is the visible expression of Fated End stack count, inspired directly by DMC's Style Rank system
- **Practical note:** Because every skill hit inflicts Fated End and every different skill applied can refresh or add stacks, Galbrena naturally accumulates and maintains 4 stacks within a few hits of entering the field; in longer fights, she essentially deals +20% DMG on all skill categories permanently

**Fated End in Short Fights**
In single-rotation encounters (bosses, challenge content), Fated End may not reach 4 stacks before Galbrena's window ends; in these cases, using Intro Skill first (which inflicts 1 stack on cast) and immediately following with Resonance Skill and Liberation gives 3 stacks within the critical window. The +15% from 3 stacks is still substantial. For prolonged fights, 4-stack Fated End is effectively permanent.

## Galbrena: Character Kit: Threshold State Attacks

**Basic Attack — Dual Execution (4-stage chain)**
- Up to 4 consecutive Fusion DMG hits; all stages generate Sinflame on hit
- The standard Basic Attack chain in Threshold State is the primary Sinflame filling mechanism; Basic Attacks have among the highest Sinflame generation efficiency per animation frame
- In Demon Hypostasis, this chain is replaced by **Basic Attack — Seraphic Execution** (see Demon Hypostasis section)

**Heavy Attack — Volley of Death**
- Fusion DMG (Stamina cost); generates Sinflame on hit; hold Normal Attack after casting to extend into **Volley of Death Stage 2**
- In Demon Hypostasis, replaced by **Heavy Attack — Flamewing Verdict**

**Mid-Air Attack — Ashfall Barrage**
- Plunging Fusion DMG from mid-air; generates Sinflame on hit
- In Demon Hypostasis, replaced by **Mid-air Attack — Hellsent Barrage**

**Dodge Counter — Blood for Blood**
- Fusion DMG after successful Dodge; generates Sinflame on counter
- In Demon Hypostasis, replaced by **Dodge Counter — Purgatory Scourge**

**Special Dodge — Hellstride**
- A dedicated special Dodge unique to Galbrena; recovers Stamina and generates Sinflame when used; the Hellstride movement is mobile enough to reposition for the next attack without breaking the Sinflame accumulation loop
- Prydwen notes Hellstride is the recommended dodge during Demon Hypostasis specifically because it avoids breaking the Purging Flame consumption chain while providing invincibility frames

## Galbrena: Character Kit: Resonance Skill

**Resonance Skill — Encroach (Threshold State, Sinflame < 100)**
- Galbrena attacks the target with a Fusion DMG hit; generates Sinflame on hit; classified as Resonance Skill DMG
- A Sinflame-generation tool rather than a primary damage skill; use it to contribute to the Sinflame gauge alongside the Basic Attack chain during the pre-Demon Hypostasis buildup phase

**Resonance Skill — Ascent of Malice (Threshold State, Sinflame = 100 — Forte Enhanced)**
- Galbrena slashes enemies with her wings, dealing Fusion DMG classified as **Heavy Attack DMG**
- **On casting Ascent of Malice:**
  - Galbrena enters **Demon Hypostasis**
  - All 100 Sinflame is converted to **100 Purging Flame** at a 1:1 ratio
  - All Afterflame is consumed; for every 1 point consumed, Galbrena's Seraphic Execution, Flamewing Verdict, Hellsent Barrage, and Ravage gain **+1% Fusion DMG Bonus** (max +40% at 40 Afterflame) — these bonuses are removed upon exiting Demon Hypostasis
  - Can be followed immediately by **Basic Attack — Seraphic Execution Stage 2** (Normal Attack input after Ascent of Malice)
- **Skill Upgrade Priority:** Second — the Afterflame-to-Fusion DMG Bonus conversion rate scales with skill level; the Heavy Attack DMG multiplier of the Ascent slash itself also scales

## Galbrena: Character Kit: Demon Hypostasis State Attacks

When Galbrena is in Demon Hypostasis, all her standard attacks are replaced by enhanced versions. Each enhanced attack consumes Purging Flame. All enhanced attacks deal **Fusion DMG classified as Echo Skill DMG** — the damage type that Qiuyuan's kit exclusively buffs.

**Basic Attack — Seraphic Execution (5-stage chain)**
- Up to 5 consecutive Fusion Echo Skill DMG hits per chain; each hit consumes Purging Flame
- **Stage 5 is immune to interruption** (with S1 equipped; see Resonance Chains)
- This is the primary Purging Flame consumption tool and the attack that fills most of Galbrena's Demon Hypostasis DPS window
- Prydwen recommends completing the full 5-stage chain before transitioning to Flamewing Verdict for maximum damage-per-Purging-Flame efficiency

**Heavy Attack — Flamewing Verdict (3-stage)**
- Up to 3 stages of wing-slash Fusion Echo Skill DMG; each hit consumes Purging Flame; classified as both Heavy Attack DMG and Echo Skill DMG
- **Stage 3 is immune to interruption** (with S1)
- Hold Normal Attack after Ascent of Malice to enter Flamewing Verdict Stage 2 directly, skipping Stage 1

**Mid-Air Attack — Hellsent Barrage**
- Aerial plunging Fusion Echo Skill DMG; consumes Purging Flame on hit
- Immune to interruption (with S1)

**Dodge Counter — Purgatory Scourge**
- Fusion Echo Skill DMG after Dodge during Demon Hypostasis; consumes Purging Flame

**Resonance Skill — Ravage (Demon Hypostasis)**
- Fusion Echo Skill DMG on cast during Demon Hypostasis; consumes Purging Flame; classified as both Resonance Skill and Echo Skill DMG

## Galbrena: Character Kit: Resonance Liberation — Hellblaze Pyre

- Galbrena unleashes a sweeping Liberation that deals **Fusion DMG classified as Echo Skill DMG**
- Galbrena's Resonance Liberation is described by Game8 as one of her strongest damaging skills in the kit
- **Key buff on cast:** Grants **+85% DMG Multiplier** to the following Demon Hypostasis attacks for **14 seconds**: Basic Attack — Seraphic Execution, Heavy Attack — Flamewing Verdict, Mid-air Attack — Hellsent Barrage, and Dodge Counter — Purgatory Scourge
  - This buff is the single largest multiplier injection in her rotation and is the reason Liberation must be cast **after** entering Demon Hypostasis, not before
  - The 14-second window covers approximately one full Purging Flame consumption cycle when executing the standard rotation
- **Eternal Hypostasis (S6 / Inherent interaction):** When the Liberation buff is active, Demon Hypostasis becomes **Eternal Hypostasis**, which retains all Demon Hypostasis effects and additionally increases the DMG Multipliers of Seraphic Execution, Flamewing Verdict, and Hellsent Barrage by a further **+60%** (S6: +120%); and the Afterflame-per-Purging conversion during the cycle grants +35% Fusion DMG Amplification
- Press Normal Attack after casting Resonance Liberation to immediately enter Basic Attack Stage 2 (or Seraphic Execution Stage 2 if in Demon Hypostasis) — the post-Liberation Normal Attack input is the standard rotation continuation shortcut
- **Skill Upgrade Priority:** First — the Liberation's own Echo Skill DMG multiplier and the +85% buff duration/magnitude both scale with skill level; this is the single most impactful upgrade

## Galbrena: Inherent Passives

**Inherent Skill 1 — Fated End (Debuff Amplification)**
- Any hit from the following skills inflicts **1 stack of Fated End** on the target: Intro Skill, Basic Attack (all stages including Seraphic Execution), Dodge Counter (all versions), Heavy Attack — Volley of Death, Heavy Attack — Flamewing Verdict, Mid-air Attack (all versions), Hellstride, Resonance Skill — Encroach, Resonance Skill — Ascent of Malice, and Resonance Liberation
- Maximum **4 stacks of Fated End** on any single target
- **Each stack** amplifies all DMG directly dealt by Galbrena's Normal Attack, Resonance Skill, Forte Circuit, Resonance Liberation, Intro Skill, and Outro Skill by **+5% for 5.5 seconds**; max +20% total at 4 stacks
- Fated End is the always-on amplification mechanic that raises all of Galbrena's skill categories simultaneously; in continuous combat, 4 stacks are maintained near-permanently from varied skill use
- **DMC reference:** The enemy Combo counter (C, B, A, S rank visible below the target's HP bar) is the in-game visualization of Fated End stack count; the Style Rank meter is a direct Devil May Cry homage

**Inherent Skill 2 — Heart of Defiance (ATK and CRIT Rate)**
- Passive ATK increase: **+1.80% ATK**
- Passive CRIT Rate increase: **+2.40% CRIT Rate**
- Unconditional stat bonuses; the CRIT Rate portion is particularly relevant for build planning — it reduces the CRIT Rate sub-stat requirement in echoes and weapons, allowing more investment in CRIT DMG; verify exact values with the Fandom Wiki (some sources cite slightly different numbers at max level)

## Galbrena: Intro/Outro Skills

**Intro Skill — Hellflare Overload**
- Triggered when Galbrena enters the field via swap with full Concerto Energy
- Deals Fusion DMG on cast; generates Sinflame on hit; inflicts Fated End on target
- **Critical rotation function:** Galbrena should always enter via Intro Skill because the Sinflame generation from the Intro hit, combined with the Fated End stack infliction, advances the rotation's first two objectives simultaneously on the first frame
- The standard opening sequence: Intro Skill → Basic Attacks (fill Sinflame to 100) → Ascent of Malice (Demon Hypostasis entry)

**Outro Skill — Wings of Perdition**
- Triggered when Galbrena swaps off-field with full Concerto Energy
- Deals high-multiplier Fusion DMG on cast (Game8 and Wutheringlab both note the Outro Skill has a notably high damage multiplier, comparable to her Forte attacks)
- Provides a Fusion DMG or ATK-type buff to the incoming character (verify exact buff type and value with the Fandom Wiki — sources conflict on whether the Outro provides a personal damage buff or a team relay buff)
- In team rotations, the Outro is the transition point from Galbrena's burst window to the sub-DPS or support's field window; it is also the first event that can refill Afterflame if the sub-DPS enters with Echo Skills active

## Galbrena: Recommended Echo Sets

**Best-in-Slot — Flamewing's Shadow (3-piece) + Flaming Clawprint or Molten Rift (2-piece)**
- The purpose-built BiS combination for Galbrena per Game8, Prydwen, LDShop, Lootbar.gg, and multiple other sources
- **Flamewing's Shadow 5-piece effect** (if 5/0 split): Increases the wearer's Heavy Attack DMG; also provides an additional effect tied to Echo Skill DMG activation — the combination directly amplifies both of Galbrena's damage categories (Heavy Attack and Echo Skill); however, the **3+2 split** is the recommended configuration
- **Flamewing's Shadow 3-piece:** Provides the core Heavy Attack DMG Bonus contribution from the set; the 3-piece threshold captures the most important stat contribution without requiring all 5 echoes from the same set
- **Flaming Clawprint 2-piece or Molten Rift 2-piece:** Both provide **+10% Fusion DMG Bonus**; either pair is appropriate as the 2-piece complement; Flaming Clawprint is slightly preferred for Fusion-specific teams per Game8; Molten Rift is equivalent in most setups
- **Why 3+2 instead of 5-piece Flamewing's Shadow:** The 2-piece Fusion DMG bonus from Flaming Clawprint or Molten Rift provides a stronger per-echo-slot contribution than the 4th and 5th pieces of Flamewing's Shadow at typical echo quality levels; this is the same reasoning applied in Changli and Encore builds with Molten Rift/Flaming Clawprint 2-piece complements

**Main Echo — Corrosaurus (3-Cost)**
- Galbrena's best main echo per VideoGamer and multiple build guides
- **On activation:** Galbrena gains **+12% Fusion DMG Bonus** and **+20% Echo Skill DMG Bonus** for a duration
- Both bonuses directly amplify her Demon Hypostasis attacks (all classified as Fusion and Echo Skill DMG)
- The +20% Echo Skill DMG Bonus is the highest single-echo Echo Skill DMG contribution available and stacks multiplicatively with Qiuyuan's Bamboo's Shade (+30%) and Outro (+50% amplification) for maximum effect
- A 3-Cost echo is preferred over a 4-Cost main echo specifically to avoid compromising the Afterflame generation from team Echo Skills — using the Corrosaurus echo skill counts as one of Galbrena's own Echo Skill casts and contributes +8 Afterflame to herself

**Afterflame Generation from Echo Skills**
The Afterflame system interacts directly with echo selection across the entire team:
- Every unique-named echo in the team that is activated contributes +8 Afterflame to Galbrena while she is off-field (or when the echo is used)
- Three characters × 1 unique echo per character = 24 Afterflame from the team (3 × 8 = 24) as the practical V2.7 maximum; this is the "24-point maximum" referenced in S1 and S6 scaling notes
- The theoretical 40 Afterflame maximum requires 5 differently-named echo activations across the team within one Demon Hypostasis window — achievable with specific echo curation but not guaranteed in all team compositions

**Echo Main Stats Priority**
- 4-Cost Echo: CRIT Rate (to ~75%+ total with passives and subs)
- 3-Cost Echoes (×2): CRIT DMG + Fusion DMG Bonus or ATK%
- 1-Cost Echoes: ATK%

**Sub-Stat Priority:** CRIT Rate (to 75% target) > CRIT DMG > Fusion DMG Bonus > ATK%. Target CRIT Ratio: approximately **75:250** (CRIT Rate : CRIT DMG) per Game8; Galbrena's Inherent Skill 2 provides +2.4% base CRIT Rate and the Forte Attribute Bonuses provide additional CRIT DMG, reducing the sub-stat pressure on both stats somewhat.

## Galbrena: Recommended Weapons

**Best-in-Slot — Lux & Umbra (5-Star Signature Pistols)**
- Purpose-built for Galbrena; the only weapon in the game that specifically buffs both Echo Skill DMG and Heavy Attack DMG simultaneously
- **Stat:** CRIT DMG (base) — the most relevant damage stat for Galbrena's scaling
- **Passive:**
  - After dealing **Echo Skill DMG**: gain **+20% Echo Skill DMG Bonus** for a duration (not specified; verify with Fandom Wiki)
  - After dealing **Heavy Attack DMG**: gain **+20% Heavy Attack DMG Bonus** for a duration
  - When **both buffs are active simultaneously**: Galbrena's DMG partially ignores enemy DEF by **+8% DEF Ignore**
  - The DEF Ignore bonus is the unique advantage of this weapon — Defense penetration scales multiplicatively with all other damage amplifications and is most effective against high-DEF enemies; LDShop calls this the "key advantage" for Demon Hypostasis burst damage
- LDShop: this is Galbrena's "ultimate match" because it synchronizes with her damage rotation and creates a self-sustaining buff cycle between her two damage types; gap vs. alternatives is meaningful but not insurmountable

**Best Standard 5-Star Alternative — Static Mist**
- Stat: CRIT DMG; ATK+ER passive; overall approximately **~20% DPS downgrade** from Lux & Umbra per LDShop
- Passive ER boost helps Galbrena reach her Liberation faster in non-Qiuyuan teams
- Game8 and multiple sources cite this as the clear best non-signature option for comparable CRIT DMG stat base

**Alternative — The Last Dance (5-Star)**
- Passive is described by Game8 as "practically useless" for Galbrena, but its raw CRIT DMG stat is the second best available among 5-star pistols
- Use when Static Mist is unavailable and raw CRIT DMG stat is the priority

**Battle Pass Option — Solar Flame (4-Star)**
- Passive has decent value for Galbrena per Game8; notably lower stat ceiling than 5-star options; recommended for Battle Pass investors who do not have a suitable 5-star pistol

**F2P Alternative — Relativistic Jet (4-Star)**
- ATK% buff passive; functional but significantly lower damage output than any 5-star option; the primary recommendation for players without Battle Pass access or standard 5-star pistols

## Galbrena: Best Teams

**Optimal — Galbrena / Qiuyuan / Shorekeeper**
- The universally recommended best team per Game8, Prydwen, and multiple sources; all three roles are maximally aligned
- **Galbrena** (Main DPS): Primary damage dealer; receives Qiuyuan's full suite: Bamboo's Shade (+30/70% Echo Skill DMG Bonus), Outro +50% Echo Skill DMG Amplification, Liberation CRIT DMG team buff (up to +30% at 65% CRIT Rate); Shorekeeper's CRIT Rate+DMG Stellarealm buff
- **Qiuyuan** (Sub-DPS / Buffer): The only character in the game providing Echo Skill DMG amplification; his Outro +50% Echo Skill DMG Amplification is the highest single-source buff of its type; his compact rotation (~6–9s on-field) maximizes time for Galbrena's Demon Hypostasis window; all three Qiuyuan buffs (Bamboo's Shade, Liberation CRIT DMG, Outro) are simultaneously active on Galbrena during her field window; Qiuyuan's Echo Skill activations also contribute **+8 Afterflame per cast** to Galbrena's gauge during her off-field window
- **Shorekeeper** (Healer / Universal Support): Best support in the game for her healing and the Stellarealm CRIT Rate + CRIT DMG buff for all active Resonators; the CRIT buff stacks with Qiuyuan's CRIT DMG buff rather than duplicating an ATK buff, making them multiplicatively beneficial rather than partially redundant
- *Team synergy note:* Qiuyuan's Bamboo's Shade is active from his Intro Skill frame; his Liberation fires CRIT DMG buff for 30s; his Outro applies +50% Echo Skill Amplification for 14s; within this 14s window, Galbrena enters Demon Hypostasis with full Afterflame buffs + S1 CRIT DMG + Fated End 4-stack + Liberation +85% multiplier + Shorekeeper CRIT buffs active simultaneously; this convergence of multipliers during the 14-second Outro window is the peak damage event of the rotation

**Mono Fusion — Galbrena / Lupa / Brant**
- Maximizes Fusion DMG Bonus contributions via full-team Fusion element synergy
- **Lupa** (Sub-DPS / Buffer): One of the highest buffing capabilities for Fusion-centric teams; provides Pack Hunt buff (ATK + Fusion DMG for the team); Lupa's Echo Skill activations contribute Afterflame to Galbrena
- **Brant** (Sub-DPS / Healer): Provides Fusion DMG Amplification and team healing; Echo Skill activations contribute Afterflame; can be replaced by Changli per DbTap for slightly different performance balance
- *Note:* Changli is a viable Brant substitute for players who have her; her Fusion DMG buffs are comparable and she provides more personal damage at the cost of healing
- Because all three characters are Fusion, the entire team benefits from Fusion element composition bonuses; Galbrena's Heavy Attack and Echo Skill DMG both receive Fusion DMG Bonus amplification from Lupa's buffs regardless of damage type classification

**Heavy Attack Focus — Galbrena / Iuno / Shorekeeper**
- Used when Qiuyuan is unavailable; Iuno provides Heavy Attack DMG Amplification
- **Iuno** (Sub-DPS): Her Outro Skill provides **Heavy Attack DMG Amplification** to the incoming character; all of Galbrena's Demon Hypostasis attacks have some degree of Heavy Attack DMG classification, making Iuno's Outro relevant to multiple hit categories; Iuno also deals meaningful damage as a Sub-DPS
- **Shorekeeper** (Healer / Support): Same role as in the Qiuyuan team
- *Note:* This team lacks the Echo Skill DMG Amplification that makes the Qiuyuan team optimal; it trades Echo Skill DMG Bonus for Heavy Attack DMG Amplification, which applies to a smaller subset of Galbrena's damage but is still meaningful given Flamewing Verdict's Heavy Attack classification

## Galbrena: Rotation Guide

**Standard Rotation (Qiuyuan team)**
Pre-rotation (Qiuyuan's window):
1. Qiuyuan enters via Intro Skill → Bamboo's Shade active; +40% Echo Skill Bonus (Emerald Sentence)
2. Qiuyuan casts Resonance Skill → CRIT DMG buff; generates Swordster's Soliloquy
3. Qiuyuan casts Liberation → CRIT DMG team buff (up to +30%); Inksplash of Mind activated
4. Qiuyuan executes To Teach → To Save → To Sacrifice Enhanced Heavy Attack chain
5. Qiuyuan Outro Skill → Galbrena enters with **+50% Echo Skill DMG Amplification for 14s**

Galbrena's window (must complete primary damage within 14s):
6. Enter via **Intro Skill — Hellflare Overload** (→ Fated End +1 stack; Sinflame generated; Afterflame from off-field team Echo Skills already loaded)
7. Activate **Echo Skill (Corrosaurus)** (→ +12% Fusion Bonus + 20% Echo Skill Bonus for Galbrena; +8 Afterflame to herself)
8. **Basic Attack chain** (× 4–5 hits until Sinflame reaches 100; each hit generates Fated End stacks)
9. **Resonance Skill — Ascent of Malice** (enters Demon Hypostasis; Sinflame → Purging Flame; Afterflame consumed → Fusion DMG Bonus applied; S1: CRIT DMG spike applied)
10. **Resonance Liberation — Hellblaze Pyre** (immediately after entry; +85% DMG Multiplier buff for 14s; Liberation Echo Skill DMG hit; Fated End stack; in Eternal Hypostasis if S6)
11. Press Normal Attack after Liberation (→ Seraphic Execution Stage 2 shortcut)
12. **Basic Attack — Seraphic Execution** × 5-stage chain (full chain before Flamewing Verdict for maximum Purging Flame efficiency)
13. **Heavy Attack — Flamewing Verdict** × Stage 3 (wing-slash hits; Echo Skill DMG)
14. Additional **Seraphic Execution / Ravage** chains until Purging Flame is depleted
15. Outro Skill → incoming support/sub-DPS field time → Afterflame refill begins for next cycle

**Key Rotation Rules**
- Liberation must always be cast **after** entering Demon Hypostasis (via Ascent of Malice); casting it before gives a Liberation hit but wastes the +85% Demon Hypostasis multiplier buff on attacks before the state is active
- Consume all Afterflame before entering Demon Hypostasis is automatic (Ascent of Malice consumes it on cast); the only action needed is ensuring Afterflame is as full as possible via team Echo Skills before Ascent of Malice fires
- Hellstride (special Dodge) is the preferred defensive action during Demon Hypostasis; it generates Sinflame for the next cycle and recovers Stamina without breaking the Purging Flame chain
- Complete the Seraphic Execution Stage 5 chain and Flamewing Verdict Stage 3 specifically because both gain interruption immunity with S1; without S1, shorter attack strings that reduce the interrupt window are preferable in high-incoming-damage content

**CRIT Build Target**
Optimal CRIT ratio: **~75% CRIT Rate / ~250% CRIT DMG** (Game8); reach CRIT Rate target first before investing in CRIT DMG subs; the Inherent Skill 2 passive provides +2.4% base CRIT Rate and the Forte Attribute Bonuses provide CRIT DMG, moderating the sub-stat investment needed for both.
