# Verina Kit Documentation

## Verina: Combat Archetype and Role

- **Element:** Spectro
- **Weapon Type:** Rectifier
- **Role:** Universal Healer and Offensive Buffer
- **Archetype:** Photosynthesis Energy manager; Coordinated Attack off-field sustain; All-Type DMG Deepen provider; fatal-damage prevention
- **Banner Type:** Standard 5-Star — available at launch from all Character Convenes; common off-banner pull from limited banners

Verina is the premier universal support of Wuthering Waves Version 1.x and remains highly relevant through subsequent versions. Her designation as "universal" is precise: her Outro Skill's +15% All-Type DMG Deepen applies to all party members regardless of element, making her the only top-tier healer whose offensive buff is not restricted to a specific damage type or team composition. Every team that does not include Shorekeeper typically runs Verina. Her healing scales with ATK rather than HP, making her buildable on offensive stats; her Photosynthesis Mark Coordinated Attacks trigger once per second off-field, providing passive Spectro damage and healing throughout the Main DPS's field window. At S0, she already outperforms Baizhi in both Concerto Energy generation speed and healing output. She is the recommended starting healer for all players, and one of the most broadly impactful characters in the game's first year.

## Verina: Key Resource — Photosynthesis Energy

Verina's entire combat identity centers on the **Photosynthesis Energy** gauge — a 0-to-4 stack resource that is generated by specific ability triggers and consumed by her Forte Circuit to produce her primary healing events.

**Photosynthesis Energy Generation (3 sources)**
- **Basic Attack Stage 5 (Cultivation — final hit of the 5-stage chain):** Grants +1 Photosynthesis Energy on hit
- **Resonance Skill Botany Experiment (on cast):** Grants +1 Photosynthesis Energy on hit; does not require the skill to fully connect with an enemy — Concerto Energy is generated regardless, but Photosynthesis Energy requires the hit to land
- **Intro Skill Verdant Growth (on hit):** Grants +1 Photosynthesis Energy on hit; this is the primary reason Prydwen notes that correctly receiving Verina's Intro Skill is important in her rotation, as it front-loads one stack immediately on swap-in

**Maximum Stacks:** 4 Photosynthesis Energy simultaneously

**Photosynthesis Energy Consumption — Forte Circuit: Starflower Blooms**
When Verina has at least 1 Photosynthesis Energy, her Heavy Attack and Mid-air Attack inputs are transformed:
- **Heavy Attack: Starflower Blooms** — consumes 1 Photosynthesis Energy; deals Spectro DMG (classified as Heavy Attack DMG); heals all characters on nearby teams; restores **+12 Concerto Energy** per consumption
- **Mid-air Attack: Starflower Blooms** — consumes 1 Photosynthesis Energy; deals Spectro DMG (classified as Basic Attack DMG); heals all characters on nearby teams; restores **+12 Concerto Energy** per consumption; can be chained after Heavy Attack Starflower Blooms

**Concerto Energy Efficiency**
Each Photosynthesis Energy stack consumed generates +12 Concerto Energy. With 4 stacks, consuming all generates +48 Concerto Energy from Forte consumption alone. Combined with Basic Attack chain generation, Resonance Skill generation, Liberation generation, and weapon effects, Verina can reliably produce over 100 Concerto Energy per rotation (120+ at S2), enabling a consistent short cycle of ~3–4 seconds per Outro Skill trigger — among the fastest Concerto generation rates in the game.

**Practical Rotation Goal**
The standard approach is to enter with Intro Skill (+1 stack, +Concerto) → Resonance Skill (+1 stack, +Concerto) → Basic Attack chain to stage 5 if needed (+1 stack) → Liberation (heals all, marks enemies, +20 Concerto) → consume remaining Photosynthesis Energy stacks via aerial Starflower Blooms chain → Outro Skill. At S2, the Resonance Skill generates an additional +1 Photosynthesis Energy and +10 Concerto, significantly accelerating this cycle.

## Verina: Character Kit: Basic Attack — Cultivation

**Standard Ground Chain (5 stages)**
- Up to 5 consecutive vine attacks, all dealing Spectro DMG
- **Stage 5 (final hit):** Grants +1 Photosynthesis Energy on hit — the only Basic Attack hit that generates a stack; the full chain must be completed to reach it
- In fast rotations, Verina typically does not complete the full Basic Attack chain; the Skill-cancel combo (Basic Attacks → Resonance Skill cancel → Liberation → aerial Starflower Blooms) reaches Concerto thresholds faster
- Generating Photosynthesis Energy from Basic Attacks is a secondary method; Intro Skill and Resonance Skill are more efficient per-cast

**Heavy Attack**
- Charges forward, dealing Spectro DMG (Stamina cost)
- If Photosynthesis Energy is held: replaced by **Heavy Attack: Starflower Blooms** (see Forte Circuit)

**Mid-air Attack**
- Up to 3 consecutive mid-air attacks, dealing Spectro DMG (Stamina cost)
- If Photosynthesis Energy is held: replaced by **Mid-air Attack: Starflower Blooms** (see Forte Circuit)
- Can be chained after Heavy Attack: Starflower Blooms

**Mid-air Heavy Attack (Hold)**
- Plunging attack from mid-air, dealing Spectro DMG (Stamina cost)

**Dodge Counter**
- Basic Attack after successful Dodge; deals Spectro DMG

**Rotation Trick — Aerial Consumption Chain**
To rapidly consume all 4 Photosynthesis Energy stacks, hold Basic Attack (Heavy Attack → Starflower Blooms), then immediately continue with the 3-hit Mid-air Attack chain (Mid-air Starflower Blooms × up to 3 additional stacks). Prydwen notes this method generates substantial Concerto Energy quickly and is the efficient Forte consumption method in a tight rotation.

## Verina: Character Kit: Resonance Skill — Botany Experiment

- Verina converges an energy field forward to grow foliage, dealing Spectro DMG in an area
- Grants **+1 Photosynthesis Energy** on hit (requires the skill to connect — no hit, no stack)
- Generates Concerto Energy on cast (amount confirmed by multiple sources; exact value varies by level)
- The skill can be **dodge-canceled** immediately after cast — Concerto Energy is still generated even with the cancel; damage does not come out and no Resonance Energy is generated; Prydwen recommends canceling immediately into Liberation if energy requirements are already met, trading minor damage/energy for faster rotation execution
- S2 enhancement: Resonance Skill additionally grants +1 Photosynthesis Energy AND +10 Concerto Energy — turning this from a single-stack generator into a double-stack generator; dramatically improves rotation speed

**Skill Upgrade Priority Note:** Botany Experiment's primary function in a standard rotation is Photosynthesis Energy and Concerto Energy generation. Its damage multiplier is secondary. Upgrade after Forte Circuit and Resonance Liberation when resources allow.

## Verina: Character Kit: Resonance Liberation — Arboreal Flourish

- **On Cast:** Deals Spectro DMG to all nearby enemies; heals all characters on nearby teams simultaneously (burst heal — the most reliable emergency heal in her kit); generates 20 Concerto Energy
- **Photosynthesis Mark Application:** Applied to all enemies hit by the Liberation; persists for 12 seconds
- **Photosynthesis Mark Effect:** Whenever any team member attacks a Photosynthesis-Marked enemy, Verina performs a **Coordinated Attack** — Spectro DMG to the target + heals the active character dealing damage — triggered once per second, independent of whether Verina is on the field
- This off-field mechanic provides continuous healing and Spectro damage during the Main DPS's entire field window without requiring Verina to be present
- **Resonance Energy Cost:** Standard liberation cost (verify exact value with Fandom Wiki)
- **Concerto Generation:** 20 Concerto Energy on cast

**Inherent Skill 1 (Gift of Nature) Interaction**
Casting Liberation triggers the +20% ATK buff for all team members (see Inherent Passives below). This makes Liberation not only a healing event but also the primary ATK buff trigger in rotations where Forte Starflower Blooms have not been cast recently.

**Skill Upgrade Priority:** Liberation is the second upgrade priority after Forte Circuit — it heals all characters simultaneously and applies the Photosynthesis Mark that sustains off-field healing through the rest of the rotation.

## Verina: Forte Circuit: Starflower Blooms

The core of Verina's healing. Both the ground (Heavy Attack) and aerial (Mid-air Attack) versions function identically in terms of outcome; the aerial version is classified as Basic Attack DMG rather than Heavy Attack DMG, which matters for specific echo or buff interactions.

**Heavy Attack: Starflower Blooms**
- Triggered when casting Heavy Attack while holding 1+ Photosynthesis Energy
- Consumes **1 Photosynthesis Energy** stack
- Deals Spectro DMG (Heavy Attack DMG type)
- Heals all characters on nearby teams
- Restores **+12 Concerto Energy** to Verina
- Triggers Inherent Skill 1 (Gift of Nature) +20% ATK buff for the team

**Mid-air Attack: Starflower Blooms**
- Triggered when casting Mid-air Attack while holding 1+ Photosynthesis Energy
- Consumes **1 Photosynthesis Energy** stack
- Deals Spectro DMG (Basic Attack DMG type — not Heavy Attack DMG)
- Heals all characters on nearby teams
- Restores **+12 Concerto Energy** to Verina
- Triggers Inherent Skill 1 (Gift of Nature) +20% ATK buff for the team
- Can be chain-cast: up to 3 additional mid-air Starflower Blooms after the initial Heavy Attack Starflower Blooms (consuming 1 stack per cast, for up to 4 stacks total consumed in one aerial chain)

**Forte Circuit Upgrade Priority:** First — this directly improves both the healing output and the Concerto Energy return of every Photosynthesis Energy consumption, which is the primary activity of Verina's kit.

## Verina: Inherent Passives

**Inherent Skill 1 — Gift of Nature**
- When Verina casts **Heavy Attack: Starflower Blooms**, **Mid-air Attack: Starflower Blooms**, **Resonance Liberation: Arboreal Flourish**, or **Outro Skill: Blossom** → all team members' **ATK is increased by +20%** for **20 seconds**
- This triggers on every one of these events, meaning the +20% ATK buff is refreshed every time Verina heals through any of these four methods during a rotation
- In a standard rotation (Liberation → aerial Starflower Blooms chain → Outro), Gift of Nature fires 3–6 times total, easily maintaining near-permanent +20% ATK for the team throughout the full DPS window

**Inherent Skill 2 — Nature's Protection**
- Verina **protects a team member from fatal damage**, preventing the killing blow; grants a **shield equal to 120% of Verina's ATK** for 10 seconds
- **Cooldown:** Once every 10 minutes — a long cooldown by design; this is an emergency failsafe, not a rotation element
- Functions even when Verina is off-field; applies to the character who would have taken fatal damage
- Unlocked at Level 70 (verify unlock level with Fandom Wiki)
- In high-difficulty content (Tower of Adversity, endgame challenges), this passive occasionally saves runs entirely, making it practically high-value even with its long cooldown

## Verina: Intro/Outro Skills

**Intro Skill — Verdant Growth**
- Triggered when Verina enters the field via swap (requires Concerto Energy to be full for the Intro to fire)
- Verina attacks the target, dealing Spectro DMG
- Grants **+1 Photosynthesis Energy** on hit — the single most efficient per-stack generator relative to the time cost, as it fires automatically on entry
- **Important Prydwen Note:** Casting Intro Skill is NOT recommended as Verina's primary rotation entry. Because it extends her on-field time and makes reaching Concerto Energy thresholds awkward, Prydwen recommends passing the Intro to another character in the team twice (letting the other characters proc their Intro Skills) rather than always using Verina's. This is a meaningful rotation optimization for players comfortable with swap timing. The stack from Intro is valuable, but not at the cost of rotation smoothness.

**Outro Skill — Blossom**
- Triggered when Verina swaps off-field with full Concerto Energy
- **Immediate heal:** Heals the incoming Resonator for **19% of Verina's ATK per second for 6 seconds** (continuous, applied to whoever enters the field)
- **Team-wide buff:** All Resonators on nearby teams gain **+15% All-Type DMG Deepen for 30 seconds**
  - This buff does not disappear when switching characters — it persists through the entire 30-second window regardless of which character is active, making it the most rotation-friendly offensive buff in the game
  - The 30-second duration typically covers the full team's remaining DPS window after Verina rotates out
- **Gift of Nature trigger:** Casting Blossom fires the +20% ATK buff for the team (Inherent Skill 1)
- S1 enhancement: Blossom additionally heals the next character for 20% of Verina's ATK every 5 seconds for 30 seconds — sustained healing for the Main DPS throughout their field time

**Why Verina's Outro is Exceptional**
Most healer Outro Skills in Wuthering Waves buff a specific damage type (e.g., Baizhi's +10% Havoc DMG, Shorekeeper's CRIT buffs). Verina's +15% All-Type DMG Deepen applies to every element and every damage type, meaning it amplifies any Main DPS without requiring elemental matching. Combined with Gift of Nature's +20% ATK, Verina's Outro window gives the Main DPS both an ATK multiplier and a flat damage amplification — two of the most broadly effective buff types available.

## Verina: Resonance Chains (Sequences)

**S1 — Rejuvenating Glow**
- Outro Skill Blossom additionally heals the next character for **20% of Verina's ATK every 5 seconds for 30 seconds**
- *Impact:* This transforms the Outro from a burst heal + team buff into an Outro heal + sustained healing for the incoming character over their full field time. The Main DPS receives continuous HP recovery throughout the 30 seconds they are active after Verina rotates out. Very high practical value; among the more impactful first sequences in the game. Recommended target if pulling Verina intentionally.

**S2 — Fertile Fields**
- Resonance Skill Botany Experiment additionally grants **+1 Photosynthesis Energy** AND **+10 Concerto Energy** per cast
- *Impact:* Doubles Botany Experiment's Photosynthesis Energy output (from 1 to 2 stacks per cast) and adds a meaningful Concerto Energy bonus. This cuts the on-field time needed to build a full stack set from multiple Basic Attack stages + one Skill to essentially Intro + Skill + Liberation. Wuwalabo notes Verina can reliably generate over 120 Concerto Energy per rotation at S2. Significantly smooths her rotation; reducing on-field time is directly valuable because it extends the Main DPS's field window. High practical impact.

**S3 — Cascading Bloom**
- Resonance Liberation Photosynthesis Mark healing is **increased by +12%**
- *Impact:* A healing amplification on the off-field Coordinated Attack heals triggered by the Photosynthesis Mark. Meaningful in sustained content or high-damage encounters where the 1-per-second tick healing needs to keep pace with incoming damage. Moderate impact for most content; higher value in difficult endgame encounters.

**S4 — Blossoming Embrace**
- Heavy Attack Starflower Blooms, Mid-air Attack Starflower Blooms, Resonance Liberation Arboreal Flourish, and Outro Skill Blossom now also increase **all party members' Spectro DMG Bonus by +15% for 24 seconds**
- *Impact:* Adds a Spectro-specific DMG bonus on top of all of Verina's existing buffs. This is the sequence that makes Verina an even stronger support for Spectro-element Main DPS characters (most relevantly: Rover Spectro, Jinhsi via Spectro team synergy, Zhezhi). In non-Spectro teams this bonus goes unused; in Spectro teams it is a significant additional offensive amplification. Wuwalabo specifically calls S4 recommended for players investing in Verina for Spectro compositions.

**S5 — Flourishing Life**
- When Verina heals a team member whose HP is **below 50%**, her healing is **increased by +20%**
- *Impact:* A conditional healing amplification that activates exactly when healing is most critical — when an ally is in danger. Does not affect her standard rotation output when the team is healthy, but meaningfully increases emergency heal output in high-damage scenarios. Solid defensive sequence for difficult endgame content; lower practical value in casual play.

**S6 — Blossoming Splendor**
- Heavy Attack: Starflower Blooms and Mid-air Attack: Starflower Blooms deal **+20% more DMG**
- They now additionally **trigger one Coordinated Attack** and **heal all characters nearby** — equivalent in heal and damage output to the Resonance Liberation's Photosynthesis Mark Coordinated Attack
- *Impact:* Transforms every Forte consumption into a mini-Liberation heal event — each Starflower Blooms cast now heals the full team (not just the active character) and triggers a Coordinated Attack. In a 4-stack consumption chain, this is 4 additional team heals + 4 additional Coordinated Attacks per Forte cycle. The highest ceiling for Verina's sustain output; dramatically improves team healing in extended high-difficulty encounters. GuildJen notes that overall sequences are not required for Verina to perform excellently and the incremental gains beyond S1–S2 are moderate.

**Sequence Pull Priority:** S0 is strong; S1 and S2 provide the most practical improvement to rotation smoothness and sustained healing; S4 is relevant for dedicated Spectro compositions; S3, S5, S6 are incremental. For free-to-play players, S1–S2 off-banner are common and welcome; intentional sequence investment beyond S2 is not recommended unless building a specialized Spectro team.

## Verina: Recommended Echo Sets

**Best-in-Slot: Rejuvenating Glow (5-piece)**
- The uncontested best-in-slot echo set for Verina; purpose-built for Spectro healing supports
- 5-piece effect: **+10% Healing Bonus** + whenever Verina heals a character, all team members gain **+15% ATK for 30 seconds** — nearly permanent ATK buff given how frequently Verina heals through Forte, Liberation, and Photosynthesis Mark Coordinated Attacks
- The 15% ATK buff from this set stacks directly with Gift of Nature's +20% ATK, providing ~35% ATK for the team simultaneously during active healing windows — an exceptional offensive multiplier for a healing set

**Main Echo: Bell-Borne Geochelone**
- The standard recommended main echo for Verina in the Rejuvenating Glow set
- On cast: generates a protective field that absorbs incoming damage AND grants the active Resonator +12% ATK and +12% DEF for 10 seconds; strong defensive utility + offensive contribution
- Wuwalabo specifically recommends using the Echo skill as part of Verina's rotation before swapping out, enabling the ATK buff to carry into the Main DPS's field window
- Alternative main echoes (if Bell-Borne is unavailable at high tuning): any Spectro 4-cost echo providing useful team utility; Bell-Borne is strongly preferred

**Echo Main Stats Priority**
- 4-Cost Echo: ATK% (healing scales with ATK; this directly amplifies all heal output)
- 3-Cost Echoes (×2): ATK% or Healing Bonus%
- 1-Cost Echoes: ATK% (sub-stats matter more at this cost tier)

**Sub-Stat Priority:** ATK% = Energy Regen ≥ Flat ATK = Healing Bonus%. Energy Regen reduces the on-field time needed to reach Liberation; ATK% directly amplifies every heal. Lootbar.gg and Game8 both note the recommended sub-stat order as ATK > ATK% > Resonance Efficiency > Liberation DMG Bonus (the last for players who want to maximize Liberation damage output, which is a secondary concern).

## Verina: Recommended Weapons

**Best-in-Slot 5-Star — Stellar Symphony**
- Stat: Energy Regen — directly reduces the on-field time Verina needs to cycle Liberation
- Passive: Increases HP by 12%; when casting a Resonance Skill that heals, increases all party members' ATK by 14% for 30 seconds
- The Resonance Skill ATK buff stacks with Gift of Nature's +20% ATK and Rejuvenating Glow's +15% ATK — the three together provide a combined ~49% ATK buff to the team during active heal windows, an extraordinary team offensive contribution from a healer
- Available on the Shorekeeper banner (Version 1.3); not always accessible; plan accordingly

**Best-in-Slot 4-Star (Standard) — Variation**
- Stat: Energy Regen
- Passive: When casting Resonance Skill, restores 8 Concerto Energy once every 20 seconds
- Prydwen and Game8 both note that Variation at higher Syntony levels can outperform a lower Stellar Symphony; prioritize whichever has higher refinement; Variation is the most accessible strong weapon for Verina from standard banner pulls

**Good 4-Star Alternatives**
- **Call of the Abyss:** 4-star; obtainable via in-game crafting or exploration in Rinascita; strong Energy Regen stat; a reliable free-to-play option
- **Rectifier#25:** Craftable 4-star from in-game synthesis; decent ATK scaling; accessible early; a valid placeholder
- **Jinzhou Keeper:** Solid at higher refinements; falls behind Variation at lower Syntony

**Avoid for Verina**
- Any weapon without ATK or Energy Regen as its primary stat; Verina's healing output and rotation speed depend on ATK (for heal scaling) and Energy Regen (for Liberation frequency); weapons providing DMG Bonus or elemental damage are minimally useful on a support character

## Verina: Best Teams

**Universal Support — [Any DPS] / [Any Sub-DPS] / Verina**
Verina's defining trait is that she fits into any team that lacks Shorekeeper. Her All-Type DMG Deepen Outro eliminates the need for elemental matching with her support partner. The following are the primary compositions she appeared in through Version 1.x:

**Version 1.0 Premium — Jiyan / Mortefi / Verina**
- **Verina** (Healer/Buffer): Outro provides +15% All-Type DMG Deepen; Gift of Nature +20% ATK; Photosynthesis Mark sustains healing during Jiyan's Qingloong Mode
- **Jiyan** (Main DPS): Aero Broadblade; benefits from both Verina's ATK buff and All-Type Deepen; Mortefi's Outro provides additional Aero DMG amplification for Jiyan
- **Mortefi** (Sub-DPS/Buffer): Outro +38% Heavy ATK DMG Amplification stacks with Verina's buffs; the combination is the defining Version 1.0 meta composition
- *Role:* Verina holds the team together — healing during Jiyan's vulnerable swap windows, buffing both Jiyan and Mortefi's output simultaneously

**Version 1.1 Premium — Calcharo / Yinlin / Verina**
- **Verina** (Healer/Buffer): Outro + Gift of Nature; heals during Calcharo's Liberation burst window
- **Calcharo** (Main DPS): Electro Broadblade; Death Messenger Liberation state benefits from Yinlin's Outro (+Electro DMG + Liberation DMG) and Verina's universal Deepen + ATK buff simultaneously
- **Yinlin** (Sub-DPS): Punishment Mark Judgment Strikes provide sustained Electro Coordinated Attack damage during Calcharo's Liberation; Outro complements Calcharo's Liberation-focused damage
- *Role:* Verina is the tertiary buffer enabling the Yinlin→Calcharo damage spike to survive at full HP; without a healer, Calcharo's high on-field commitment during Death Messenger is unsafe

**Budget / Early Game — Rover / Yangyang / Verina**
- **Verina** (Healer/Buffer): Full support rotation; Outro's All-Type Deepen benefits Rover regardless of their current element; Gift of Nature +20% ATK amplifies both Rover and Yangyang
- **Rover** (Main DPS — any element): The most accessible DPS; Verina's non-elemental buff does not require Rover to run a specific element
- **Yangyang** (Sub-DPS): Aero support; Concerto Energy regeneration for Rover via Outro; AoE grouping; Verina's healing keeps the team safe while Yangyang and Rover execute
- *Note:* Game8 recommends this as the starting team for players whose only 5-star is Verina; it is functional from early game through early-endgame content

**Spectro Composition — Rover (Spectro) / Zhezhi / Verina**
- Verina's S4 provides an additional +15% Spectro DMG Bonus on top of her standard buffs; this makes her the preferred healer for dedicated Spectro compositions once S4 is accessible
- Without S4, Shorekeeper is typically preferred for Spectro teams due to her CRIT buff and Spectro synergy; Verina functions well but loses the Spectro-specific bonus

**Versus Baizhi**
Verina is universally preferred over Baizhi for the following reasons (per Wutheringlab, Prydwen, and Game8): higher Concerto generation rate enabling faster Outro cycling; stronger offensive Outro (All-Type Deepen vs. Baizhi's element-limited buff); better raw healing output per rotation; Inherent fatal-damage protection. Baizhi is a strong free-to-play alternative for players who have not obtained Verina and remains functional through endgame content, but Verina is objectively superior in all dimensions.

## Verina: Rotation Guide

**Standard Rotation (with Intro Skill)**
1. Swap to Verina (Intro Skill fires → +1 Photosynthesis Energy + Spectro DMG + Concerto)
2. Resonance Skill: Botany Experiment (→ +1 Photosynthesis Energy + Concerto; dodge-cancel into Liberation if energy is sufficient)
3. Resonance Liberation: Arboreal Flourish (→ burst heal all characters + Photosynthesis Mark on enemies + 20 Concerto; triggers Gift of Nature +20% ATK)
4. Echo Skill: Bell-Borne Geochelone (→ defensive field + ATK/DEF buff for incoming character)
5. Aerial Starflower Blooms chain (Heavy Attack → Mid-air Attack ×1–3, consuming remaining Photosynthesis Energy stacks; each consumption heals all characters + 12 Concerto; each triggers Gift of Nature +20% ATK refresh)
6. Outro Skill: Blossom (→ heal incoming character 19% ATK/s × 6s; all team +15% All-Type DMG Deepen × 30s; triggers final Gift of Nature +20% ATK)
7. Swap to Main DPS (receives all active buffs: +15% All-Type Deepen, +20% ATK, S1 sustained healing, Photosynthesis Mark Coordinated Attack heals)

**Prydwen Optimization Note:** Prydwen recommends skipping Verina's Intro Skill in favor of letting other characters use their Intro Skills twice. In this pattern, Verina enters without Intro activation (soft swap); this saves on-field time but loses the +1 Photosynthesis Energy from Verdant Growth. The tradeoff is faster rotations at the cost of a small stack-generation deficit. For players comfortable with swap timing, the rotation without Intro Skill is: Basic Attacks stage 3 → Resonance Skill → Liberation → Starflower Blooms chain → Outro.

**Key Rotation Rules**
- Do not forget the Echo Skill of Bell-Borne Geochelone — it contributes the ATK/DEF buff that carries into the Main DPS's window
- Resonance Skill can be dodge-canceled without losing Concerto Energy; useful for reducing total on-field time when Liberation is already ready
- Consuming all 4 Photosynthesis Energy stacks before Outro ensures maximum pre-swap healing; the aerial chain is the fastest consumption method
- Photosynthesis Mark Coordinated Attacks continue once per second while any team member attacks the marked enemy — Verina does not need to be on the field for these to fire
