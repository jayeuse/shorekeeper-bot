# Black Shores Main Quests

<!-- Sources: https://wutheringwaves.fandom.com/wiki/Chapter_I, https://wutheringwaves.fandom.com/wiki/Chapter_II -->

## Overview

The Black Shores is the setting for the closing acts of **Chapter I: Jinzhou Rising** (Acts VII and VIII) and the post-Rinascita bridging acts of **Chapter II** (Act XII and the Segue). These quests collectively mark the Rover's reintegration into the organization they founded, the resolution of the Tethys System crisis, and the transition into the Lahai-Roi arc. Mechanically, they introduce the Black Shores as an explorable region, the portal-gun traversal mechanic in Tethys' Deep, and the **Fallacy of No Return: Tethys System** world boss.
