## Segue: Flowing Starlight in the Iris — Overview

- **Chapter:** Chapter II — Even When Divinity Remains Silent (Afterstory/Segue)
- **Act Type:** Segue (post-Chapter epilogue)
- **Region:** Black Shores (primarily Tethys Hub and Shashou Cafe)
- **Release Version:** Wuthering Waves Version 2.8 (released simultaneously with Act XII, November 20, 2025)
- **Prerequisite:** Completion of Chapter II Act XII: Stagnant Dawn on Wastelands
- **Spotlight:** Chisa's character conclusion; teaser for Lahai-Roi (Chapter III)
- **Significance:** Closes the Chapter II era; reveals the **Startorch Academy** in Lahai-Roi as the next destination; unlocks the **Stranger Things in Honami** permanent event.

## Segue: Flowing Starlight in the Iris — Synopsis

Following Chisa's escape from Honami City, the Rover waits for her to recover from her Overclocking injuries at the Black Shores medical facilities. Upon her recovery, the Rover seeks her out at the Tethys Hub. The quest begins by speaking to **Valentina**, who provides an update on Chisa's condition, then locating **KU-Shosho** (a KU-robot) in the Haven of Sprouts, who reveals where Chisa wandered off to and how she is faring post-treatment.

Chisa, having experienced a two-month subjective stay in a loop that spanned twenty external years, is grappling with the disorientation of being a "fish out of temporal water" — the world she knew before entering Honami has moved on in ways she cannot immediately comprehend. The Rover finds her and spends time helping her reorient.

The climax of the Segue is a small **party at Shashou Cafe** — attended by Buling, Abby (Abraxas), Namipon, Shorekeeper, and Camellya — celebrating Chisa's recovery and officially welcoming her and Shashou into the Black Shores family. The Rover falls asleep at the cafe after the party.

## Segue: Flowing Starlight in the Iris — The Lahai-Roi Tease

Upon waking, the Rover searches for Chisa and finds her near the cafe's al fresco seating area. In the final cutscene, the game officially reveals **Startorch Academy** — a research institution located deep underground beneath the snowy terrain of Lahai-Roi. Chisa expresses her intention to return to her old school, and the Rover, guided by the Black Shores' next mission parameters, agrees to travel there together. This marks the official handoff from Chapter II to Chapter III, and establishes Lahai-Roi as the next explorable region.

## Segue: Flowing Starlight in the Iris — Key Revelations

- **Chisa's temporal displacement:** Two months inside the loop equated to twenty years outside. This dislocation is a defining character trait going forward — Chisa must adjust to a world that has aged around her.
- **Startorch Academy and Lahai-Roi confirmed:** The final cutscene visually teases the underground academy, with its distinctive aesthetic of subterranean warmth beneath frozen ice layers, officially establishing the Chapter III setting.
- **The full party at Shashou Cafe:** The gathering serves as an emotional capstone for the Black Shores' post-Rinascita era — confirming all key members are safe, oriented, and moving forward.
- **The Abraxas thread persists:** Abby's presence at the party (addressed by Leviathan as "Abraxas" during Chapter II) underscores the ongoing mystery of her identity, which remains unresolved into Chapter III.
- **Connection to Rover's Past:** A link is established between Chisa and the Rover's pre-amnesia past; Chisa reveals she had read a **course guide written by the Rover** at Startorch Academy, cementing the Rover's role as a former scholar/teacher there.
- **The Kirigami Artwork:** Chisa gives the Rover a Kirigami artwork at the end of the quest, symbolizing her acceptance of the "stagnant" 20-year gap as her new reality and her gratitude for being saved.

## Segue: Flowing Starlight in the Iris — Gameplay Significance

- Completes Chapter II and bridges directly to Chapter III.
- Unlocks the **Stranger Things in Honami** permanent event for long-term Chronorift Metropolis exploration.
- Unlocks the **"You, The Starlight"** trophy, granting 10 Astrites.
- The Lahai-Roi visual tease directly preceded the Version 3.0 regional launch.
