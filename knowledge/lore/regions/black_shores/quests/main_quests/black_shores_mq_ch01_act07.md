## Act VII: Thaw of Eons — Overview

- **Chapter:** Chapter I — Jinzhou Rising
- **Act Number:** Act VII
- **Region:** Huanglong (Mt. Firmament / Hongzhen) — precursor arc to the Black Shores storyline
- **Release Version:** Wuthering Waves Version 1.1 (June 27, 2024)
- **Prerequisite:** Completion of Chapter I Act VI: Grand Warstorm, and Chapter I Interlude: A New Companion
- **Unlock Requirement:** Completion of the interlude quest "A New Companion"
- **Subquests (in order):** Dreamweaver's Ascent → To Mt. Firmament → Truthseeker's Pass → Road of Pilgrimage → Mianloong Chamber → The Commander of Time → Dawn of Snowstorm
- **Significance:** Introduces the Sentinel Jué, the region of Mt. Firmament and Hongzhen, and fully resolves Jinhsi's Secondary Resonance Awakening; the Sentinel's stabilization of its Temporal Programs is the direct prerequisite that allows the Rover to proceed to the Black Shores arc.

## Act VII: Thaw of Eons — Synopsis

Act VII follows the Rover to the sealed, snow-shrouded peaks of **Mt. Firmament**, southeast of Jinzhou — a region inaccessible to civilians, where time flows unnaturally slowly due to the presence of the **Sentinel Jué**. The Rover journeys through Hongzhen, the ancestral homeland of Jinzhou's people, guided by **Jinhsi**, the Magistrate of Jinzhou and Jué's Appointed Resonator.

Upon reaching the **Mianloong Chamber** — the colossal vehicle that carried Jué to Solaris-III in ancient times — the Rover witnesses the Sentinel's true history: Jué arrived as a guardian from a faraway star, sustained crippling injuries that surpassed its natural immortality, and lost one of its **Temporal Programs** (the ability to freely manipulate time), leaving it capable only of stopping and reversing time locally. Jué's partial temporal control is the cause of Mt. Firmament's strange temporal distortions.

The climax involves the **Sentinel's Arbiter** (the Rover, whose Arbiter identity is implied throughout) approving the transfer of the lost **Temporal Mandate** from Jué to Jinhsi through her **Secondary Resonance Awakening**. Jinhsi inherits the full Temporal Program — including the lost ability Jué could no longer access — completing the delegation of Jinzhou's guardianship. Following this transfer, the Temporal Disruptions in Mt. Firmament subside, and Jué vows to only offer aid when truly needed, as Jinhsi has fully assumed leadership.

## Act VII: Thaw of Eons — Key Revelations

- **Jué's Ancient Arrival:** In ancient times, Jué descended on Mt. Firmament in the **Mianloong Chamber**, a massive hibernation vehicle. Early Hongzhen settlers believed it came from a faraway star.
- **Jué's Lost Temporal Program:** Jué sustained injuries exceeding its natural immortality, causing it to lose one of its Temporal Programs. Mt. Firmament's time distortions are a direct consequence of this damage.
- **The Temporal Mandate Transfer:** The Sentinel's Arbiter (the Rover) approves the formal transfer of Jué's Temporal Mandate to Jinhsi during her Secondary Resonance Awakening.
- **Jinhsi's Infant Resonance:** A flashback reveals Jinhsi's backstory — as an infant in the ruins during a TD siege, her unique resonant cries were detected by Jué. After the infant died, Jué used Mt. Firmament's temporal anomalies to reverse her death and designate her as its Appointed Resonator.
- **Jinhsi's Hidden Inheritance:** Jinhsi unknowingly carried Jué's lost Temporal Program within her Resonance Ability from infancy. Her Awakening reveals she can access the full scope of temporal power that Jué itself could no longer reach.
- **Rover as Arbiter:** The Act strongly implies that the Rover's role as the entity whose approval is needed for the Temporal Mandate transfer is connected to their broader identity as an Arbiter and founder of the Black Shores.

## Act VII: Thaw of Eons — Key Locations

- **Mt. Firmament** — A snow-covered peak southeast of Jinzhou, sealed from civilians and perpetually enshrouded in mist where time flows abnormally slowly. Newly opened to exploration in Version 1.1.
- **Hongzhen** — A town built by the ancestral settlers of Jinzhou in the shadow of Mt. Firmament. Rich with historical and cultural artifacts relating to the Sentinel's early relationship with the people.
- **Loong's Crest** — A carving on Mt. Firmament's mountainside made by early Hongzhen settlers depicting Jué, created as an act of reverence.
- **Mianloong Chamber** — The ancient vehicle that transported Jué to Solaris-III. Now serves as Jué's resting place and the site of the Act's climactic revelation.

## Act VII: Thaw of Eons — Important NPCs

- **Jinhsi** — Magistrate of Jinzhou and Jué's Appointed Resonator. Protagonist of Act VII alongside the Rover. Undergoes her Secondary Resonance Awakening and inherits the full Temporal Mandate.
- **Jué** — The Sentinel of Jinzhou, taking the form of a massive Loong (Chinese dragon). Provides the Act's central revelation about its history, injuries, and the nature of Temporal Programs.
- **Changli** — Counselor to the Jinzhou Magistrate and former Secretary-General of the Central Secretariat. Accompanies the journey.

## Act VII: Thaw of Eons — Combat Encounters

- **Tacet Discords at Mt. Firmament:** Multiple new TD types indigenous to Mt. Firmament are encountered throughout the arc, including the **Lightcrusher** (Elite), **Lumiscale Construct** (Elite — composed of Jué's defensive modules, known as Loong's Scales), **Glacio Dreadmane** (Elite wolf-mimic), **Dwarf Cassowary** (Common, flightless ratite-mimic), **Lava Larva** (Common, larva-mimic near underground heat sources), and **Clang Bang** (Common, immature humanoid TD that mimics the sound of colliding ice).
- **Jué (Weekly Boss — Post-Quest):** After completing Act VII, Jué becomes available as a **Calamity-class Weekly Boss** — the first of its kind introduced in the game. Players engage Jué in a challenge simulation version within the Mianloong Chamber. Jué has high resistance to Electro and Spectro damage; Aero, Fusion, Havoc, and Glacio characters are recommended. Jué drops the **Sentinel's Dagger**, which is required to level up Jinhsi's Resonance skill.

## Act VII: Thaw of Eons — Gameplay Significance

- Completion of Act VII unlocks Mt. Firmament and Hongzhen as permanent explorable areas.
- Completes the Jinhsi character arc and fully establishes her as a 5-star playable Resonator (released alongside the Act in Version 1.1).
- Unlocks **Jué** as a Calamity-class Weekly Boss.
- Required to unlock **Chapter I Act VIII: To the Shore's End**.
- **Rewards:** Union Experience, Astrites, Rover's Waveband, character and weapon upgrade materials.
