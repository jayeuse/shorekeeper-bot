# Act XII: Stagnant Dawn on Wastelands

## Act XII: Stagnant Dawn on Wastelands — Overview

- **Chapter:** Chapter II — Even When Divinity Remains Silent
- **Act Number:** Act XII (Final Act of Chapter II)
- **Region:** Black Shores / Chronorift Metropolis (Honami City Sonoro Sphere)
- **Release Version:** Wuthering Waves Version 2.8 (November 20, 2025)
- **Prerequisite:** Completion of Chapter II Act XI: Dawn Breaks on Dark Tides (Rinascita Final Act)
- **Spotlight Character:** Chisa (5-star Havoc Broadblade, playable in Phase 1 of 2.8)
- **Significance:** Introduces Honami City and the Chronorift Metropolis; resolves the Irideglow time-loop crisis; introduces Chisa; teases the Lahai-Roi arc; expands the Tethys System's frequency feature library using Leviathan's Gem data.

## Act XII: Stagnant Dawn on Wastelands — Synopsis

Following the resolution of the Leviathan crisis in Rinascita, the Black Shores recovers the **Threnodian's Gem** — Leviathan's concentrated essence. The **Tethys System** runs a deep analysis of the Gem, significantly expanding its **frequency feature library** and gaining the ability to detect previously unidentifiable special frequencies.

During this expanded scan, the System detects a fragmented message among the new frequencies: one signal points to an unknown location, and another points **directly to the Black Shores itself**. The Rover is dispatched to investigate the anomaly offshore. **Valentina** (Remote Commander of the Emergency Response Team) oversees the Black Shores operations while the Rover investigates. **Buling**, an eccentric fortune-teller with frequency-sensing abilities, has also noticed the disturbance and arrives at the Black Shores; her Pendant of Shifting Yao is vibrating intensely, pointing to a dangerous formation.

The anomaly manifests as the **silhouette of a city skyline** appearing off the shores of the Black Shores — the Chronorift Metropolis, a Sonoro Sphere that has been sealed for twenty years. A mysterious hand reaches out and drags the Rover inside.

## Act XII: Stagnant Dawn on Wastelands — Inside the Chronorift Metropolis

Within the Honami City Sonoro Sphere, the Rover encounters **Chisa** — a young Resonator and former Startorch Academy student who has been trapped inside the sphere's time loop for subjectively two months (but twenty externally elapsed years). Chisa had been pulling the Rover into the loop as an ally, as previous escape attempts all failed.

The Sonoro Sphere is infested with **Irideglow Crystal** contamination, which has caused a time loop trapping all those caught inside. A fellow trapped individual named **Sumika** — a former Black Shores member — sacrificed herself to give Chisa the opening needed to pull the Rover in and finally break the loop. This sacrifice is what made the Rover's entry possible.

The primary antagonist controlling the Irideglow infestation and maintaining the loop is the **Chronorift Yakujin** — an Overlord-class entity that treats the entire city as its territory, using Irideglow contamination to control local TDs and block all entry and exit. **Shashou Cafe**, protected by the spatial-cutting Resonance ability of its owner, serves as the primary safe haven.

The **Stability** system governs the Rover's condition inside the Honami City Sonoro Sphere: taking damage from TDs or contact with Irideglow walls depletes Stability, requiring management via **Sparkling Water** consumables and **Namipon Stickers** (collectibles found throughout the city).

## Act XII: Stagnant Dawn on Wastelands — Resolution and Aftermath

After navigating the **Meishin Passageway** and confronting the Chronorift Yakujin (boss: **Elegy of the Fallen**), the Rover defeats the entity and ends the Irideglow time loop. The Sonoro Sphere stabilizes, and both the Rover and Chisa escape to the outside world.

The Act ends with major consequences: **Chisa** is treated for Overclocking injuries at the Black Shores medical facilities, as she pushed herself to the brink of Overclocking multiple times within the loop. **Shashou** is recruited by the Rover to join the Black Shores. A trophy scene — **"The Proof of Her Existence"** — can be triggered post-quest by speaking to **Mie** in the Error Cell's basement, where Mie describes her findings about **Sumika's** presence in the Honami loop and confirms the former Black Shores member's last actions.

## Act XII: Stagnant Dawn on Wastelands — Key Revelations

- **Honami City has been sealed for 20 years:** Inside the Sonoro Sphere, time passed at a compressed rate — only two months for those inside, while the outside world experienced twenty years. Chisa is effectively a temporal fish-out-of-water upon escape.
- **The Tethys System's expanded capability:** Analysis of Leviathan's Gem has given the System the ability to detect new categories of special frequencies, directly enabling the detection of the Honami anomaly.
- **The frequency message sent to the Black Shores:** An unknown entity embedded a message in the anomalous frequencies detected after the Rinascita crisis, specifically addressed to the Black Shores — suggesting someone or something knew precisely how to reach the organization.
- **Sumika's sacrifice:** A former Black Shores member named Sumika willingly sacrificed herself within the loop to give Chisa the opening to pull the Rover inside. Her existence and final act are documented in Tethys' Deep post-quest.
- **Chisa's connection to Startorch Academy and Lahai-Roi:** Chisa was a student at Startorch Academy before being trapped in Honami City. Her desire to return there is a major tease for the Chapter III arc.
- **The "Abraxas" thread:** Earlier in Chapter II, Leviathan recognized the Rover's companion Abby by the name "Abraxas." This thread continues to be unresolved and carries forward into the next arc.
- **Controllable Sonoro Project:** Research logs found during the quest reveal that the Fractsidus were experimenting in Honami City to expand Sonoro Spheres and potentially control the Lament — a "Controllable Sonoro Project."
- **Polmes (Namipon):** The Rover is accompanied by a Namipon companion named "Polmes" (a detective persona) throughout the investigation, serving as a guide and mascot for the arc.

## Act XII: Stagnant Dawn on Wastelands — Key Locations

- **Tethys Hub (Black Shores HQ)** — Starting point of the Act. Players speak with Valentina at the Error Cell and investigate the offshore anomaly from the shore.
- **Chronorift Metropolis (Honami City)** — The Sonoro Sphere's interior. A cyberpunk-aesthetic cityscape of ruins and neon, including the Neon Tower, Shashou Cafe, Meishin Passageway, Sazanami Avenue, and Shiawase Fountain Square.
- **Shashou Cafe** — Central safe haven inside the Sonoro Sphere, clipped into a protected pocket by its owner's spatial-cutting ability.
- **Error Cell (Tethys' Deep)** — The Shorekeeper's Error Cell in the underground serves as the computational hub during the Act, where she runs expanded frequency analysis. Also post-quest location for the Sumika memorial scene.
- **Meishin Passageway** — A key navigational route within Honami City, leading to the area where the Chronorift Yakujin must be confronted.

## Act XII: Stagnant Dawn on Wastelands — Important NPCs

- **Chisa** — Spotlight character and arc hero. A Resonator and Startorch Academy student trapped in the loop for two months. Emotionally scarred by isolation and failure; the Rover's presence is the first time her escape attempts succeed. Becomes a playable 5-star Havoc Broadblade Resonator.
- **Valentina** — Remote Commander of the Emergency Response Team. Oversees Black Shores operations during the quest. Briefs the Rover on the Tethys System's expanded frequency analysis.
- **Shorekeeper** — Conducts computational analysis of the anomaly from the Error Cell while the Rover investigates. Her expanded scan is what detects the fragmented message pointing to the Black Shores.
- **Buling** — A fortune-teller with genuine frequency-sensing abilities who arrives at the Black Shores having independently sensed the anomaly. Becomes a playable 4-star Electro Rectifier Resonator.
- **Sumika** — Deceased former Black Shores member whose sacrifice inside the loop enabled the Rover's entry. Her story is reconstructed post-quest via Mie's investigation.
- **Shashou** — Owner of Shashou Cafe; a Resonator whose ability to cut through space was used to carve the cafe into a safe haven. Recruited to the Black Shores at the Act's conclusion.

## Act XII: Stagnant Dawn on Wastelands — Combat Encounters

- **Abnormal Tacet Discords (Honami City):** Irideglow-contaminated TDs throughout the city, rendered simultaneously stronger and berserk by the crystallization. They reject all outsiders and go into uncontrolled rage upon being affected.
- **Chronorift Yakujin (Final Boss — Elegy of the Fallen):** The Overlord-class entity controlling the Irideglow infestation and maintaining the time loop. Defeated at the end of the Act to break the loop. Post-completion, its Nightmare variants can be farmed for the **Thread of Severed Fate** Sonata set.
- **Stability Mechanic:** A unique system within the Honami City Sonoro Sphere. Stability functions as a secondary resource depleted by enemy attacks and environmental hazards. Managed using Sparkling Water and Namipon Stickers.

## Act XII: Stagnant Dawn on Wastelands — Gameplay Significance

- First exploration access to the **Chronorift Metropolis (Honami City)** as a permanent region (initially gated; permanent access established via the Stranger Things in Honami event and subsequent segue).
- Introduces **Chisa** (5-star Havoc Broadblade) and **Buling** (4-star Electro Rectifier) as new playable Resonators.
- Introduces the **Thread of Severed Fate** Sonata set (3-piece: +20% ATK, +30% Liberation DMG for Havoc Bane characters).
- Introduces the **Stability** gameplay mechanic within Honami City.
- Introduces 36 **Summer Flowers** collectibles in Chronorift Metropolis (daily hunt via Sensor system).
- **Rewards:** Astrites x200, Union EXP x2000, Shell Credits x120,000, Sealed Tubes x7, Resonance Potions x7.
- Required prerequisite for the Chapter II Segue: Flowing Starlight in the Iris.
