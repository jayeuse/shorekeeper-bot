# Ragunna Territory

## Ragunna City

**Region Type:** Urban, Coastal, Political Center  
**Controlling Authority:** Order of the Deep, Montelli Family (influence)  
**Climate:** Mediterranean maritime

The spiritual and commercial heart of Rinascita, Ragunna City serves as the primary port of entry for visitors and the center of religious devotion to Sentinel Imperator. Built along the southern coast with canals threading throughout, the city's architecture blends practical maritime design with ornate religious symbolism.

## Ragunna City: City Square

The central gathering place and social hub of Ragunna. Features:

- Genesis Nexus (Nexus Tower) - The central Terminal controlling all Common Echoes
- Public performance spaces used for Carnevale and daily entertainment
- Hocus Pocus Echoes providing gifts and tricks for residents
- Statues and shrines dedicated to Imperator
- Access to major thoroughfares and Gondola routes

The Genesis Nexus inscription reads: "With might and justice, I rule from the throne, silencing ocean's tumult under my reign alone. For those who refuse to drown in swirling waves, heed this doctrine: Seek salvation from where all began."

This location serves as the registration point for visitors, granting them access to the city's Common Echo system. The Nexus embodies Rinascita's founding legend and technological mastery.

## Ragunna City: Bank Street

The financial district housing Ragunna's major banking institutions and commercial operations. Features:

- Averardo Bank (primary Montelli family institution)
- Mercurio Echoes providing automated banking services
- Business offices for major trading families
- Insurance and investment firms
- Secure transport routes for valuable goods

The architecture here is more austere than other districts, emphasizing security and professional respectability. The Montelli family's influence is strongest in this area.

## Ragunna City: Montelli Quarter

Residential and business district associated with the Montelli family interests. Features:

- Family estates and allied residences
- Reserved Terminal development facilities
- Private Echo training grounds
- Warehouses for imported and exported goods
- Meeting halls for commercial negotiations

This area represents new wealth and progressive attitudes, contrasting with the Order's conservative strongholds. Recent tensions with the Fisalia family have made security a priority.

## Ragunna City: Order of the Deep

The religious and administrative center of Ragunna's governing body. Features:

- Cathedral of Mercury (main religious edifice)
- Confession rooms and prayer chambers
- Acolyte quarters and training facilities
- La Guardia Echo barracks
- Archives containing religious texts and historical records
- Primus Fenrico's official residence and audience chambers

The Cathedral dominates the skyline with its distinctive architecture, serving as a constant reminder of the Sentinel's presence. All major policy decisions and religious ceremonies originate here.

## Ragunna City: Trattoria Margherita and Restaurant District

Culinary and social gathering areas throughout the city. Notable establishments:

- Trattoria Margherita (featured restaurant serving traditional cuisine)
- Various trattorias, cafes, and wine bars
- Gelato vendors and street food stalls
- Nectarwine tasting rooms
- Bakie and other service Echoes working in hospitality

Ragunnan cuisine emphasizes fresh seafood, proper cooking techniques, and social dining. The saying "If you can't appreciate good wine, you don't deserve good water" reflects the culture's culinary pride.

## Ragunna City: Lighthouse of Dawn

The iconic lighthouse marking Ragunna's entrance and serving as both navigation aid and symbol. Features:

- Towering structure visible from distant waters
- Beacon powered by Echo technology
- Observation deck with city and sea views
- Legend says it represents the Sentinel's radiant gaze

This landmark is one of the first sights greeting visitors arriving by sea, embodying the hope and guidance Imperator provides to the lost and displaced.

## Ragunna City: Port and Dock District

Maritime infrastructure supporting Ragunna's role as a major trading hub. Features:

- Customs inspection stations (operated by Order Acolytes)
- Gondola terminals connecting to outer islands
- Commercial shipping berths
- Fish markets and maritime trade centers
- Warehouses for import/export operations
- Shipwright workshops and Echo boat maintenance facilities

The port maintains strict protocols, requiring declaration of Reserved Terminals and screening all visitors. Despite regulations, it remains vibrant with merchant activity.

## Ragunna City: Pioneer Association Headquarters

Center for Ragunna's explorers, researchers, and cultural institutions. Features:

- Wutherium Film Studio
- Exhibition halls showcasing discoveries
- Meeting rooms for expedition planning
- Fulmine's office and operations center
- Archives of exploration reports and maps

This organization represents Ragunna's curiosity about the wider world and desire to document and share knowledge.
