## Internal Chapter I: New Arrival in The Big City

## Internal Chapter I: Synopsis

The player controls a young Augusta arriving in Septimont. She navigates the harsh "eat or be eaten" culture of the city. Rejected by official channels due to her lack of status, she seeks a mentor and finds Cato. The chapter focuses on her struggle to pass Cato's entry test: landing a single blow on him despite being vastly outmatched.
Key Gameplay Element: Management of Health, Martialism, Willpower, and Reputation.
