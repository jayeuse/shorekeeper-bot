## Segue: A Stranger in a Strange Land

## Segue (Stranger): Synopsis

In the aftermath of the war, the bard Ciaccona recounts the tale of Qiuyuan, a swordsman from Huanglong who aided in the defense of Ragunna. The player relives Qiuyuan's journey: arriving in the city, defending civilians, and confronting the corrupted form of Fenrico the First.

## Segue (Stranger): Key Revelations

Fenrico's Redemption: Fenrico had succumbed to the Threnodian's corruption but accepted his death as destiny, handing over the "First Relic" (Napoli's Lantern) to Qiuyuan to pass to the Rover.

Huanglong Connection: Qiuyuan was sent by the Grand Marshal of Mingting to assist the Rover, establishing a diplomatic link between the nations.

Geshu Lin: Qiuyuan reveals he was originally tasked to execute General Geshu Lin but discovered the General had fallen to the Threnodian, adding context to the Jinzhou storyline.

## Segue (Stranger): Important Characters

Qiuyuan: An agent of the Mingting Internal Security Agency.

Fenrico: The fallen Primus.
