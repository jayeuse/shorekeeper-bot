## Prologue: Through the Sea Thou Break

## Prologue: Synopsis

After learning that Abby's frequency is weakening, Shorekeeper directs Rover to seek help from Sentinel Imperator in Rinascita. Rover journeys across the treacherous Stormy Sea alongside the Troupe of Fools (Brant, Roccia, and Lario), encountering the massive sea creature Cetus before arriving at Ragunna's port.

## Prologue: Key Revelations

- **Abby's Condition**: Abby's frequency is deteriorating and requires the Sentinel's intervention
- **Past Rover's Message**: Past Rover left encoded messages in Tethys System accessible only after resolving core issues
- **Ancient Pact**: Rover once made a pact with Sentinel Imperator
- **Troupe of Fools**: Traveling performers who celebrate freedom and counter-culture, serving as guides to Rinascita
- **Cetus Encounter**: First contact with Rinascita's unique marine megafauna
- **Ragunna Introduction**: Rover gains first impressions of Ragunna's Venice-like canal city structure and customs procedures

## Prologue: Key Locations

- **The Black Shores**: Starting point, Shorekeeper's domain
- **Stormy Sea**: Dangerous waters between Black Shores and Rinascita, known for violent storms
- **Cetus Territory**: Mid-sea region where the massive creature dwells
- **Ragunna Port**: Entry point to Rinascita, customs station
- **Genesis Nexus Registration**: First stop for registration and identification

## Prologue: Important NPCs

- **Shorekeeper**: Provides initial information about Abby's condition
- **Brant**: Captain of the Troupe of Fools, experienced sailor
- **Roccia**: Troupe member, provides cultural insights
- **Lario**: Troupe member
- **Customs Officials**: First contact with Ragunna's bureaucracy
