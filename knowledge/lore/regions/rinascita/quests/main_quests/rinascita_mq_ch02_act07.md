## Act VII: Dreamcatchers in the Secret Gardens

## Act VII: Synopsis

The Rover travels to the **Fabricatorium of the Deep**, a massive underwater facility where Echoes are manufactured and stored. They are betrayed and thrown into the sea by the corrupt Primus Fenrico but are rescued by a wounded **Phrolova**. Forming a fragile temporary alliance, they navigate the **"Lost Beyond,"** a surreal Sonoro Sphere created by Phrolova to store human consciousness. They discover that Primus Fenrico intended to save Ragunna by uploading everyone's minds into this space ("Unity"), but his plan was twisted by the Threnodian's corruption. Phrolova reveals her own motivation: an obsession with reviving the dead. In the climax, she sacrifices herself to merge with the Lost Beyond to contain the Threnodian's energy, while Rover secures the truth about the "Dark Tide."

## Act VII: Key Mechanics & Locations

- **The Secret Gardens (Lost Beyond):** A distorted, dream-like realm constructed from "petrified dreams" (preserved frequencies).
  - **Mechanic:** Phrolova uses **"Dream Weaving"** to manifest paths and bridges from sound waves.
  - **Visuals:** Floating islands of debris, red spider lilies (Lycoris), and spectral projections of the past.
- **Fabricatorium of the Deep:** The physical facility housing the core. It is an industrial underwater complex contrasting with the ethereal Lost Beyond.

## Act VII: Confrontation Dialogue (Phrolova & Fenrico)

_Key lines from the climax:_

**Phrolova to Fenrico:**

> _"You call this salvation? Stuffing souls into a jar like fireflies? You have no art, Fenrico. Only fear."_
> _"I didn't keep them here to 'save' them. I kept them because I couldn't let go. There is a difference."_

**Fenrico's Justification:**

> _"The Dark Tide is coming! The flesh is weak! Only in the frequency domain can Rinascita survive forever! I am the Shepherd of the New Age!"_

**Phrolova's Sacrifice:**

> _"This isn't redemption, Rover. Don't look at me like that. It's just... keeping the garden quiet."_

## Act VII: Key Revelations

- **Fenrico's "Paradise"**: Fenrico's plan was to create a digital afterlife (the Lost Beyond) to save his people from the physical destruction by the Dark Tide.
- **Phrolova's Obsession**: Phrolova is driven by a personal promise to resurrect her dead village (Ostina), not by abstract Fractsidus ideology.
- **The Threnodian of War**: The energy source for the entire facility is the dormant power of the Threnodian (Leviathan), which Phrolova was harvesting.

## Act VII: Political Developments

- **Collapse of the Order**: The exposure of Fenrico's corruption effectively decapitates the Order of the Deep's leadership.
