## Act I: The Sacred Breeze So Often Breathes

## Act I: Synopsis

After registering at the Genesis Nexus, Rover is assigned Zani as a guide. During a Carnevale rehearsal, a Vitreum Dancer Echo loses control, claiming to be Imperator and asking for help. This triggers an investigation into Echo malfunctions that reveals political tensions between the Montelli and Fisalia families, with connections to a disaster 10 years ago when the Carnevale was suspended.

## Act I: Key Revelations

- **Imperator's Plea**: A malfunctioning Echo claims to be the Sentinel calling for help
- **Echo Control System**: Common Echoes in Ragunna have physical autonomous existence via Genesis Nexus
- **Carnevale Suspension**: The festival was halted 10 years ago following widespread Echo control failures
- **Family Rivalry**: Montelli family (progressive, Reserved Terminal developers) vs Fisalia family (traditional, Order-aligned)
- **Reserved Terminals**: Portable Echo technology independent of Order control, controversial innovation
- **Order's Authority**: Order of the Deep treats Echo malfunctions as divine punishment requiring "purification" (memory reset)
- **Political Investigation**: Rover gets caught in power struggles between noble families

## Act I: Key Locations

- **Ragunna City Square**: Central hub with Genesis Nexus
- **Bank Street**: Location of Averardo Bank (employer of Zani) and Mercurio Echoes shop
- **Cathedral of Mercury**: Religious center of the Order of the Deep
- **Montelli Quarter**: Reserved Terminal facilities and family holdings
- **Carnevale Rehearsal Stage**: Where the first Echo malfunction occurs
- **Trattoria Margherita**: Restaurant district, gathering place
- **Lighthouse of Dawn**: Coastal landmark
- **Pioneer Association**: Exploration guild headquarters

## Act I: Important NPCs

- **Zani**: Averardo Bank employee assigned as Rover's guide, pragmatic and weary
- **Phoebe**: Order Acolyte, earnest believer investigating Echo incidents
- **Carlotta**: Montelli family member, politically savvy
- **Cristoforo**: Mysterious playwright working on script about Blessed Maiden
- **Alberto**: Head of Averardo Bank, Zani's superior
- **Margherita**: Restaurant owner, information source
- **Fenrico (Primus)**: "Enlightened One," Imperator's only known Resonator, leader of Order
