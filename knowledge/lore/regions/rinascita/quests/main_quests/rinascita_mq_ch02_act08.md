## Act VIII: By Sun's Burning Hand

## Act VIII: Synopsis

The "High Tide" season arrives, bringing a surge of Tacet Discords to the Sanguis Plateaus. Ephor Augusta mobilizes the city's forces for a massive hunt. The Rover joins the expedition, ostensibly to participate in the hunt but secretly to investigate the Three Heroes' Crest. As the hunt progresses, political tensions flare as the noble houses vie for credit, but Augusta's leadership unites the factions against a massive Corrosaurus: Tyrannos.

## Act VIII: Key Revelations

The Three Heroes: The "Three Heroes' Crest" is a sacred site named after ancient heroes who previously held back the tide. It is revealed to be a focal point for the Dark Tide's energy.

Iuno's Role: While Augusta leads the charge, Priestess Iuno is revealed to be maintaining the metaphysical barriers (Anti-Corrosion Wards) that protect the soldiers from the "Blightcloud."

## Act VIII: Key Locations

Sanguis Plateaus: A red-earthed volcanic region.

Hunter's Den: The forward operating base for the Gladiators.

Three Heroes' Crest: The sacred summit where the final confrontation occurs.

## Act VIII: Important Characters

Augusta: Displays her military prowess and ability to command loyalty.

Iuno: Shows signs of strain from holding back the corruption.

Avidius: A mysterious figure who appears during the hunt, seemingly connected to the Dark Tide.

## Act VIII: Political Developments

Unification: The successful hunt consolidates Augusta's power, effectively ending the squabbling of the minor houses and preparing Septimont for total war.
