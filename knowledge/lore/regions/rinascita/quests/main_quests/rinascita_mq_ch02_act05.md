## Act V: Shadow of Glory

## Act V: Synopsis

Cartethyia vanishes after events in Inverted Tower. Following faint traces, Rover arrives in Septimont, a city-state known for its gladiatorial tradition and rejection of religious devotion to the Sentinel. The Great Agon (championship held every four years) is underway, drawing crowds from across Solaris-3. Rover teams up with gladiator Lupa to compete in the tournament while investigating strange Echo anomalies and Cartethyia's whereabouts. Dark forces manipulate events from shadows, and Rover receives cryptic warnings from Phrolova about an impending catastrophe tied to Lupa herself.

## Act V: Key Revelations

- **Septimont's Philosophy**: "All for glory, fear no death" - revere heroes, not Divinity
- **Arsinosa**: Septimont's Guardian Echo, half-human half-lion, born from settlers' battle-lust and pride
- **Lupa's Secret**: She carries Cartethyia's frequency and transforms into another woman (Mya) in brief flashes
- **Mya's Fate**: Famous gladiator who disappeared 20 years ago during Great Agon, possibly due to betting scandals and mental breakdown
- **Bloody Agon**: Former brutal tournament system before Ephor Augusta's reforms
- **Match-Fixing Conspiracy**: House Silva involved in illegal betting and Echo manipulation
- **Lycoris Influence**: Same corrupting flower petals from Ragunna appear affecting Echoes
- **Phrolova's Warning**: Claims Lupa will trigger Dark Tide surge in Septimont within three days
- **Nameless One**: Fractsidus member manipulating events, obsessed with heroic tales
- **Anomaly Overlap**: Real world and anomalous "Bloody Agon" overlap during finals
- **Cartethyia's Parallel Journey**: She fights in shadow alongside Rover, confronting Cristoforo

## Act V: Key Locations

- **Septimont Archipelago**: Warrior city-state opposite from Ragunna
  - Capitoline Hill (upper city, government center)
  - Lower City districts
  - Vale of Glory (burial ground for heroes)
- **Colosseum Olymdos**: Grand arena on hilltop for championship matches
- **Hero's Way**: Ceremonial path gladiators walk to reach Colosseum
- **Leonidas Hotel**: Premier accommodation, Lupa's lifetime suite gifted to Rover
- **Solis Restaurant**: Fulvia's establishment, gathering place for gladiators
- **Gryphon Fortress**: Noble district with ancient Gryphon statues
- **Old District Ruins**: Submerged former city area beneath waters, dangerous with Kerasaurs
- **Tercules Statue**: Ancient monument by water where Cartethyia passed through
- **Village Entry Point**: First settlement after arriving from Ragunna
- **Hero's Legacy Armor**: Ancient statue containing Echo awaiting worthy warrior
- **Temple**: Priestess Iuno's domain with eternal flames
- **Training Arenas**: Scattered smaller venues for qualifier matches

## Act V: Important NPCs

- **Lupa**: Star gladiator who left House Silva team over match-fixing
  - Exceptional combat skills, beloved by fans
  - Struggles with fear of losing favor and identity
  - Unknowingly connected to Mya and anomaly
- **Mya**: Legendary gladiator from 20 years ago
  - Disappeared during Great Agon under mysterious circumstances
  - Exploited by manager and instructor, mentally unstable
  - Used as cover for noble schemes involving bloodshed
- **Ephor Augusta**: Current leader of Septimont
  - Former champion four years ago, rose from humble origins
  - Implemented reforms ending "Bloody Agon" brutality
  - Swift in judgment, commands great respect
- **Priestess Iuno**: Prophetess with authority for final judgments
  - Born during eclipse, most gifted in her line
  - Spends time in hunting grounds rather than Temple
  - Augusta's staunch ally, makes decisive call against Silva
- **Julia Silva**: Head of House Silva, arrogant noble
  - Obsessed with titles and "perfect Septimontian" image
  - Behind match-fixing scheme and Echo manipulation
  - Banished from Septimont after exposure
- **Cartur**: Former manager of Lupa, pragmatic and calculating
  - Handled Silva's sponsorship of Bough of Legions team
  - Becomes Lupa's manager again after Silva's fall
- **Emilio**: Former teammate of Lupa in Bough of Legions
  - Compromised by Silva schemes
  - Attempts ambush before tournament
- **Buling**: Young Taoist from Mengzhou (trained with Jianxin)
  - Detects feng shui problems in Rinascita
  - Provides Copper Mirror of Truth
  - Appears and vanishes mysteriously
- **Fulvia**: Restaurant owner, supporter of gladiators
- **Phrolova (Fractsidus Overseer)**: Appears in Rover's dream
  - Gives play predicting future events
  - Claims Nameless One (her associate) is manipulating story
  - Offers choice: kill Lupa or face catastrophe
- **Nameless One**: Fractsidus member obsessed with heroic tales
  - Creator/manipulator of anomaly affecting Septimont
  - Believes protagonists influence stories through proximity
  - Cristoforo appears to be connected
- **Cartethyia**: Fights in parallel shadow realm
  - Rescues Nameless Girl from Tidal Blight
  - Introduces self as "wandering knight from Ragunna"
  - Confronts Cristoforo in unknown arena

## Act V: Great Agon Structure

- **Preliminary Stage**: Year-round points-based matches
- **Special Qualifiers**: Fast-track for newcomers by defeating ranked opponents
- **Semi-Finals**: Top teams compete, broadcast widely
- **Finals**: Championship match where Arsinosa appears
- **Format**: Two gladiators + their Echo partners per team
- **Rewards**: Noble recognition, hefty bonus, secret perks, fame

## Act V: Combat Encounters

- **First Qualifier**: vs Vulcan Mining team
- **Second Qualifier**: vs Blue Furies (top-ranked team)
- **Semi-Finals**: vs Bough of Legions (Lupa's former team)
  - Lupa's spear controlled by Dark Tide frequency
  - Julia Silva's tampering exposed
- **Finals**: vs unknown opponents + overlapping Bloody Agon anomaly
- **Shadow Battle**: Arsinosa confrontation in anomalous realm

## Act V: Cultural Elements

- **Gladiator Worship**: Star fighters receive massive fan followings
- **Echo Bonding**: Hunting and taming Echoes is fundamental skill
- **Noble Patronage**: Houses sponsor fighters, tied to political power
- **Strength Doctrine**: Ability determines status, weakness despised
- **Glory Pursuit**: Victory brings spotlight, defeat brings shadows
- **Arena Politics**: Match outcomes affect family standings
- **Hero Statues**: Throughout city commemorating famous fighters
- **Gryphons**: Ancient creatures native to archipelago, respected for strength
- **Reverberations**: Emotional frequencies settle into objects, creating Echoes

## Act V: Political Landscape

- **Ephor System**: Single ruler chosen through merit and strength
- **Senate**: Governing body with historical corruption issues
- **Noble Houses**: Compete for power and resources
  - House Silva: Founding family, traditionalist (now banished)
  - Other houses vie for position through Agon success
- **Temple Authority**: Priestess can make binding judgments
- **Betting Networks**: Illegal underground gambling on matches
- **Reform Era**: Augusta's changes reduced brutality, increased fairness

## Act V: Anomaly Details

- **Tidal Blight**: White mist-like Dark Tide manifestation
- **Echo Instability**: All Septimont Echoes exhibit unstable power
- **Temporal Overlap**: Present and past (Bloody Agon) merge
- **Unnoticed by Locals**: Only Rover, Buling, and special individuals detect
- **Lycoris Flowers**: Corrupting influence similar to Ragunna
- **Mya Connection**: Past tragedy bleeding into present
- **Three-Day Countdown**: Timeline given by Phrolova

## Act V: Copper Mirror of Truth

- Artifact provided by Buling
- Shows visions of past events from 20 years ago
- Reveals Mya's exploitation and nobles' conspiracy
- Questions arise whether visions are real or illusion
- Used multiple times to uncover hidden history

## Act V: Thematic Elements

- **Glory vs Shadow**: Spotlight brings both elevation and scrutiny
- **Identity Questions**: Lupa's fear of losing self, Mya's tragic story
- **Strength Philosophy**: Power worship and its consequences
- **Manipulation**: Hidden forces controlling outcomes
- **Dual Realities**: Present action masking past trauma
- **Fame's Price**: Celebrity status comes with heavy burdens
- **Choice and Fate**: Phrolova's ultimatum vs free will
- **Parallel Battles**: Rover and Cartethyia fighting in light and shadow

## Act V: Character Development

- **Lupa**: Grapples with purpose - does she love fighting for itself or recognition?
- **Rover**: Faces moral dilemma from Phrolova's warning, investigates while competing
- **Cartethyia**: Operating independently in shadow realm, protecting and investigating
- **Abby**: Remains silent within Tacet Mark throughout Septimont visit

## Act V: Key Items

- **"Script"**: Play given by Phrolova predicting events
- **Copper Mirror of Truth**: Reveals past visions
- **Champion's Medal**: Victory trophy
- **Hero's Legacy Armor**: Echo befriended by Rover
- **Lupa's Spear**: Tampered weapon from Julia Silva

## Act V: Relationship Development

- Rover and Lupa form strong partnership through combat
- Mutual respect and understanding grows
- Lupa opens up about fears and insecurities
- Bond based on honest combat rather than politics

## Act V: Unresolved Questions

- How is Lupa connected to Mya?
- What is Nameless One's true goal?
- Why is Cartethyia fighting Cristoforo?
- What is nature of the anomaly's origin?
- Will Dark Tide truly manifest as Phrolova claimed?
- Can past tragedy be resolved without new catastrophe?

Quest awards Champion's Medal and "Shadow Under Spotlight" achievement. Sets up continued investigation into Septimont anomalies and Cartethyia's activities.
