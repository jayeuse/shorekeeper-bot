# Rinascita - Places and Locations

## Overview

Rinascita is a maritime nation comprised of independent city-states scattered across a vast archipelago. With no single administrative center, the region presents a unique geography where water connects rather than divides communities. The sixteen documented regions span diverse environments from coastal cities to underground vaults, from elevated plateaus to sunken depths.

This comprehensive guide details each location within Rinascita's two major city-states—Ragunna and Septimont—along with the surrounding territories, natural features, and points of interest that define the Land of Echoes.

---

## Geographic Organization

Rinascita can be broadly divided into several geographic zones:

## Ragunna Territory

The southern coastal zone, centered around Ragunna City, encompasses maritime trade routes, fertile peninsulas, island chains, and the mysterious underground vault systems.

## Septimont Territory

The elevated inland zone, dominated by plateaus, canyons, and the martial city-state of Septimont built among dramatic terrain.

## Transitional Zones

Areas of contested control, neutral ground, or natural barriers between the major city-states.

## Special Locations

## The Stormy Sea

**Region Type:** Open Water, Hazardous  
**Controlling Authority:** None (natural forces)

The treacherous waters between the Black Shores and Rinascita, legendary for its dangers.

**Features:**

- Persistent mists and fog banks
- Waveworn Phenomena creating hazardous conditions
- Cetus the Tidebreaker and other giant TDs
- Strange aquatic TDs without coherent thought
- Unpredictable currents and weather
- Required navigation route marked by buoys and lighthouses
- Gondola and ship travel require Echo assistance
- Historical shipwrecks littering depths

The Stormy Sea represents both physical and metaphorical boundary between Rinascita and the outside world.

## Environmental Features

## Water Systems

Rinascita's defining characteristic is its relationship with water:

**Canal Networks**  
Ragunna City's extensive canal system serves as primary transportation infrastructure. Gondola Echoes glide through these waterways, making "a third of a Ragunnesi's life spent on a Gondola" literal truth.

**Tidal Patterns**  
Strong tidal influences affect coastal access, particularly for Pilgrim's Sails and fishing operations. Some areas become accessible only at specific tides.

**Underground Waterways**  
Beneath the archipelago, extensive cave systems fill with seawater, creating subterranean ocean access and mysterious depths where strange creatures dwell.

## Climate Zones

Despite the archipelago's relatively small size, microclimates create environmental diversity:

**Mediterranean Coastal** (Ragunna, Whisperwind Haven)  
Mild temperatures, moderate rainfall, ideal for agriculture and comfortable living. The "sacred breeze" mentioned in legends creates pleasant conditions.

**Highland Continental** (Septimont, Sanguis Plateaus)  
Greater temperature extremes, harsher conditions building resilient populations. Red soil coloration from iron-rich minerals.

**Perpetual Mist** (Nimbus Sanctum, parts of Stormy Sea)  
Areas where natural fog combines with possible Waveworn Phenomena to create persistent mist. Both hazard and spiritual significance.

**Underground** (Vault systems, Fabricatorium)  
Artificial climate control in some areas, natural cave conditions in others. Stable temperatures but high humidity near water-filled sections.

## Architectural Styles

## Ragunnan Maritime

Characterized by:

- Integration of canals into city planning
- Ornate religious decoration on public buildings
- Balconies and terraces maximizing water views
- Stone construction with stucco facades
- Gondola docks at most buildings
- Colors reflecting the sea (blues, whites, golds)

## Septimontian Highland

Characterized by:

- Fortress-like construction emphasizing defense
- Stone work showcasing engineering prowess
- Geometric patterns and angular designs
- Platforms and levels exploiting elevation
- Minimal ornamentation, functional beauty
- Colors of earth and blood (reds, browns, grays)

## Ancient Avinoleum

Characterized by:

- Massive stone construction
- Astronomical alignment and precise measurements
- Religious symbolism distinct from Imperator worship
- Advanced engineering for water management
- Tower structures serving multiple functions
- Unknown written language on inscriptions

## Order-Controlled Structures

Characterized by:

- Dominant presence and imposing scale
- Extensive use of Imperator iconography
- Light-focused design (skylights, windows)
- Acoustics optimized for hymns and ceremonies
- Security features disguised as decoration
- White/gold color schemes suggesting purity and divinity

## Economic Geography

## Resource Distribution

**Fagaceae Peninsula:** Oak forests for wine barrels, timber  
**Sanguis Plateaus:** Minerals, game, dangerous but valuable TD materials  
**Beohr Waters:** Fish, pearls, underwater materials, Echo spawning grounds  
**Whisperwind Haven:** Wine, agricultural products, grains  
**Riccioli Islands:** Fish, maritime trade facilitation, ships  
**Vault Underground:** Secure storage itself as service, Echo research

## Trade Routes

**Primary Maritime Routes:**

- Black Shores ↔ Ragunna Port (international trade)
- Ragunna ↔ Riccioli Islands (domestic shipping)
- Coastal ring route connecting Ragunna settlements
- Septimont riverine access (limited)

**Overland Routes:**

- Ragunna ↔ Whisperwind Haven (agricultural supply)
- Border Mountains passes (Septimont access)
- Sanguis Plateaus resource extraction routes
- Fagaceae Peninsula timber transport

**Underground:**

- Vault networks (secure valuable transport)
- Sewer connections (smuggling, unofficial movement)
- Cave systems (exploration, resource access)

## Notes for Explorers

## Navigation Challenges

- Gondola Terminal system requires learning but efficient
- Mist and fog can disorient even experienced travelers
- Tide tables essential for coastal exploration
- Underground areas require light sources and Echo support
- Certain areas restricted by Order or dangerous conditions

## Cultural Considerations

- Show respect at religious sites and Imperator statues
- Declare Reserved Terminals at customs
- Learn local dining etiquette in Ragunna
- Understand martial culture when visiting Septimont
- Be cautious about discussing Carnevale tragedy
- Political neutrality advised in family disputes

## Safety Warnings

- Stormy Sea requires experienced navigator
- Sanguis Plateaus has dangerous TD concentrations
- Vault Underground needs proper authorization
- Abandoned areas may have structural hazards
- Some Echoes territorial or dangerous if provoked
- Political tensions can turn violent
