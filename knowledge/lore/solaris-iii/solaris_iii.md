## Overview

Solaris-III, also designated **Sol-III** and commonly referred to simply as **Solaris**, is the third planet from the sun and the primary setting of _Wuthering Waves_. It is a post-apocalyptic world defined by cycles of catastrophic destruction and tenacious human rebuilding.

## Solaris-III Lore Summary

Solaris-III is a world sealed from the cosmos by a metaphysical barrier called the **Etheric Sea**. Its reality is governed by **frequencies** — where sound, memory, and vibration dictate the laws of physics. For over 10,000 years, humanity has endured a recurring cycle of mass extinction events known as **The Lament**, which actively destabilizes physical laws and rewrites reality usually by erasing civilizations.

Despite this relentless cycle of ruin, humanity has proven extraordinarily resilient. The same catastrophic energies that devastated civilization also gave rise to **Resonators** — individuals capable of perceiving and manipulating frequencies. The world has rebuilt itself into a patchwork of distinct nations, each shaped by specific disasters and protected by divine entities called **Sentinels**.

The central conflict of the current era involves the **Fractsidus**, an organization seeking to trigger a final "True Lament," and the **Black Shores**, a covert group working to prevent it.
